> You breathe oxygen all day. You do NOT thank the atmosphere.
> Your parents cook 10,000 meals. You do NOT thank them for a single one.
>
> But you nearly drown, surface, breathe — oxygen = INTENSELY GOOD.
> You return home after 5 years away, eat your mother's cooking — that meal = INTENSELY GOOD.
>
> Same oxygen. Same food. DIFFERENT delta.
> VTA has habituated → gift invisible → body does NOT reward → "normal."
>
> "Thank you" is NOT social convention. "Thank you" is a NEURAL TOOL.
> PFC activates comparison: "I HAVE vs I DO NOT HAVE" →
> artificial delta → VTA fires again → real body-base reward →
> ❸ valence toward source += positive → connection MAINTAINED.

> Religion discovered this mechanism through thousands of years of trial-and-error:
> prayer before meals, periodic fasting, Thanksgiving.

> WHY "thank you" feeds THE SPEAKER'S OWN BODY (5 simultaneous chains),
> the GIFT-OBLIGATION spectrum, and WHY understanding gratitude = understanding
> how the brain evaluates EVERYTHING it receives.

### §1.1 — Definition v2.0

```
⭐ DEFINITION:

  Gratitude = observable pattern when the body's valence system fires
  NET POSITIVE toward an AGENT-source, after:
    ① Body-base ACTUALLY rewards from gift (Body-Feedback-Precondition met)
    ② PFC Comparison creates artificial prediction-delta ("have vs don't have")

    ④ Self-Pattern-Modeling simulates giver's effort → empathy
    ⑤ Obligation assessment: debt threat LOW ENOUGH for net positive

    ✗ A single emotion ("the feeling of gratitude")

      → Social convention = lips move, body does NOT fire.
        Real gratitude = body fires.

      → PFC knowing ≠ body firing. "Knowing I should be grateful" without body fire

    ✗ Automatic when receiving a gift ("give → grateful")
      → NEEDS 9 components. Missing any → DOES NOT FIRE.
      → = Why "giving without gratitude" = common, NOT abnormal.

  Gratitude IS:
    ○ Observable pattern when multiple sub-systems fire simultaneously
    ○ Emergent — NO ONE "decides" to be grateful. 9 components meet → emerges.
    ○ Agent-only — fires only toward agents (real or virtual)
    ○ Gradient — from very mild (micro appreciation) to very strong (life-changing)
```

```
      → Precondition-1 MET: schema pending for exactly what is missing
```

### §2.3 — ③ PFC Quality Comparison

```
⭐ PREREQUISITE 3: PFC HOLDS "HAVE" VS "DON'T HAVE" → CREATES DELTA

  (PFC-Hold-Dimensions.md — ~4±1 dimensions)

  This is the PRIMARY TOOL against habituation:

  MECHANISM:
    PFC holds 2 states SIMULTANEOUSLY:
      State A: "I HAVE this gift" (current reality)
      State B: "I DO NOT HAVE this gift" (counterfactual)
    → Compare → delta = A - B > 0 → VTA fires → opioid
    → = ARTIFICIAL delta — no NEW gift needed, only COMPARISON

  WHY COMPARISON IS NEEDED:
    No comparison → gift habituated → baseline = invisible
    With comparison → delta RESTORED → reward RESTORED → gratitude POSSIBLE
    = "Thank you" = TOOL that activates comparison (§6)

  CHUNKS PREREQUISITE FOR COMPARISON:
    PFC can only compare when it HAS chunks about a DIFFERENT state:
      → Child volunteers in a poor area → "poverty" chunks compile →
        returns home → compares "I HAVE vs they DO NOT HAVE" → delta → gratitude
      → Child has never seen poverty → NO comparison chunks →
        no delta → no gratitude

  COMPARISON IS A PFC FUNCTION, NOT AN OBSERVATION:
    → Comparison SERVES multiple observations:
      Gratitude: "have vs don't have" → appreciate
      Status: "me vs others" → calibrate
      Boredom: "now vs want" → bored
```

### §3.1 — Root Problem: VTA Habituates → Hedonic Treadmill

