## §1 — UNIFIED FORMULA: DISSONANCE + UNCLEAR IMAGINE-FINAL

### §1.1 — Formula (VALIDATED + EXTENDED)

```
⭐ "BOREDOM" = Body-feedback dissonance + Imagine-Final NOT CLEAR ENOUGH

  DIMENSION 1 — Body-feedback (dissonance direction):
    "Something is off" — body detects mismatch.
    → Can be MILD (sluggish, restless) or STRONG (strong dislike of something).
    → Can be DIFFUSE (doesn't know what's missing) or CLEAR (knows what it's bored of).
    → 6 different sources (§2).

  DIMENSION 2 — Imagine-Final clarity:
    No SOLUTION clear enough for the body to COMMIT.
    → DOESN'T KNOW what it wants (diffuse).
    → KNOWS what it wants but not clearly enough / not ready to act (locked).
    → HAS NOTHING to want (void).

  → BOTH dimensions SIMULTANEOUSLY = "boredom."
  → MISSING 1 dimension = NOT "boredom":
    Dissonance PRESENT + Imagine-Final CLEAR = "drive" or "strong dislike"
        (NOT bored)
    Dissonance ABSENT + Imagine-Final diffuse = "relaxed" / "fine"
        (NOT bored)

  🟡 2-dimension formula = framework synthesis.
  🟢 "Unfulfilled desire for satisfying activity" (Eastwood 2012) ≈ dimension 1.
```
