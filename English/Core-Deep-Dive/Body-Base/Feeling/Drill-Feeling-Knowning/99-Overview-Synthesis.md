- Mismatches manifest as: anxiety, repeated bad decisions, relationship patterns,

- Feeling literacy = ability to READ the compiled layer

**What it involves** (from Feeling-Accuracy.md §5):
1. Access — notice feelings (Feel-Consciousification–Feel-Observation)
2. Location — where in body
3. Vocabulary — richness of labels
4. Attribution — mapping feeling to cause
5. Discrimination — distinguishing similar feelings
6. Calibration — knowing accuracy per layer
7. Regulation — acting wisely on readings

**Modern relevance** (from Feeling.md §5): Ancestral feelings were simple + reliable
for ancestral environments. Modern mismatches require an INTERPRETER bridge —
literacy is that bridge.

**How to train** (Feeling-Accuracy §7):

### §6.2 — Decision making: Body-first + verify

**Damasio's insight operationalized**: Feelings are **necessary for decisions**, not
optional luxuries. VMPFC patients prove this negatively (reasoning intact →
paralysis).

**Practical decision framework** (synthesizing framework):

**Small decisions** (daily):
- Trust body signals at Feel-Consciousification–Feel-Observation
- Don't over-deliberate
- Examples: "which route to work," "what to eat" — feeling-guided is fine

**Medium decisions** (weeks-months):
- Run Imagine-Final previews for each option
- Check body response to each
- "How does this option FEEL?" — not as metaphor, as literal body state
- Integrate body preview + logic analysis

**Large decisions** (months-years):
- Multi-preview over time
- Sleep on it (BACKGROUND phase, Imagine-Final §1.5)
- Notice: does the feeling persist across days?
- Integration over time, not instant answer

**High-stakes / urgent**:
- Trust compiled expertise (if in domain)
- Expert intuition = compiled feelings (Klein 1998)
- Trust body's "something's off" signal (de Becker "Gift of Fear")

**Framework's warning**: Decisions based on Feel-Explanation interpretations are

  work
- "I'm angry at the driver" → road rage vs. actually stressed + hungry

**Practical rule** (from Feeling-Accuracy.md):
> "Check body basics first (HALT: Hungry, Angry, Lonely, Tired). Wait 30 minutes
