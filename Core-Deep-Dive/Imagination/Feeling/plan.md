### Phase 2 categories to fill

**Spectrum from basic to existential:**

1. **Newborn baseline** (0-3 months)
   - Crude distress (đói/khát/nóng/lạnh/mệt → khóc undifferentiated)
   - Reflexive responses
   - First chunks forming

2. **Infant discrimination** (3-12 months)
   - Distress type discrimination (parents recognize types)
   - First social signals (smile contagion, joint attention)
   - Body comfort/discomfort

3. **Toddler verbal emergence** (12-24 months)
   - "Đói", "đau", "buồn đái" verbal labels
   - Body part identification
   - Comforting behavior toward distressed others (Hoffman)

4. **Early childhood emotional vocabulary** (2-5 years)
   - Emotion words (vui, buồn, sợ, giận)
   - Theory of mind emerging (false belief task)
   - Pretend play (using simulator)

5. **Middle childhood complex feelings** (5-10 years)
   - Complex emotions (ấm ức, ghen tị, tự hào, xấu hổ)
   - Multi-perspective thinking
   - Sense of fairness

6. **Adolescent abstract feeling** (10-18 years)
   - Identity formation feelings
   - Abstract emotional reasoning
   - "I feel betrayed", "I feel misunderstood"

7. **Adult metacognitive** (mature)
   - Feeling of knowing (math problem will solve)
   - Confidence judgments
   - Predictive interoception (buồn đái, ốm sắp tới)

8. **Refined adult / training** (specialized)
   - Felt sense (Gendlin)
   - Subtle body states (meditation)
   - Deep social inference (therapist-level)
   - Aesthetic feelings (artist-level)

9. **Existential / philosophical** (highest)
   - "Tại sao mình lại làm như vậy"
   - "Tại sao không kiềm chế được"
   - "Ý nghĩa cuộc đời là gì"
   - "Tôi là ai"

**Each example should map:**
- WHICH channel (body / cognitive / social / metacognitive / etc.)
- WHICH chunk formula activated (or composition of chunks)
- WHERE on 5 axes (depth, breadth, speed, integration, action coupling)
- WHICH developmental stage (0-9)
- WHAT scaffolding required to develop
- HOW COMMON in population
