## §3 — 7 BẰNG CHỨNG CHỐNG "DOPAMINE = REWARD"

### §3.1 — Bằng chứng 1: Berridge rat lesion (decisive)

🟢 **Berridge & Robinson 1998, Peciña & Berridge 2005**:

> Chuột bị block/lesion hệ dopamine:
> - **VẪN** thể hiện "liking" facial reactions khi được cho sugar (lick lips, tongue protrusion)
> - **KHÔNG** seek sugar (không bấm lever, không đi tìm)
>
> Chuột bị kích thích dopamine (amphetamine, optogenetic):
> - Seek sugar NON-STOP (seeking tăng dramatic)
> - Facial pleasure reactions KHÔNG tăng (liking không tăng)

**Implication**:
- Dopamine = wanting system (seeking, motivation)
- Opioid = liking system (actual pleasure)
- → **Dopamine và opioid là 2 hệ KHÁC NHAU**. Không thể đồng nhất.

**Framework verdict**: ✅ Bằng chứng decisive. Framework's "chuông cửa ≠ quà" chính là mô tả hiện tượng này.

### §3.2 — Bằng chứng 2: Schultz PE = salience, không phải pleasure

🟢 **Schultz 1997 classic finding**:
- Monkey neuron VTA fire khi receive unexpected juice (reward prediction error positive)
- Sau training, VTA fire khi receive CUE (not juice itself)
- Khi reward expected nhưng không đến → VTA fire GIẢM (prediction error negative)

**Classical interpretation**: Dopamine = reward signal tracking value.

**Framework reinterpretation** 🟡 (PFC-Analysis §8.3 + 03-Reward §2.4):
- VTA fires on **DELTA** (habituation-based detection), không cần tính prediction error
- "Unexpected juice" = large delta → VTA fire
- "Expected but missing" = delta khỏi expected pattern → VTA fire (direction reversed)
- Habituation model **computationally cheaper** nhưng cho same observable result

**Key**: Schultz's observations **vẫn đúng**. Framework không phủ nhận. Framework tái diễn giải:
- Dopamine signal = salience/delta detection (attention direction)
- KHÔNG phải reward value signal
- "Feel" của reward = opioid downstream (step 5 sau VTA)

### §3.3 — Bằng chứng 3: Parkinson's anhedonia incomplete

🟢 **Clinical observation** (Sienkiewicz-Jarosz 2005, Loas 2012):
- Parkinson's = dopamine neuron loss in substantia nigra + VTA
- Expected (nếu dopamine = pleasure): mất khả năng enjoy anything
- Actual: **motivation/seeking impaired** (apathy common), **enjoyment partially preserved**
  - Bệnh nhân vẫn enjoy food khi eat (liking)
  - Nhưng không còn drive để đi tìm food (wanting)

**Implication**:
- Dopamine depletion → wanting impaired (matches framework: no VTA alert → no attention boost)
- Dopamine depletion → liking preserved (matches framework: opioid system intact)

**Framework verdict**: ✅ Consistent with 7-step mechanism. Block VTA → steps 2-4 fail, step 5 (opioid) có thể vẫn fire nếu stimulus đủ mạnh để bypass attention gate.

**v1.2 NOTE** (Parkinson-Analysis.md §6.2): 3 dopamine pathways affected at different rates (nigrostriatal TRƯỚC → mesolimbic → mesocortical SAU). Wanting/liking separation maps CHÍNH XÁC to 7-step: Steps 2-4 (VTA → PFC) weakened, Step 5 (opioid body-base) preserved. = CLEANEST clinical dissociation. Chi tiết + 3-way comparison: §5.5.

### §3.4 — Bằng chứng 4: Opioid = direct pleasure (morphine, endorphin)

🟢 **Pharmacological evidence**:
- Morphine/heroin (opioid agonist): **direct pleasure** signal, KHÔNG cần dopamine surge trước
- Naloxone (opioid antagonist): **block pleasure** rõ rệt cả khi dopamine normal
- Endogenous opioids (endorphin, enkephalin): fire khi eat, sex, social bonding, flow state
- Runner's high = endorphin (established), không phải dopamine

**Critical experiment** (Peciña & Berridge 2005):
- Inject opioid agonist vào NAcc "hedonic hotspot" → liking reactions tăng
- Inject dopamine agonist vào same region → liking KHÔNG tăng (wanting tăng)

**Framework verdict**: ✅ Opioid = reward thật. Dopamine surround opioid system nhưng khác function.

### §3.5 — Bằng chứng 5: Addiction wanting/liking dissociation

🟢 **Addiction research** (Robinson & Berridge 2003, Koob & Volkow 2010):
- Người nghiện **WANT drug intensely** (craving, seeking dominates life)
- NHƯNG **LIKE drug dần GIẢM** (tolerance, pleasure diminishes)
- → Classical "dopamine = pleasure" KHÔNG giải thích được

**Framework mechanism**:
- Dopamine system sensitized (hyperreactive to drug cues) → intense wanting
- Opioid response attenuated (tolerance) → decreased liking
- Body-need match KHÔNG thực hiện (drug hijacks VTA mà không fulfill body needs)
- → Loop: VTA fire → attention → seek → consume → no body satisfaction → VTA fire again

**Implication cho framework**:
- Scroll phone, slot machine, social media = **mini-addiction pattern**
- Micro-delta trigger VTA (posts mới, notifications) → dopamine + wanting
- Body-need KHÔNG match (không fulfill social, meaning, rest needs) → no opioid
- "Empty" feel = VTA active + opioid absent

### §3.6 — Bằng chứng 6: Novelty alone without pleasure (scroll empty)

🟡 **Daily observable phenomenon** (Novelty.md §2.4 scroll case):
- Scroll phone: mỗi post = micro-delta (hình mới, text mới)
- VTA fire nhẹ mỗi swipe → dopamine micro-dose
- **Nhưng**: body feel "mệt, trống, chán mà không dừng được"
- → Dopamine present (seek continues) + opioid absent (no body satisfaction)

**Classical explanation fails**:
- Nếu dopamine = pleasure → scroll phải feel good
- Thực tế: scroll feel "chán empty" nhưng vẫn tiếp tục

**Framework explanation**:
- VTA detect delta (satisfies Precondition 3) → dopamine fire
- Body-need KHÔNG match (no schema pending fill, no connection, no meaning) → Precondition 1 fail
- → No opioid → no reward thật
- Loop stuck: VTA keeps firing (new posts) + opioid never fires → "want more" without "getting satisfaction"

**Critical insight**: Pop "dopamine detox" logic sai ở đâu? Đúng intuition (scroll = vấn đề) nhưng sai mechanism (không phải "quá nhiều dopamine", mà **dopamine-opioid mismatch** + body-need bypass).

### §3.7 — Bằng chứng 7: Eureka moment = opioid, không phải dopamine spike

🟡 **Framework N+10 correction** (Chunk-Internal-Processing/01 §86):

> OLD mainstream: "Eureka = dopamine spike + cortisol drop"
> NEW framework: Eureka reward = **opioid** (schema match). Dopamine = salience alert. Cortisol drop = relief bonus (nếu có threat context).

**Mechanism**:
- Schema đang có dissonance (Precondition 1 met)
- Chunk mới xuất hiện → VTA detect delta → dopamine → PFC attend
- PFC simulate chunk → body-base check → chunk fits gap → **opioid RELEASE**
- Subjective: "AHA! EUREKA!" = opioid burst
- Nếu có threat đi kèm (Einstein thinking under stress): cortisol drop thêm → explosive Eureka
- Nếu không threat (pure curiosity): opioid alone → deep satisfaction nhưng less explosive

**4 Eureka intensity contexts** (01-Chunk-Connection-Logical.md §86):
1. Einstein mode (threat + schema dissonance): opioid + cortisol drop = CỰC SƯỚNG
2. Deep research (no threat, deep gap): opioid strong, less explosive
3. Light puzzle solve: opioid nhẹ + minor cortisol drop
4. Routine task complete: body-need "an toàn" KHÔNG match → NO proper opioid reward

**Implication**: Pop "dopamine rush = insight" = WRONG. Insight reward = opioid-based.

## §4 — FRAMEWORK ALTERNATIVE: 7-STEP MECHANISM

### §4.1 — Overview: 2-level reward

🟡 **Framework core claim** (03-Reward.md §1.2, Feeling-Mechanism-Deep §4 definitive):

> **Reward = 2-level mechanism**, KHÔNG phải 1 signal:
>
> **Level 1 — DOPAMINE ALERT (VTA)**: "CÓ BIẾN ĐỘNG — chú ý!" — salience, không phải value
> **Level 2 — OPIOID REWARD (body-base)**: "chunk KHỚP body-need → có giá trị thật" — actual reward
>
> VTA dopamine = **chuông cửa** (doorbell). Opioid = **quà đằng sau cửa** (gift behind door).

### §4.3 — Khác biệt cốt lõi: Body-base check (Step 5)

⭐ **Step 5 là chỗ framework khác tất cả các position khác**:
- Classical: bỏ step này (dopamine = pleasure, không cần body check)
- Berridge: acknowledge opioid khác dopamine, không specify WHEN/HOW opioid fires
- Framework: opioid fires WHEN chunk fits body-need (H10 5 preconditions 03-Reward §3)

**H10 5 preconditions cho opioid fire**:
1. Schema pending status (body có need đang chờ fill)
2. Chunks base adequate (đủ substrate để decode)
3. VTA delta threshold (biến động đủ lớn)
4. Goldilocks zone (match ratio 40-70%)
5. Chunk association tag (opioid-tagged not threat-tagged)

**ALL 5 must be met** → full reward. Miss ANY → weak/absent/wrong-direction signal.

### §4.4 — Addiction giải thích qua 7-step

**Addiction mechanism**:
- Drug bypass normal route: fire VTA + opioid receptors directly
- Steps 1-4: hyperactive (VTA sensitized to drug cues)
- Step 5: **BYPASSED** — drug fires opioid receptors artificially, không cần body-need match
- Step 6: reinforce drug-seeking chunks (strong Hebbian due to direct opioid hit)
- Step 7: tolerance — receptor adaptation (less response over time)

**Result**: Wanting sensitized (steps 2-4 boost) + Liking habituates (step 5 receptor adapt) + Body-need never actually fulfilled (step 5 bypass)
- → Pattern: "need more, enjoy less, never satisfied"
- Classical cannot explain. Framework 7-step can.

**Extended to behavioral addictions**:
- Slot machine: random reward schedule → VTA hyperfire + rare body-match → intermittent reinforcement
- Social media scroll: micro-delta per post → VTA constant fire + body-need rarely met → "empty chase"
- → Same mechanism, different substrate

### §4.5 — Integration với Chunk compile (Cortisol-Baseline v2.0 §3)

**Chunk compile direction gate** (Cortisol-Baseline v2.0 §3.5):
- Khi chunk compile, **cortisol level + body-state direction** tag chunk (opioid vs threat)
- Opioid-tagged chunk: future retrieval fires satisfaction
- Threat-tagged chunk: future retrieval fires discomfort

**Dopamine role in compile**:
- Step 2-4 (VTA → PFC attention) = điều kiện để chunk được compile
- Không có VTA fire → PFC không attend → chunk không consolidate
- → Dopamine **enables** compile, không trực tiếp reward

**Cortisol role in compile**:
- Cortisol state tại moment compile = tag direction
- HIGH cortisol + body holding: threat-tagged
- MODERATE cortisol + body open: novelty-tagged, opioid-associated

→ **Dopamine + Cortisol phân công rõ**: Dopamine = attention gate; Cortisol = direction tag; Opioid = actual reward signal.

### §4.6 — Integration với Imagine-Final (PFC simulation)

**Step 5 body-base check** dùng Imagine-Final mechanism:

- → Imagine-Final = **internal test** trước khi commit action

**Implication**: Reward can fire on **imagined** outcome, không cần external consumption:
- Reading → imagining → body satisfies → opioid
- Daydream of goal achievement → body micro-opioid
- → Explains why imagination itself can reward (pure thought pleasure)

**5 extensions beyond Berridge**:

**1. Mechanism của VTA fire (Step 2)**:

**2. Mechanism của liking (Step 5)**:

  - Schema pending + chunks adequate + delta threshold + Goldilocks zone + chunk tag
- → Each precondition falsifiable independently

**3. Body-base grounding (PFC → body simulate)**:
- Berridge: opioid "liking" happens somewhere in brain
- Framework: opioid fires sau khi **PFC simulates chunk → body checks fit với body-need**
- → Body-base = source of truth for reward (Body-Base.md L1 substrate)
- → Explains per-person reward variability (body-needs differ)

**4. Chunk compile integration (Cortisol direction gate)**:
- Berridge: không tích hợp với learning/memory consolidation
- Framework: chunk compile tagged opioid-vs-threat based on cortisol state tại compile moment
- → Explains: same content, different emotional association per person
- → Explains: trauma vs expertise cùng mechanism, khác direction

**5. Addiction mechanism specific (Step 5 bypass)**:
- Berridge: incentive sensitization theory (wanting pathological)
- Framework: **Step 5 body-base check BYPASSED** — drug fires opioid receptors directly without body-need match
- → Why wanting sensitizes + liking tolerates + body-need never fulfilled
- → Applies identically to behavioral addictions (scroll, gambling)
