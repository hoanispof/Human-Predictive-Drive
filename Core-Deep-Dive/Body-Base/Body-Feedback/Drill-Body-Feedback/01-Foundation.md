## §2 — Dual-Pull Principle (Hardware × Domain)

### §2.1 — User's articulation (§0.5.1 verbatim)

> "Schema (chunks or melody, viewed through the melody lens) is pulled simultaneously by both Hardware and Domain. This means all schema at any given moment must run smoothly on the hardware (a melody that doesn't run smoothly is dissonance). And the final outcome humans want to achieve is a broader, more accurate map of domain reality."

This is the **architectural starting point** for the entire drill. The framework committed (🟡) to this at `Personal-Melody.md` §5 "Two-Axis Tension" — this file formalizes it for dissonance/reward decoding.

### §2.2 — Hardware Pull (internal, conservative, wants smooth)

**Definition** 🟡:

> A force pulling schema toward **body-base comfort** — neurons fire familiar patterns without requiring recalibration, without causing discomfort.

**Substrate mechanisms**:
- **Hebbian LTP**: neurons that fire together wire together → familiar patterns fire more strongly with repetition
- **Habituation**: VTA habituates to regular patterns → doesn't fire → doesn't demand attention → smooth
- **Metabolic efficiency**: compiled pattern = low energy cost (§3 Chunk.md, Chunk-Practical brain fog analysis)
- **Body-base homeostasis** (L1 Body-Base reframe): body-inputs adaptive baseline — signal is quiet when pattern = baseline

**Features when hardware pull wins**:
- Comfort zone, routine, habit, "lying on the beach," contentment (🟡 Boredom-Analysis §2)
- Cortisol IDLE mode (Cortisol-Baseline §1)
- Personal melody smooth, no dissonance

**Dark side**: Hardware pull too strong → stagnation → not mapping domain → "living by old patterns" → evolution lag (Why-Body-Knows §6 ①: sugar, social media)

**Metaphor** (from Personal-Melody §5):
> "The audience wants to hear the OLD SONGS"

### §2.3 — Domain Pull (external, demands adaptation, maps reality)

**Definition** 🟡:

> A force pulling schema toward **domain reality mapping** — new chunks must be loaded even if the body isn't ready yet, because domain reality doesn't care whether the melody is smooth.

**Substrate mechanisms**:
- **Novelty drive** (Novelty.md): genetic drive to explore
- **prediction-delta detection** (PFC-Analysis §8.3): new pattern → VTA fires dopamine → PFC attention
- **Prediction error updating** (Friston/Clark): mismatch with expectation → schema update pressure
- **Evolutionary advantage**: species that don't map reality → don't survive → genes eliminated (Why-Body-Knows v1.1 §3 Tier 1 Darwinian)

**Features when domain pull wins**:
- Learning, exploration, curiosity, growth
- Cortisol ACTIVE/FOCUSED mode
- Melody builds via arcs (Melody-Arc §2)

**Dark side**: Domain pull too strong → burnout → neural wear → melody noise (§3 Cortisol-Baseline §3 repair deficit)

**Metaphor**:
> "The stage demands NEW SONGS — the audience isn't ready"

### §2.4 — The Tension: Evolutionary FEATURE, not Bug

**Critical insight** 🟡:

These two forces are NOT a design flaw. They are an **architectural tension intentionally evolved**:

- **Hardware only**: organism won't adapt → domain changes → dies
- **Domain only**: organism hyperlearns to burnout → resources exhausted → dies
- **BOTH**: oscillation between consolidation (hardware) and exploration (domain) → survive + thrive

→ Like the **immune system**: simultaneously remembers old pathogens (memory cells) and stays ready to respond to new ones (naive cells). Neither can be dropped.

The body's 3 signals map directly onto dual-pull:

| Signal | Direction | Dominant force |
|---|---|---|
| **Body-Reward** | "Good, keep going" | Both aligned → melody builds |
| **Body-Satisfaction** | "Enough, stop" | Hardware met (baseline reached) |
| **Body-Dissonance** | "Something's off, change" | Hardware OR Domain unmet → tension |

### §2.5 — "A Good Melody" — 4 Criteria (Personal-Melody §5)

🟡 Framework commitment:

> "A good melody" ≠ the most pleasant melody for the body. "A good melody" = a melody that meets **ALL 4 criteria simultaneously**:
>
> ① **Runs smoothly on your own hardware** (body-base comfortable, not forced — per-person)
> ② **Maps domain accurately** (reflects reality correctly, not a pleasant illusion)
> ③ **Maps domain as broadly as possible** (wide coverage = flexibility, adaptable to many situations)
> ④ **Playable long-term** (sustainable, livable, developable in the real world)

**Implication**: Evaluating a schema/melody is NOT just "does the body find it pleasant" (only ①) or "is it correct" (only ②-③). All 4 are needed.

**Examples of incomplete "good melodies"**:

| Case | ① Hardware | ② Accurate | ③ Broad | ④ Sustainable | Outcome |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Heroin addiction | ✅ max | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | Intensely pleasant body reward but domain punishes |
| Comfort zone | ✅ | ✅ partial | ❌ | ⚠️ stable but narrow | Doesn't expand |
| Workaholism | ❌ burnout | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ neural wear | Domain mapped but body collapses |
| Delusion | ✅ feels right | ❌ wrong | ❌ | ❌ reality clash | Body pleasant + domain punishes (Imagine-Final-Evaluation §4) |
| Passion (sweet spot) | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | Jensen Huang / Einstein cases |

### §2.6 — Body-Base Reward ≠ Domain Reward (critical split)

🟡 Framework commitment (Personal-Melody §5):

> **Body-base reward** pays out when the melody is SMOOTH. BUT smooth ≠ domain-correct. Body accuracy ~90% (Why-Body-Knows.md) → 10% CAN BE WRONG.
>
> - Addiction = strong body reward → domain PUNISHES (health)
> - Comfort zone = smooth body → domain stagnates (no progress)
>
> **Domain reward** pays out REAL reward (surviving, developing). Always correct BUT not always pleasant.
>
> - Learning something hard = body uncomfortable → domain rewards (new skill)
> - Waking early to exercise = body lazy → domain rewards (health)

**Why this split matters for the drill**: Every "reward case" (C1.1-C1.7, C2.1-C2.5, C3, C4) must be analyzed on **2 separate axes**:
- What does body-base reward pay out? (opioid, smooth melody preview)
- What does domain reward pay out? (skill, resource, status, survival)

Einstein breakthrough (C1.5) = both aligned. Heroin user = body only. Workaholism = domain only. Car matching expectations (C2.1) = both aligned per-person.

**Optimal = both simultaneously** = "Passion" = complete Imagine-Final (Imagine-Final §1 "body wants × domain confirms").

### §2.7 — "Passion" = 2 Forces Aligned

🟡 Framework:
> Melody that is both smooth (body enjoys) and maps domain well (domain accepts) = "I LOVE doing what the world ALSO NEEDS"

Cases the framework cites as passion achieved:
- **Jensen Huang + GPU**: 30 years consistent. Hardware (curiosity about graphics) + Domain (AI boom accelerated GPU importance) aligned from early
- **Einstein + physics**: Hardware (body felt "Newtonian physics isn't coherent enough") + Domain (reality confirms relativity via experiments)

Cases the framework cites as failed alignment:
- **Child forced to become a doctor**: Imagine-Final of parents (domain-driven external) + body doesn't feel → empty "skilled but resentful"
- **Making money because "you have to"**: Domain goal (money) + body not moved by money accumulation → hedonic trap (B4.1, drill P2 §9)

> **Externalization note**: Dual-Pull doesn't only exist in the brain — it PROPAGATES into every system humans create (software, law, medicine, organizations). Same tension, different form because domain differs. See **Collective-Body.md §3.5** — Dual-Pull Propagation.

## §3 — Interface Loop (6-Step Formalization)

### §3.1 — User's verbatim articulation (§0.5.1)

> "The interaction interface: domain => body => body feedback (PFC processes more carefully or unconsciously) => schema update => Action to domain => domain affects body => body feedback reward/dissonance => ..."

This is a **continuous cycle** — there is no start or end point. It describes the ongoing state of human ↔ world interaction.

The framework committed to this partially across multiple files:
- `Why-Body-Knows.md` §6 "Complete cycle"
- `Chunk.md` §3 (input → body check → compile → action)
- `Anchor-Schema.md` §1 6-step flow
- `Learning-Cycle.md` §3 8-stage cycle (for specific learning case)

This file **consolidates** them into a generic 6-step loop.

### §3.2 — 6-Step Loop Diagram

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                                                                 │
│  Step 1: DOMAIN → BODY sensory                                  │
│     (external reality → sensory organs)                         │
│                             ↓                                   │
│  Step 2: BODY → FEEDBACK                                        │
│     (unconscious 95% OR PFC gateway 5%)                         │
│     Body check: match known patterns? coherence?                │
│     Output: body signal (reward / dissonance / neutral)         │
│                             ↓                                   │
│  Step 3: FEEDBACK → SCHEMA update                               │
│     (compile new chunks / modify existing / reconsolidate)      │
│     Hebbian LTP + sleep consolidation                           │
│                             ↓                                   │
│  Step 4: SCHEMA → ACTION                                        │
│     (compiled schemas fire → motor output)                      │
│     PFC executes or unconscious auto-executes                   │
│                             ↓                                   │
│  Step 5: ACTION → DOMAIN                                        │
│     (body physically affects world)                             │
│                             ↓                                   │
│  Step 6: DOMAIN → BODY feedback                                 │
│     (reality responds → new sensory input)                      │
│     Body reward (if action worked) / dissonance (if not)        │
│                             ↓                                   │
│                        [LOOP back to Step 1]                    │
│                                                                 │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### §3.3 — Step 1: Domain → Body Sensory

**Mechanism**:
- External reality (light, sound, touch, temperature, smell, taste, internal state) → sensory organs → neural transduction → cortex
- **Exteroception** (5 channels): Vision, Audition, Olfaction, Gustation, Tactile
- **Proprioception** (3 channels): Body position, Vestibular, Kinesthetic
- **Interoception** (9 channels, per Body-Input-Enumeration §4): Thermoreception, Nociception, Respiratory, Cardiovascular, Visceral, Metabolic, Hormonal-sensed, Sleep/Circadian, **Self-signal interoception §4.9**

**Key point**: Step 1 is **boundary-crossing** — domain reality must pass through the body interface. There is NO "direct access" from PFC to reality. All knowledge about domain is filtered through body sensors.

🟢 Established: Perception is reconstructive, not a direct copy (Helmholtz, Gregory, modern predictive processing).

### §3.4 — Step 2: Body → Feedback

**Mechanism**:
- Sensory signal → unconscious processing (95%) or PFC gateway (5%)
- Body **checks** signal: does it match known patterns? Is it coherent with chunk background? Does it fit the Imagine-Final expectation?
- Output: body signal
  - **Reward** (opioid) if it fits body-need (§5 PFC-Analysis §8.3 step 5)
  - **Dissonance** (signal "something's off") if mismatch
  - **Neutral** if pattern = baseline, no delta

**Who handles it**:
- **Unconscious (95%)**: compiled schemas auto-check, prediction-delta detection, body-base simulation
- **PFC (5%)**: when dissonance signal is strong enough → PFC engages (Imagine-Final §1 "PFC reactive, body calls it")

**Why the split**: PFC bandwidth is too small for the full signal stream — unconscious handles the background scan, PFC only for novelty/conflict/decisions.

🟡 Framework (Chunk.md §3): "Unconscious = primary operator (~95%), PFC = secondary operator (~5%)".

### §3.5 — Step 3: Feedback → Schema Update

**Mechanism**:
- Body signal → triggers schema modification
- 4 compile pathways (Chunk.md §2):
  - **Repetition**: slow + stable (Hebbian LTP, Bliss & Lømo 1973 🟢)
  - **Emotional peak**: fast + deep (amygdala + NE, Brown & Kulik 1977 🟢)
  - **Multi-modal**: strong (wires across multiple cortex regions)
  - **Sleep consolidation**: offline integration (Walker 2017 🟢)
- Reconsolidation window: recall → temporarily unstable → can modify → re-compile (Nader 2000 🟢)

**Chunk.md §2 key claim** 🟡: "No one can delete a chunk. Chunks only STRENGTHEN / WEAKEN / MODIFY — they do NOT delete."

**Implication for the drill**: Trauma loop (B3), hedonic trap (B4), status shift (C5) — all involve the schema update mechanism. You cannot "reset" an old schema; you can only compile a new schema STRONG ENOUGH to override it.

### §3.6 — Step 4: Schema → Action

**Mechanism**:
- Compiled schemas fire → motor output
- 2 paths:
  - **Unconscious auto**: compiled skill (familiar driving, walking, eating) — PFC not involved
  - **PFC direct**: novel situation, decision, deliberate action — PFC spotlight + top-down boost

**Framework** (Anchor-Schema §1 step ②): "Most of life: step ② → schema EXISTS → skip to ⑤. Only when ② fails → PFC is called."

🟡 ~80% of behavior = compiled schemas auto-fire (Anchor-Schema §3 comparison table).

### §3.7 — Step 5: Action → Domain

**Mechanism**:
- Body movement → physical effect on world
- Speech → social effect
- Tool use → environmental modification

**Key point**: Step 5 is the **second boundary-crossing** — body → reality. The action ends on the body side; the effect happens on the domain side.

### §3.8 — Step 6: Domain → Body Feedback (loop closure)

**Mechanism**:
- Domain reality responds to action → new sensory input via Step 1
- **Reward signal** if action achieved the intended effect
- **Dissonance signal** if action failed or produced unintended effect
- **Partial signal** if mixed result

**Loop closure**: Step 6 triggers Step 1 again for a new cycle. The cycle never stops during waking life.

**Example (simple)**:
1. Hungry (Step 6 domain feedback: body-state delta)
2. Body signals hunger (Step 2)
3. Schema "find food" fires (Step 3 retrieval)
4. Walk to kitchen, open fridge (Step 4-5)
5. Eat food (Step 5)
6. Body satisfied (Step 6) → loop continues

**Example (complex — Einstein C1.5)**:
1. Read Newtonian physics (Steps 1-6, many iterations)
2. Body dissonance "not coherent" (Step 2)
3. Build chunks about physics (Step 3, years)
4. Discuss with Grossman (Steps 4-5)
5. Domain feedback: new formula matches reality (Step 6 external confirmation)
6. Extremely strong body reward (Step 2 back)
7. Loop continues to breakthrough

### §3.9 — Loop timing + who handles each step

| Step | Timing | Primary handler | PFC role |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 Domain → Body | ms (sensory latency) | Body sensors | None |
| 2 Body → Feedback | ms-sec | Unconscious 95% | 5% (when dissonance significant) |
| 3 Feedback → Schema | sec-days (immediate compile + sleep consolidation) | Unconscious (compile), PFC (direct create) | Create new chunks, reconsolidate recall |
| 4 Schema → Action | ms-sec | Unconscious auto 80%, PFC 20% | Choose + execute novel action |
| 5 Action → Domain | varies (physical) | Body motor | Monitor execution |
| 6 Domain → Body | varies (reality response) | Body sensors | Observe outcome |

**Key insight**: PFC is a **gateway**, not a **controller**. PFC receives + directs attention + makes novel decisions, but the unconscious runs the loop 95% of the time.

### §3.10 — Why the loop cannot be short-circuited

🟡 Framework claim:

> Every step in the loop is **architecturally necessary**. None can be skipped or compressed.

**Examples of attempted short-circuits that fail**:

- **"Understand from books, no need for experience"** → Skips Steps 5-6 (no action, no domain feedback)
  → Fake explicit (verbal-only chunks), body doesn't confirm → fragile, unusable in practice (Somatic-Articulation-Loop §1.3)

- **"Only PFC thinking needed, not the body"** → Skips Step 2 (no body signal)
  → No direction → existential boredom / decision paralysis (Imagine-Final §1 "dormant when harmony, fires when dissonance")

- **"Only need to feel it, no need to verify"** → Skips Steps 3-5 (no schema update, no action test)
  → Body rewards coherence (not truth) → can be wrong in domain (Why-Body-Knows §6 ①)

**Implication for the drill**: Cases like trauma loop (B3), hedonic trap (B4) typically involve **loop breakdown** — one step encounters a problem → the entire loop degrades → dissonance accumulates.

## §4 — Why Body "Knows" (2-Tier Calibration)

### §4.1 — The meta-question

> The framework says "listen to your body." But **why is the body trustworthy**?

If this can't be answered → the framework rests on something unexplained. Like saying "trust GPS" without explaining why GPS knows the route.

`Why-Body-Knows.md` §1 poses 3 core questions; this file consolidates the answers.

### §4.2 — Tier 1: Darwinian Selection (millions of years, gene-wired)

🟢 Research-backed:

> Natural selection = "MASSIVE trial-and-error" — millions of organisms × millions of years × millions of experiments. Genes that "reward the right pattern" get passed on. Genes that "reward the wrong pattern" get eliminated (their owner died).

**Wired-in examples**:
- Fire is hot → pain → avoid → gene "fire = pain" passes on
- Ripe fruit → sweetness → nutrition → gene "sweet = reward" passes on
- Snake → fear → avoid → gene "snake = danger" passes on

**Framework interpretation** 🟡:
> Body reward system = "survivor's GPS" — tested through millions of real journeys, keeping only the correct routes.

**Limit**: Evolution calibrates SLOWLY (thousands to millions of years). Environment changes FAST (a few hundred years for industrial society) → **evolution lag** (§4.6 below).

### §4.3 — Tier 2 Path 2a: Domain Contact (lifetime experience)

🟢 Established + 🟡 framework extension:

> Each person CONTINUES to calibrate the body against the CURRENT domain. Each experience = 1 cycle of "try → domain feedback → body calibrates."

**Mechanism**: chunk accumulation through DIRECT EXPERIENCE → body evaluates with increasing nuance:
- Little experience: body evaluates "good/bad" (binary, coarse)
- Extensive experience: body evaluates "this part is good, that part is bad" (detailed)
- Deep expertise: body evaluates "if I change this → better" (simulate variant)

→ "Expert" = body has more measuring tools, not "smarter."

**Relation to body-feedback vs feeling** (§5 below): Domain contact = substrate for the feeling layer to compile. Chunks from experience = the material for PFC to label body states.

### §4.4 — Tier 2 Path 2b: Trust-Injected — Culture (generational transmission)

🟡 Framework:

> Knowledge accumulated across generations — transmitted via word of mouth, books, education. Shared calibration: 1 person tries → everyone knows.
> ⚠️ Culture = an INPUT SOURCE for Tier 2 Hebbian, NOT a separate tier.
> Same compile mechanism, different entry point (through TRUST AMPLIFIER instead of direct domain contact).

**Examples**:
- Parent teaches "don't touch fire" → child doesn't need to try themselves (trust parent → compile)
- School teaches "gravity = F=mg" → student doesn't need to drop a stone 1000 times
- Books teach "history of war → lessons" → no need to experience it

**Mechanism**: External injection (Anchor-Schema §3 source 4) → trust amplifier → Hebbian compile → body-usable.

**Limit**: External injection can compile INCORRECTLY if the source is wrong → child believes "Santa Claus is real," adult believes "rich = happy." Trust-injected can transmit both truth and error.

### §4.5 — Tier 2 Path 2b: Trust-Injected — AI Era (unlimited chunks)

🟡 Framework claim:

> AI = amplifies Path 2b to an extreme degree. Contains the knowledge of all humanity, instant access. Speed of chunk transmission × 1000 compared to books.
> ⚠️ AI = 1 different Trust-Injected source, same Hebbian mechanism as culture, books, teachers.

**Mechanism**: Human asks AI → receives chunks → trust amplifier → body checks → Hebbian compile. AI = unlimited + instant chunk source.

**Limit**: AI only provides chunks — the body STILL must check. AI can be wrong → domain contact (Path 2a) is always the final arbiter.

**Implication**: AI doesn't replace Tier 1 or Path 2a. It **amplifies** the speed + breadth of Path 2b specifically.

### §4.6 — Coherence Check ≠ Truth Check

🟡 Critical distinction (Why-Body-Knows §3):

> Body checks **COHERENCE** (does the new pattern match already-known patterns?), NOT **TRUTH** (is the pattern correct in real domain?).
>
> - Coherence matches → opioid → "pleasant / understood / good"
> - No match → mild cortisol → "strange / uncomfortable / wrong"
> - Unknown → dopamine → "curious / need to investigate"

**Why coherence ≈ truth (most of the time)**:
- 2-tier calibration (Darwinian + Hebbian) has tested coherence against reality millions of times
- Most coherence = truth (because the chunk background is already accurate)
- Body accuracy ~90% 🟡

**Why coherence ≠ truth (the 10% gap)**:
- Flat Earth: "looks flat when you look straight" → coherent with visual experience → body OK → WRONG in actual domain
- Addiction: strong body reward → unconscious OK → but domain punishes
- Limerence: body intensely pleasant → "perfect" → hasn't yet been tested against domain over time

**Key claim**: Body rewards **COHERENCE**, not **TRUTH**. External verification is needed for the 10%.

### §4.7 — 3 Failure Modes (Why-Body-Knows §6)

🟡 Framework commits to 3 ways the body can err:

**① Evolution Lag** — body reward system correct for old environment, wrong for new:
- Sugar: rare in ancestral environment → reward "sweet = precious" → modern easy access → sugar addiction
- Social media: ancestral approval was rare → reward "likes" → modern unlimited → scroll addiction
- Fat/Salt: ancestral scarcity → reward → modern junk food → obesity epidemic

**② Wrong Chunk Background** — coherence matches but background is wrong:
- Flat Earth (classic)
- "Cortisol = stress hormone" (widespread misconception, Cortisol-Baseline §2.5 corrects this)
- Outdated scientific consensus

**③ Schema Override** — knowing something is wrong but still following it:
- Addiction: body "knows" sugar is harmful but compiled schema "sweet = reward" is too strong → overrides
- Procrastination: PFC knows work is needed but compiled "scrolling = reward" overrides
- Addiction generally: Schema overrides body check

**Implication for the drill**: Cases in P2 (dissonance) and P3 (reward) must consider all 3 failure modes. Trauma loop (B3) involves ② + ③. Hedonic trap (B4) involves ① + baseline shift.

## §5 — ⭐ Body-Feedback vs Feeling Architecture (CORE)

> **This is the LARGEST section of P1 Foundation**. Foundation for the entire complex feeling system. Directly addresses the user's distinction.

### §5.1 — Definition distinction

🟡 Framework commitment (consolidated from Somatic-Articulation-Loop §1 + Theme A §2.4 + Chunk.md §3):

| | **Body-Feedback layer** | **Feeling layer** |
|---|---|---|
| **What** | Raw body signal | PFC reception of integrated signals + chunk-based labeling |
| **Latency** | Automatic, pre-verbal | Requires chunks + reception time |
| **Substrate** | Body sensors + insula + ACC upstream integration | PFC + working memory + compiled labels |
| **Who does it** | Unconscious 100% | Unconscious integration → PFC reception interface |
| **Developmental** | Present from birth | Builds over years via mirroring + chunks |
| **Verbal** | Pre-verbal (no words needed) | Verbal (language emerges) |
| **Training** | Genetic + maturation | Mirroring + chunks + meditation |
| **Layer in 7-layer model** | Feel-RawSignals — Feel-Integration | Feel-Consciousification — Feel-Explanation |

**Critical**: These are NOT 2 separate systems. They are a **gradient** — body-feedback is the substrate, and the feeling layer builds on top of it through chunk accumulation.

### §5.2 — 3 States of Knowledge (Somatic-Articulation-Loop §1 verbatim)

🟢 + 🟡 Framework commitment:


#### §5.2.1 — IMPLICIT ("Body knows, PFC doesn't know it knows")

Knowledge compiled into the body (schemas, motor programs, emotional patterns, chunks) but PFC has no direct verbal access.

**Examples** (from Somatic-Articulation-Loop §1.1):
- **Motor**: riding a bike — can do it but can't explain "how to balance"
- **Language**: native speakers speak correct grammar but can't explain the rules
- **Social**: "walking into a room and feeling the heavy atmosphere" — body has processed hundreds of micro-signals
- **Emotional**: "meeting someone I don't trust, not knowing why"
- **Aesthetic**: "seeing a painting and finding it beautiful, can't precisely explain why"
- **Expertise**: chess master "sees" a good move without calculating

🟢 Research support:
- Implicit learning (Reber 1967+)
- Tacit knowledge (Polanyi 1958: "We can know more than we can tell")
- Expertise chunks (Chase & Simon 1973)
- Somatic markers (Damasio 1996)

#### §5.2.2 — TRANSITIONAL ("Felt sense" — Gendlin 1978)

Body-knowledge begins to surface into PFC awareness, but there are no words yet. PFC senses "something is there" (coherence signal) but doesn't know "what."

**Phenomenology** (Somatic-Articulation-Loop §1.2):
- **Vague** (unclear, not sharp)
- **Coherent** (something unified, not random noise)
- **Bodied** (felt somewhere — chest, stomach, shoulder, throat)
- **Resistant to labels** ("it's not exactly happy, not exactly sad")
- **Directional** (has a direction — positive/negative, open/tight)
- **Unstable if not articulated** — fades if not captured
- **Satisfying when correctly articulated** — body "settles"

**Examples**:
- Creative impasse: "something is wrong" — don't know what
- Decision ambivalence: "the job offer is theoretically good but body is hesitant"
- Relationship dissonance: "uncomfortable around that person, not knowing why"

🟢 Gendlin's research at Rogers lab (Chicago): clients with felt sense access had significantly better therapy outcomes. Measurable "felt shift" — galvanic skin response changes during successful Focusing sessions.

#### §5.2.3 — EXPLICIT ("Verbal + body both know")

Knowledge can be verbalized, communicated, taught, argued about. PFC has access; body still has signal. **BOTH** know.

🟡 **Critical distinction** — TRUE EXPLICIT vs FALSE EXPLICIT (verbal-only):

| | TRUE EXPLICIT | FALSE EXPLICIT (verbal-only) |
|---|---|---|
| PFC | Has words | Has words |
| Body | Has corresponding signal | No signal |
| Applicable | Can be used for real action | Theory only |
| Stable | Consistent over time | Fragile, easily lost when PFC is busy |
| Example | "I love my mother" (body warm when thinking of her) | "I should love" (PFC label, no body feeling) |

**Examples of false explicit** (Somatic-Articulation-Loop §1.3):
- Child taught "you should love others" but without experience → verbal exists, felt sense missing → cannot apply in real emotional moments
- Student memorizes math formulas without understanding → symbolic explicit, conceptual implicit missing → cannot apply to new problems
- Reading self-help books → knows all frameworks but cannot apply them → "knows but can't do" pattern

→ False explicit = chunks from source ④ External Injection (Anchor-Schema §3) without fill from ①②③
→ True explicit = chunks through FULL loop: body experience → implicit → transitional felt sense → articulation → explicit with body-confirmation

### §5.3 — 7-Layer Feeling Reception Model (Theme A §2.4+§2.7)

🟡 Framework commitment (Feeling.md + Theme A):

> Feeling = what PFC receives from unconscious processing. PFC has NO direct access to raw signals. Integration happens UPSTREAM (insula + ACC + VMPFC) before PFC sees anything.

**7-layer gradient** (same mechanism, different signal strength):

| Layer | Clarity | Description | Clear example (obvious hunger) | Fuzzy example |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Feel-RawSignals** | 100% body truth | Pre-integration signal | Gastric ghrelin signal | Schema partial fire |
| **Feel-Integration** | 95% | Upstream integration (insula + ACC + VMPFC) | Multi-channel stomach+blood glucose | Multi-schema integration |
| **Feel-Consciousification** | 90% | Signal strong enough to break into awareness | "Clearly hungry" | "Something is there..." |
| **Feel-Observation** | 85% | PFC notices signal exists | "I am hungry" | "I'm feeling something" |
| **Feel-Location** | 70-90% | PFC localizes signal | "Hungry in my stomach" | "In the chest but vague" |
| **Feel-Labeling** | 40-80% | PFC applies verbal label via chunks | "Hungry" | "Uneasy" |
| **Feel-Explanation** | 20-70% | PFC explains via narrative + chunks | "Because I haven't had breakfast" | "Because... I don't know" |

**Key claims** 🟡:

**Claim 1**: Body-feedback layer = Feel-RawSignals — Feel-Integration (pre-PFC). Feeling layer = Feel-Consciousification — Feel-Explanation (PFC reception).

**Claim 2**: **Clear signals** (hunger, obvious pain) reach Feel-Location — Feel-Labeling reliably. **Fuzzy signals** stay at Feel-Consciousification — Feel-Observation. These are not 2 different mechanisms — **same reception interface seeing signals of different strength**.

**Claim 3**: PFC has NO direct access to Feel-RawSignals raw. Integration upstream → Feel-Consciousification surfaces. PFC only receives Feel-Consciousification+ output.

**Claim 4** (Theme A §2.5): PFC does 3 functions (RECEIVE + LABEL + CHOOSE), does NOT generate feelings:
- RECEIVE (read integrated upstream signals)
- LABEL (via chunks — error-prone at Feel-Labeling — Feel-Explanation)
- CHOOSE (executive function between competing signals)
- NOT: execute interoception, run schemas, generate feelings, access raw body directly

### §5.4 — Self-Signal Interoception KEYSTONE (Body-Input-Enumeration §4.9)

🟡 Framework commitment — ARCHITECTURAL KEYSTONE:

> **Self-signal interoception = the body's capacity to READ its own internal state**. Meta-level sensing. Without it, other inputs fire but the body "cannot hear itself."
>
> = Prerequisite for feeling layer development regardless of how many other chunks exist.

**Substrate**:
- **Anterior insula (AI)**: primary interoceptive hub (Craig 2002, 2009 🟢)
- **Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)**: affective-motivational dimension, valence assignment
- **VMPFC**: integration of bodily feelings into decisions (Damasio somatic marker hypothesis 🟢)
- **Posterior insula**: first-order interoceptive representation
- **Predictive interoception** (Seth 2013, Barrett 2017 🟢): brain continuously generates predictions about body state, matches against actual

**3 levels of awareness** (Garfinkel et al. 2015 🟢):
1. **Interoceptive accuracy**: objective performance on heartbeat/respiratory detection tasks
2. **Interoceptive sensibility**: subjective self-report of body awareness
3. **Interoceptive awareness** (metacognitive): match between accuracy + sensibility = "knowing what you know about your body"

All 3 dimensions **trainable + variable + clinically relevant**.

**Why this is the keystone**:
- Body signals fire from §4.1-§4.8 (thermoreception, nociception, cardiovascular, metabolic,...)
- BUT if §4.9 reading capacity is compromised → PFC cannot access the signal → **silent deficit**
- Silent deficit accumulates without PFC awareness → feeling layer cannot develop
- → Architectural prerequisite, not a nice-to-have

**Compromise pathways** (§4.9 (c)):

🟢 **Clinical alexithymia** (Bird & Cook 2013): inability to identify + describe emotions — documented condition affecting **~10% of the population**. Strong correlation with:
- Autism (Bird research: alexithymia, not autism per se, drives emotional difficulties)
- Depression
- Somatic symptom disorders
- Eating disorders (cannot distinguish hunger from emotional states)

🟢 **Dissociation**: trauma adaptation, disconnection from body signals. Protective acutely, damaging chronically.

🟢 **Trauma-induced suppression**: body signals become dangerous (PTSD, complex trauma) → suppression learned, "body = enemy territory" (van der Kolk "The Body Keeps the Score")

🟡 **Chronic codependency**: constant attunement to others' states → own state awareness atrophies

🟡 **Helicopter parenting starvation**: children whose states are always intercepted → don't develop own interoceptive reading → alexithymic adults

🟡 **Modern screen culture**: constant external attention → minimal self-attention → atrophy of interoceptive capacity at a population level

**Rebuild pathways** (§4.9 (d)):

🟢 **Metacognitive body awareness** (mindfulness practices): Farb research — measurable improvements in interoceptive accuracy

🟢 **Documented meditation effects**: Vipassana, MBSR improve insula thickness + interoceptive accuracy. Hölzel 2011: 8-week MBSR → measurable insula changes via fMRI

🟢 **Gendlin focusing capacity**: specific technique for accessing felt sense — proto-verbal body knowledge

🟢 **Somatic therapy** (SE, sensorimotor psychotherapy): trauma-focused body-based therapy rebuilds interoceptive connection

🟢 **Contemplative traditions**: Buddhist body scan, yoga, Tai Chi — explicit interoceptive training methods

**Developmental origin** (§4.9 (b)):

🟡 **Caregiver mirroring**: infants learn interoceptive awareness by being mirrored — caregiver labels the baby's state ("you're hungry," "you're tired," "that hurt") → baby learns to read own states.

🟡 **Emotional labeling culture**: languages with rich emotional vocabulary support interoceptive development.

🟢 **Secure attachment**: foundation for interoceptive trust (Fonagy mentalizing research).

**→ Critical claim**: Self-signal interoception is **developmentally scaffolded** by caregiver mirroring during the infancy critical period. Adult training can partially compensate but not fully replace early scaffolding.

### §5.5 — Developmental Gradient (User's Examples Mapped)

🟡 User's verbatim examples (§0.5.6) mapped to framework:

#### §5.5.1 — Urination gradient

| Stage | Body-feedback state | Feeling layer state | 7-layer mapping |

#### §5.5.5 — Meta-principle from gradient

🟡 **Body-feedback is INVARIANT across developmental stages** (raw signal doesn't change much — same ghrelin, same nociceptor, same cortisol).

🟡 **Feeling layer is TRAINABLE** — via 3 pathways:
1. **Caregiver mirroring** (infancy critical period) — scaffolds Feel-Consciousification — Feel-Observation emergence
2. **Chunk accumulation** (lifetime) — populates Feel-Location — Feel-Explanation labels
3. **Meditation/somatic training** (adult) — refines access to Feel-Consciousification — Feel-Observation (felt sense)

🟡 **Critical implication**: "Not feeling" ≠ "no body signal." Body ALWAYS fires signals. The question is whether PFC can RECEIVE them (Feel-Consciousification+) and LABEL them (Feel-Labeling+).

→ **Alexithymia** = feeling layer underdeveloped despite body-feedback intact. Not "no emotions" — emotions present at Feel-RawSignals — Feel-Integration, absent at Feel-Observation — Feel-Explanation.

## §6 — Unconscious vs PFC: Division of Roles (Chunk.md §3)

### §6.1 — The architectural split

🟡 Framework commitment (Chunk.md §3):

> Chunk = DATA. **2 operators** work with the same database:
> - **Unconscious = PRIMARY OPERATOR** (~95% of processing)
> - **PFC = SECONDARY OPERATOR** (~5%, but determines direction)

### §6.2 — What the Unconscious CAN DO (from Chunk.md §3)

- **Detect**: body-base state (current state)
- **Compile**: patterns from experience (match/mismatch)
- **Run**: compiled schemas automatically
- **Create Imagine-Final**: expectation patterns at every scale (micro → long-term)
- **Check Imagine-Final body-base match**: "does reality match expectation?"
- **Pay reward/penalty**: opioid when match, cortisol when mismatch

**→ Unconscious is good at**: "HOW does body feel RIGHT NOW?" + "Is this pointing in the RIGHT DIRECTION?" — answers IMMEDIATELY, ACCURATELY — at the **body-base** level.

### §6.3 — What the Unconscious CANNOT DO

- **Check domain accuracy**: "is this smoothness actually correct in the domain?"
- **Simulate the future**: "what will happen in 5 years?"
- **Compare with memory**: "last time this pattern → bad outcome"

**→ Unconscious ONLY measures body-base** → does NOT measure domain accuracy.

**Implication**: If body is pleasant → unconscious says "OK" → **EVEN IF domain may be WRONG**.

**Examples** (Chunk.md §3):
- Addiction = body pleasant → unconscious "OK" → domain PUNISHES
- Limerence = body intensely pleasant → unconscious "perfect" → hasn't been tested against domain
- Comfort zone = body smooth → unconscious OK → domain stagnates

### §6.4 — What PFC CAN DO (that Unconscious CANNOT)

- **Simulate the future**: "if I continue → what result?"
- **Compare with memory**: "last time this pattern → bad outcome"
- **Check domain accuracy**: "body enjoys this BUT is reality sustainable?"
- **Check Imagine-Final against domain**: "body wants this direction → does domain allow it?"
- **Choose between competing Imagine-Finals**: short-term vs long-term
- **Create new evaluative chunks** → load into unconscious → UPDATE the database

**→ PFC is THE ONLY SYSTEM** that verifies whether Imagine-Final is feasible + correct in domain.

### §6.5 — What PFC CANNOT DO (that Unconscious CAN)

- **Feel directly**: PFC doesn't sense — it must RECEIVE from body
- **Process 95% of background**: PFC bandwidth is too small
- **Compile automatically**: PFC only drafts → unconscious does the actual compiling

### §6.6 — Why BOTH Are Needed

🟡 Critical architectural claim:

> Unconscious is sufficient for animals (simple domain, body-base well-calibrated via evolution). Humans evolved PFC TO COMPENSATE FOR the 10% where body-base can be wrong. PFC = the domain-checking system that the unconscious lacks.

**Consequences when one side is lost**:

**PFC switched off** (alcohol, drugs, extreme stress, limerence):
- Loses the only domain-checking system
- Unchecked body-base → may follow the wrong direction
- Examples: limerence, addiction, comfort zone, impulsive decisions

**Unconscious switched off** (impossible in practice, but hypothetical):
- PFC alone cannot feel → doesn't know what it wants
- No compiled skills → everything at slow PFC pace → not viable
- Animals suggest → cognition but no self-aware feeling system

**Balance when both operate well** = "a good melody" (§2.5) = body pleasant + domain correct.

**Implication for the drill**:
- Trauma loop (B3): PFC M2 damage → unconscious draft is poor quality → feeling layer degrades
- Hedonic trap (B4): PFC pursues domain goal (money) → unconscious body-base drifts → mismatch detected too late
- Car paradox (C2): different chunk base → same object → unconscious body-base check differs → different reward
- Schema update latency (C5): PFC receives positive info → unconscious hasn't updated compiled self-schema → coherence gap

## §7 — Architectural Claim: Schema Serves Body-Base

### §7.1 — The claim

🟡 Framework commitment (PFC-Analysis §8.3 step 5 + Architecture v7.5):

> **Schema serves body-base**. Body-base is the **SOURCE** of reward/dissonance. Reward = **evaluation function** — body decides which schemas are worth keeping.

**NOT**:
- ❌ "Schema drives behavior, body is output"
- ❌ "PFC decides, body follows"
- ❌ "Rational agent with body as constraint"

**IT IS**:
- ✅ "Body drives, schemas serve"
- ✅ "Unconscious primary, PFC receiver + director"
- ✅ "Embodied agent with PFC as extended capability"

### §7.2 — VTA/Opioid split confirms

PFC-Analysis §8.3 7-step loop step 5:
> "PFC receives chunk → integrates into workspace → PFC sends chunk DOWN to body-base: 'simulate → how does body feel?' Body-base simulates: Chunk MATCHES body-need → **opioid release → REAL reward**. Chunk DOESN'T match → body 'meh' → PFC discards."

**Key claim** 🟡:
- **Dopamine** (VTA) = signal "there's a change" (**doorbell**)
- **Opioid** (body-base) = reward "there's real value" (**gift behind the door**)

VTA detects delta → alerts PFC → PFC checks chunk → sends to body → body verifies. **Body is the final arbiter of value**.

### §7.3 — Implication: Reward is PERSONALIZED

🟡 Corollary:

> Schema serves body-base OF THIS PERSON. Different person → different body-base state → different reward for the same stimulus.

**Example** (Car paradox C2):
- Person A: body-need "want a car" pending → beautiful car → fills body-need → opioid
- Person A: body-need "want a car" doesn't fit a motorcycle → no opioid
- Person A: no body-need for airplane → no reward
- Person A: no body-need for Van Gogh → no reward
- Person B: body-need "vintage car with historical value" pending → vintage car → opioid
- Person B: doesn't see "new model car" filling a body-need

→ **Reward = PERSONALIZED function** of (body-need × chunks base × schema pending). Drill P3 §5 analyzes the 5-scenario matrix.

### §7.4 — Schema update requires body-base confirmation

🟡 Framework commitment:
> Schema update (Step 3 in the interface loop) is not just PFC "deciding to believe X." It requires body-base confirmation through repeated experience.

**Why C5 (status shift) takes time**:
- External information received (Feel-RawSignals — Feel-Integration)
- PFC attempts label (Feel-Labeling)
- BUT body-base self-schema unchanged
- Body "doesn't confirm" → PFC label is fragile
- Repeated external evidence + schema drifts over time → body gradually confirms → schema becomes stable

→ **Schema update has latency**. Cannot "decide to believe" instantly. Body must verify through multiple interface loop iterations.
