### §2.1 — Neural overlap: parenting + addiction

```
⭐⭐ PARENTING DÙNG CÙNG CIRCUITS VỚI ADDICTION:

  🟢 Strathearn et al. 2009 (30 first-time mothers, fMRI):
    Mẹ nhìn con cười → VENTRAL STRIATUM + hypothalamus fire
    = CÙNG VÙNG với cocaine reward
    Secure mothers: stronger activation + more oxytocin
    → Parenting reward = neurologically GIỐNG substance reward
    
  🟢 Rutherford et al. 2011, 2017 (reviews):
    Parenting + addiction share: VTA, nucleus accumbens, PFC, HPA axis,
    extended amygdala, ACC, insula, precuneus
    → "Reward-stress dysregulation model": addiction DISRUPTS
    same circuits that support sensitive caregiving
    → Implication: overparenting MAY BE dysregulated reward loop
    
  🟢 Fisher et al. 2004, 2010 (romantic love):
    In love → VTA bilateral (dopamine salience)
    Rejected → VTA + caudate + NAcc = cocaine withdrawal circuits
    → Romantic love = drive (same level as hunger, thirst)
    → Rejection = withdrawal response

  🟢 Burkett & Young 2012 (review):
    "Neurobiology of social attachment and pair bonding share
    molecular mechanisms with addiction"
    OT + opioid system → social bonding = "social addiction"
    
  🟢 Insel 2003:
    Social bonding = "addiction to specific individuals"
    OT receptor density → pair bond strength (prairie vole model)
```
