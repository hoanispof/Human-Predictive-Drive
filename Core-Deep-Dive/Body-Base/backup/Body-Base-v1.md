## §1 — ĐỊNH NGHĨA BODY-BASE

### §1.1 Formal definition

**Body-Base** = hệ thống các **body-input substrates** mà cơ thể phải
được fed để:
- Duy trì **homeostasis** (short term — minutes to hours)
- Stay **calibrated** cho optimal functioning (medium term — days to weeks)
- Maintain **long-term health và wellbeing** (years to decades)
- Support **higher-order cognition** + feeling system (upstream)

Đặc trưng bởi:
- **Pre-verbal**: body knows what it needs without PFC articulation
- **Evolutionary grounded**: 2M+ năm calibration through survival
  selection (Why-Body-Knows.md §4 Tier 1)
- **Signal-producing**: deviations từ baseline produce signals (loud
  hoặc silent depending on input + magnitude)
- **Multi-timescale operation**: signals operate trên different timescales
  (seconds cho L0 threats, minutes-hours cho L1 acute, weeks-months cho
  L1 slow-decay, years cho cumulative compound deficits)

### §1.2 Substrate reframe (2026-04-14)

Trước Phase R reframe test (2026-04-14), framework used **channel-based
model**: body-base có ~19 "channels" (Loud/Acute, Silent/Slow-Decay,
Higher-Order). Phase R test produced **substrate reframe** committed
as Phase C foundation.

**Substrate reframe — 3 layers**:

**L0 — Alive threshold**:
- Binary cease-function threats: oxygen deprivation (minutes), critical
  injury (immediate), extreme temperature (hours), water (days),
  starvation (weeks)
- **Qualitative property**: binary, không graded. Crossed = death.
- Suppresses all other processing (Body-Dissonance.md §2 emergency)
- Single-track survival response

**L1 — Body-inputs với adaptive baseline**:
- 17 sub-categories enumerated trong §4.2 below
- Each input có **evolved baseline range** + **individual baseline**
  shifted bởi development
- **Deviation below baseline → dissonance**
- **Deviation above baseline → reward → Novelty fires → baseline shifts
  upward** (hedonic treadmill mechanism)
- **Loss of elevated baseline → dissonance at new floor**
- 3 sub-categories theo neuroscience standard:
  - **Exteroception** (external world): vision, audition, olfaction,
    gustation, tactile
  - **Proprioception** (body position): proprioception, vestibular,
    kinesthetic
  - **Interoception** (internal state): thermoreception core,
    nociception, respiratory, cardiovascular, visceral, metabolic,
    hormonal-sensed, sleep/circadian, **self-signal interoception META**

**L3 — PFC drives as operators (không layer of needs)**:
- **Novelty** drive: shifts L1 baselines upward, seeks above-baseline
  inputs
- **Status** drive: organizes social environment để proxy L1 access
  (NOT body-input itself — explained §14.6 Body-Base-Example finding)
- **Protect** drive: guards L1 inputs từ threats, extends via Empathy-
  Mirror to others' body-states

**Tại sao 3 layers, không phải 2 hoặc 4**:
- L0 ≠ L1 vì binary (cease-function) vs graded (deviation magnitude
  scales response). Different mechanism cần qualitative split.
- L2 Quality layer **REMOVED** trong reframe. "Quality channels" (silk
  vs cotton, gourmet vs rice, music vs noise) reframed as L1 với
  baseline shifted upward by Novelty drive. Slow-decay phenomenon =
  baseline adaptation + asymmetric decay mechanism, không cần category
  riêng.
- L3 không phải layer of needs trên L1. L3 = **operators** acting on
  L1 substrate. Different functional role, không different architectural
  layer.

**"Channels" trong reframe** = vocabulary tiện cho practical guidance
+ phenomenological description. Channels là **aggregates** của body-
inputs + drives + schema, không primitives. §4.4 documents shorthand
rule.

**Reference**: Body-Input-Enumeration.md §1.2 cho full reframe specification.

### §1.3 Body-Base ≠ "physical needs" narrow

Traditional usage của "body needs" thường narrow = food/water/sleep
physiological. Framework expands:

- **Includes social needs** (Social Baseline Theory — agent co-presence
  inputs là body-level, không chỉ psychological)
- **Includes environmental needs** (Biophilia — nature exposure aggregate
  của visual fractals + auditory pink noise + olfactory phytoncides +
  thermal varied + circadian natural light, all body-level)
- **Includes movement** (ancestral 8-15 km/day walking documented qua
  Pontzer hunter-gatherer research)
- **Includes touch** (CT affective fibers, oxytocin pathway, Romanian
  orphan research validates developmental criticality)
- **Includes interoceptive states** (cardiovascular, respiratory, visceral,
  hormonal — body senses own internal state via §4.2.3.9 self-signal)
- **Acknowledges meaning** as primarily schema-level, không pure body-
  input cluster (clarification in reframe — meaning đặc biệt)

Tất cả qualify as body-base vì:
1. Body signals deficiency (physiologically measurable — cortisol, HRV,
   immune markers, sleep architecture, metabolic markers)
2. Evolutionary calibration exists (Tier 1 — Why-Body-Knows.md §4)
3. Deprivation has real health consequences (documented across research
   literature)
4. Deficiency pre-verbal (body knows before PFC articulates — explains
   §4.2.3.9 self-signal interoception keystone role)

### §1.4 Đặc tính critical

**(1) Body computes first, feeling emerges, PFC observes last**
(inherited từ Feeling.md definition)

**(2) Pre-verbal signals** exist độc lập với conscious awareness.
Alexithymia (clinical condition, ~10% prevalence — Bird & Cook 2013)
involves body signals firing without verbal access. Self-signal
interoception (§4.2.3.9 keystone) là biological mechanism cho reading
own state.

**(3) Multi-timescale operation** — signals operate across radically
different timescales:
- L0 immediate: pain, suffocation, danger (seconds)
- L1 acute: hunger, thirst, fatigue, cold (minutes-hours)
- L1 slow-decay: nature deficit, movement deficit, social depth deficit
  (days-weeks-months)
- L1 cumulative: chronic stress, baseline drift, microbiome dysbiosis
  (months-years)
- L1 developmental: childhood calibration shaping adult baselines
  (decades-permanent)

Framework phải handle all timescales, không chỉ acute.

**(4) Substrate-level analysis required**: surface-level patterns
(channels, behaviors, life choices) often misleading. Sustainable
patterns vs pathological patterns differ at substrate level (multi-
input feeding) more reliably than at surface level. Body-Input-
Enumeration.md §9 case tests demonstrate this — 15/15 cases analyzed
at substrate level reveal which are sustainable.

## §2 — BA BODY SIGNALS

[Brief overview — full detail trong Body-Dissonance.md §1-§2]

### §2.1 Signal types

Body có 3 primary signal types:

**Body-Satisfaction** = "Đủ rồi, dừng" = harmony ĐẠT
- Homeostasis met, no action needed
- PFC often doesn't notice (too quiet — interoceptive silence is good)

**Body-Reward** = "Hay, tiếp tục" = harmony đang BUILD
- Dopamine-driven forward movement
- Fires during progress, novelty, coherence, mastery
- Mechanism: positive prediction error (input above baseline)

**Body-Dissonance** = "Chưa ổn, thay đổi" = harmony BỊ PHÁ
- Widest spectrum (14 sub-types từ micro lấn cấn đến emergency ngất)
- Each sub-type drives different response
- Source-classified into 6 categories (Body-Dissonance.md §2.5)
- Reframe split: 3 sources body-input layer (L1) + 3 sources schema
  layer (PFC) — see §6.1 below

### §2.2 SHUTDOWN as circuit breaker

Reference Body-Dissonance.md §1: when delta quá lớn (either direction —
reward OR dissonance), body triggers SHUTDOWN bất kể direction. Vagal
syncope, freeze response, amygdala hijack. Magnitude-specific, không
direction-specific. Cross-cuts all 3 signals at extremes.

### §2.3 Signal quality — loud vs silent

**Loud signals**: produce acute verbalizable sensations
- Hunger pangs, thirst, pain, cold, fatigue (acute L1)
- Body-Dissonance §2 emergency-mạnh-trung spectrum
- Mainstream medicine well covers
- PFC easily notices và responds

**Silent signals**: produce diffuse non-verbalizable states
- Multi-input aggregate deficit (nature, movement, social depth)
- Slow accumulation, không acute crisis
- §6.3 mechanism explains via §4.2.3.9 self-signal erosion
- Hard to detect, hard to articulate, real consequences

**Reframe insight**: silent signals không separate signal type. Đây
là body-input dissonance (Body-Dissonance §2.5 Source 2 body-state
delta) at low intensity, accumulating gradually, reading capacity
(self-signal interoception) compromised.

## §3 — BỐN TẦNG CALIBRATION

[Brief overview — full detail trong Why-Body-Knows.md §2-§4]

### §3.1 Tại sao trust body signals?

Body signals đáng tin vì body has been calibrated qua **4 overlapping
tầng**:

**Tầng 1 — EVOLUTION** (triệu năm — gene-level):
- Natural selection = massive trial-error
- Genes producing correct signals survived
- Genes producing wrong signals eliminated
- Body hiện tại = "survivor of trillion trials"
- Reframe interpretation: Tier 1 establishes **evolved baseline ranges**
  cho body-inputs (§5.1 below)

**Tầng 2 — DEVELOPMENT** (đời sống cá nhân — experience):
- Mỗi person continues calibrating throughout life
- Chunks accumulate → body evaluations increasingly precise
- Personal domain (culture, language, profession) calibrated by
  experience
- Reframe interpretation: Tier 2 = **individual baseline shifting**
  within evolved range (§5.1 below)

**Tầng 3 — CULTURE** (qua thế hệ — shared knowledge):
- Culture shares calibration via teaching, tradition, language
- "One person tries, everyone learns"
- Dramatically accelerates individual calibration
- Reframe interpretation: Tier 3 = **collective baseline shaping**

**Tầng 4 — AI** (hiện tại — unlimited chunks):
- AI shares calibration at scale 1000× faster than culture
- Body still filters AI input (AI có thể wrong)
- Body check remains final arbiter

### §3.2 Implications cho body-base needs

All 4 tầng calibrated body FOR specific inputs:
- Evolution (2M năm) calibrated cho ancestral environment — natural
  visual fractals, pink noise auditory, varied tactile, daily walking,
  whole foods, natural light cycles
- Development calibrates cho specific cultural context
- Culture provides shortcuts via language (body signal vocabulary)
- AI era: new needs emerging, old needs endangered

**Framework insight (Why-Body-Knows §5 evolution lag)**: modern
environment mismatches evolutionary calibration → "silent deficiencies"
indicate this mismatch. Body calibrated FOR something modernity doesn't
provide.

### §3.3 Body có thể wrong — 3 error modes

Reference Why-Body-Knows.md §5:

1. **Evolution lag**: body reward calibrated cho old environment fails
   trong new (sugar scarce → "ngọt = reward" → modern sugar plentiful
   → over-consumption). Hall 2019 NIH directly measures.

2. **Bad foundation chunks**: body checks coherence, không truth. Nếu
   chunks wrong, "coherent" feels right despite being wrong. Requires
   external check.

3. **Schema override**: body knows wrong, vẫn does anyway. Addiction,
   procrastination. Body signal present but compiled schema overrides.

## §4 — BODY-INPUT SUBSTRATE

**⭐ CORE SECTION — reframe substrate model**.

Section này restructures body-base architecture từ "channels" (mô hình
cũ) to body-input substrate (Phase R reframe committed 2026-04-14).
Brief overview here — full enumeration với research + cases trong
**Body-Input-Enumeration.md §2-§4**.

### §4.1 L0 Alive threshold

**Binary cease-function threats** — input deviations crossing survival
threshold:

- **Oxygen deprivation**: minutes → cerebral hypoxia → death
- **Extreme hyperthermia/hypothermia**: hours → organ failure
- **Critical dehydration**: days → circulatory collapse
- **Starvation**: weeks → metabolic failure
- **Critical injury / blood loss**: immediate → shock
- **Suffocation, drowning, electrocution**: seconds → neural death

**Qualitative property**: binary. Threshold crossed → homeostatic
function collapse → single-track survival response. Suppresses all
other processing.

**Distinct từ L1**: L1 có thể deviate mà function maintained (đói, mệt,
khó chịu nhưng functional). L0 crossed → function không còn.

**Evolutionary logic**: L0 inputs có signals **loud, universal, non-
suppressible** vì mistakes here = dead. Negativity bias + loss aversion
amplified ở L0 (Baumeister 2001, Kahneman & Tversky 1979).

**Reference**: Body-Input-Enumeration.md §1.2.1 cho L0 detail.

### §4.2 L1 Body-inputs với adaptive baseline

**The core substrate layer**. 17 sub-categories trong 3 standard
neuroscience taxonomies.

#### §4.2.1 Exteroception — sensing external world (5 inputs)

**Vision**: photons, fractal dimension D ≈ 1.3-1.5 (Taylor 2006), spatial
patterns, biological motion, faces (FFA), depth cues, light cycle
signaling. Ancestral baseline: 12-hour daylight cycle, fractal natural
environments, biological motion prevalent, mixed vegetation + open sight
lines (Savanna hypothesis, Orians 1992).

**Audition**: sound pressure waves, frequency, location, voices, pink
noise (1/f) natural soundscape (Voss & Clarke 1975). Ancestral baseline:
tribal voices 30-150, environmental ambient (lá xào xạc, suối, gió),
silence only temporary.

**Olfaction**: ~400 receptor types (Buck & Axel 1991, Nobel 2004),
direct limbic access. Ancestral baseline: natural mixed smells (vegetation,
earth, food, fire, bodies, animals, phytoncides — Li 2010 Shinrin-Yoku).
Olfactory adaptation FASTEST của all senses (minutes for receptor).

**Gustation**: 5 basic tastes + fat (6th — Running 2015). Distinct from
flavor (taste + retronasal olfaction + tactile mouthfeel). Ancestral
baseline: ~30-35% carbs (complex), 20-35% protein, 30-40% fat (Eaton &
Konner 1985), seasonal variation, refined sugar/salt absent.

**Tactile skin**: thermoreception (TRP channels) + mechanoreception
(Meissner/Pacinian/Merkel/Ruffini) + nociception cutaneous + **affective
touch (CT fibers — Löken 2009, McGlone 2014)**. CT system specifically
for social grooming, infant care, pair bonding via oxytocin pathway.
**Critical**: CT affective touch là separate pathway từ discriminative
touch — sex ≠ hugs ≠ massage despite all "touch."

**Reference**: Body-Input-Enumeration.md §2 cho 8-field schema mỗi input.

#### §4.2.2 Proprioception — sensing body position (3 inputs)

**Proprioception**: muscle spindles + joint receptors + Golgi tendon
organs + efference copy → **body schema** (Sherrington 1906 foundational).
Limb position awareness without vision. Ancestral baseline: varied
postures throughout day, ground-level living, weight-bearing activities,
balance challenges.

**Vestibular**: inner ear semicircular canals + otoliths → head orientation,
acceleration, gravity. Integration với vision via VOR. Ancestral baseline:
varied walking + running + climbing motion patterns. NOT prolonged
stillness hoặc passive high acceleration.

**Kinesthetic**: muscle activity dynamics, exertion, fatigue, recovery,
metabolic signals (lactate, adenosine). Distinct từ proprioception
(focuses on dynamics, không position). Ancestral baseline: walking
8-15 km/day documented (Pontzer hunter-gatherer research), varied
exertion + recovery cycles.

**"Movement channel" reduces** đến proprioception + kinesthetic +
vestibular + cardiovascular + metabolic + endorphin/BDNF compound. B9
Blue Zone farmer fed all simultaneously; B5 karoshi starved all
simultaneously.

**Reference**: Body-Input-Enumeration.md §3 cho detail.

#### §4.2.3 Interoception — sensing internal state (9 inputs)

**Critical category** cho slow-decay phenomena. Most channel-lexithymia
barriers trace to interoceptive gaps. Includes architectural keystone
(§4.2.3.9 self-signal META).

**§4.2.3.1 Thermoreception core**: hypothalamic preoptic area, core
temp ~37°C set point. Distinct từ skin thermoreception (§4.2.1 tactile).
Ancestral baseline: diurnal variation ~0.5°C, behavioral thermoregulation
primary. Crosses L0 at extremes.

**§4.2.3.2 Nociception**: A-delta fast + C-fiber slow pain, TRP receptors,
substance P, gate control (Melzack & Wall 1965). Dual dimension: sensory-
discriminative (where, intensity) + affective-motivational (how bad
feels). CIPA (SCN9A mutation, Cox 2006) = lethal proof pain necessary.

**§4.2.3.3 Respiratory**: breath rate, depth, CO2 chemoreception (primary
trigger), nasal vs mouth breathing, vagus nerve integration via RSA
(Porges polyvagal). **Unique**: voluntary + involuntary, bridge between
conscious + autonomic.

**§4.2.3.4 Cardiovascular**: heart rate, blood pressure, HRV (interoceptive
gold standard — Schandry 1981, Thayer + Lane), vagal tone, baroreceptors.
**Cardiovascular là Craig insula's MAIN interoceptive input** (Craig
2002, 2009). High HRV = healthy autonomic flexibility.

**§4.2.3.5 Visceral**: enteric nervous system (~100-500M neurons), gut-
brain axis via vagus nerve (**80% afferent**, body listens to gut more
than commands), microbiome signaling, satiety integration (CCK, leptin,
PYY). Mayer 2016, Cryan & Dinan, Gershon 1998 "Second Brain."

**§4.2.3.6 Metabolic**: blood glucose, hydration, ghrelin/leptin
hormones, hypoglycemia detection. Crosses L0 at severe. Hall 2019 NIH
clinical trial directly measures supernormal stimulus baseline shifting
mechanism trong food.

**§4.2.3.7 Hormonal-sensed states**: cortisol, testosterone, oxytocin,
dopamine, serotonin, endorphins, melatonin, etc. Not directly
interoceptive (no dedicated receptors), but **become inputs via
downstream effects on autonomic + cardiovascular + visceral systems**.
Hormonal systems show **strongest hedonic treadmill patterns** —
addiction research directly validates baseline shifting.

**§4.2.3.8 Sleep / Circadian**: sleep architecture (REM/NREM), SCN
master clock, melatonin signaling, adenosine homeostatic pressure,
chronotype variation. Unique: state-transition input, không just signal.
Walker "Why We Sleep", Foster circadian research.

**§4.2.3.9 Self-signal interoception (META keystone)** ⭐:

**Architectural keystone** của reframe. Body's capacity để **read** its
own internal state. Without §4.2.3.9 functional, other inputs fire but
body "cannot hear" → silent deficits accumulate without PFC awareness.

**Mechanism**:
- **Insula** (especially anterior insula) — primary interoceptive hub
  (Craig 2002, 2009)
- **Anterior cingulate cortex (ACC)** — affective-motivational dimension
- **Predictive interoception** (Seth 2013, Barrett 2017) — body actively
  predicts own state, prediction errors update model
- **Garfinkel 3-dimensional model** (2015): interoceptive accuracy
  (objective heartbeat detection) / sensibility (subjective awareness) /
  awareness (metacognitive match)

**Developmental basis**: caregiver mirroring during infancy → child
learns to read own states. Securely attached children develop strong
self-signal. Helicopter parenting (B15 Body-Base-Example) hoặc
parentification (B12) → self-signal atrophy → adult alexithymic
tendencies.

**Clinical alexithymia** (Bird & Cook 2013, ~10% population): inability
to identify + describe emotions. Strong correlation với autism, depression,
somatic symptom disorders, eating disorders.

**Why architectural keystone**: §4.2.3.9 explains "silent deficiency
phenomenon" mechanism. Body fires deficit signals (from any L1 input),
but if reading capacity compromised, PFC doesn't access → deficits
accumulate silently.

**Cases validating role**: B11 codependent (chronic outward attunement
→ self-signal atrophy), B12 parentified, B15 helicopter parent (causes
B11/B12 patterns developmentally), B25 contemplative practice (trains
self-signal), B35 screen addiction (constant external attention demand
suppresses self-signal), B46 Proenneke (developed strong self-signal
trong solo decades — explains sustainable solo).

**Therapeutic targets**: mindfulness practices, Gendlin focusing, somatic
therapy (van der Kolk), Vipassana — all train §4.2.3.9.

**Reference**: Body-Input-Enumeration.md §4.9 cho extensive coverage
of this architectural keystone.

#### §4.3.2 Status drive

#### §4.3.3 Protect drive

**Function**: guards L1 inputs từ L0/L1 threats. Operates at multiple

**Self-protection**: avoiding direct body damage, reflex responses, risk

**Extended self**: offspring, family, in-group (Hamilton inclusive

**Empathy-Mirror level**: mirrored body-states của others trigger Protect

1. Observe other in body-state (suffering, hungry, threatened)
2. Empathy-Mirror creates weak copy của other's state trong own body
3. Own L1 body-inputs fire (lower intensity)
4. Protect drive activates to resolve dissonance
5. Action: help other → resolves mirrored state → own dissonance resolves
6. Reward via mirror resolution

**Explains "altruistic" behavior** without mysterious component. Helping
other = feeding own L1 via mirror mechanism. User's "giúp bộ đội có
gạo" question answered: empathy mirrors soldiers' suffering + schema

## §5 — BASELINE ADAPTATION MECHANISM

**Theoretical core của reframe**. Section này explains baseline shifting
mechanism that makes "quality channels" trong mô hình cũ unnecessary
as primitives.

### §5.1 Evolved + individual baselines

**Evolved baseline ranges** (Tier 1 calibration, Why-Body-Knows.md §4):

Mỗi body-input có **evolved baseline range** calibrated qua 2M+ năm
natural selection. Baselines không random — chúng là **compiled survival
wisdom** stored trong genome.

Examples documented:
- **Vision**: fractal dimension D ≈ 1.3-1.5 (Taylor 2006), 12-hour
  daylight cycle
- **Audition**: pink noise (1/f) ambient (Voss & Clarke 1975), tribal
  voices 30-150
- **Tactile**: frequent affective social touch
- **Kinesthetic**: 8-15 km/day walking (Pontzer)
- **Gustation**: ~30-35% carbs / 20-35% protein / 30-40% fat (Eaton &
  Konner 1985)
- **Sleep**: sunset-to-sunrise alignment
- **Hormonal**: cortisol diurnal cycle với recovery, oxytocin frequent

**Individual baselines** (Tier 2 calibration, Why-Body-Knows §4 +
Body-Input-Enumeration.md §5.2):

Mỗi person shifts baseline within evolved range via 4 sub-mechanisms:
1. **Neural adaptation** (sensory cortex response curve adjustment)
2. **Receptor sensitivity adjustment** (downregulation/upregulation)
3. **Behavioral habituation** (predictive processing — expected stimuli
   subtracted)
4. **Schema-level calibration** (PFC compiled schemas about "normal")

**Critical periods**: some baselines locked during developmental sensitive
periods (native phoneme by age 7-10 — Kuhl 1992, attachment style by
age 1-3, taste preferences early childhood, color category perception,
**§4.2.3.9 self-signal interoception** developmental foundation).

**Cumulative exposure**: small daily changes × sustained time = large
shift. Years của urban life cumulate auditory baseline shift, decades
sedentary cumulate kinesthetic baseline lowering.

### §5.2 Delta rule + Novelty operator

**Core mechanism formalism** (Helson 1964 adaptation-level theory,
Rescorla & Wagner 1972 learning rule):

```
  BL(t+1) = BL(t) + α × (input_quality(t) - BL(t))

  where:
    BL(t)            = current baseline
    input_quality(t) = current input
    α                = learning rate (varies by input category)
    BL(t+1)          = updated baseline
```

**Novelty drive** fires on **positive prediction error** (input > baseline
expected). Mechanism:

1. Body detects input quality via receptors
2. Compares to baseline expectation
3. If input > baseline → positive prediction error → VTA dopamine →
   reward → seek repeat
4. Repeated exposure → baseline shifts upward
5. Same input now AT new baseline → no prediction error → no reward
6. Seeking escalation cho re-experience positive PE
7. **Hedonic treadmill established**

**Empirical validation**: Hall 2019 NIH Cell Metabolism clinical trial.
Same participants, same calories offered, ultra-processed diet → +500
kcal/day overconsumption. **Direct experimental measurement của baseline
distortion mechanism**.

**Universal across body-input categories**: Novelty drive operates trên
any input. One mechanism explains:
- Food (Hall 2019, B31)
- Sex (B16, B13)
- Visual (B35 screen addiction)
- Auditory (engineered music)
- Social validation (likes, B36 fame)
- Substances (all addictions)
- Mastery (B30 Jiro — same mechanism, constructive direction)

**Research foundation**: Brickman & Campbell 1971 hedonic treadmill,
Frederick & Loewenstein 1999, Tinbergen 1951 supernormal stimulus,
Barrett 2010, Berlyne 1960 optimal novelty, Zajonc 1968 mere exposure,
Schultz dopamine PE, Kahneman wealth-happiness, Wilson & Gilbert
affective forecasting.

**Mechanism is morally neutral**: same baseline shifting can be exploited
pathologically (addiction) OR constructively (mastery progression).
Direction matters, không mechanism itself.

### §5.3 Asymmetric decay

**Core asymmetry**: baseline **shifts UP fast**, baseline **shifts DOWN
slow**.

**Pattern A — Input removed → baseline stays elevated → dissonance at
new floor**:
- Silk → cotton: cotton feels rough
- Gourmet → simple food: simple feels bland
- Affective touch frequent → deprivation: painful absence
- Music produced compressed → natural: feels thin

Over weeks-months baseline re-adapts downward, removed input no longer
causes dissonance. Rate varies by input (olfactory fastest, hormonal
slowest).

**Pattern B — Input continuously high → habituation → no longer reward**:
- Gourmet every meal → expected
- Constant affective touch → expected (absence still acute)
- Luxury accommodation routine → "deserved"

**Asymmetry evolutionary logic**: evolution favors quick exploitation
of new opportunities (fast UP) + slow relinquishment of old assets
(slow DOWN). Trade-off optimizing survival.

**Modern consequence**: engineered supernormal stimuli hijack "up"
mechanism rapidly, recovery to ancestral baseline takes much longer.
Cumulative population-level baseline drift upward over generations
(sugar tolerance, screen time, noise, etc.).

**Replaces "pattern fire + storage + decay" concept** từ original outline
với cleaner mechanism. Storage IS elevated baseline. Decay = slow
re-baselining. 3 concepts → 1 mechanism. Parsimony gain.

**Reference**: Body-Input-Enumeration.md §5 cho full mechanism detail.

## §6 — DISSONANCE SUBSTRATE

Section này maps **6 sources từ Body-Dissonance.md §2.5** onto reframe

### §6.1 Body-input vs schema layer split

Body-Dissonance.md §2.5 documents 6 nguồn dissonance. Reframe re-classifies
into 2 architectural layers:

**Body-input layer (L1 substrate)** — 3 sources:
- **Source 2 — Body-state delta**: direct L1 deviation (đói, mệt, nóng,
  lạnh, khát). This **IS** reframe's substrate mechanism. Existing
  framework already implicitly recognized.
- **Source 4 — Physical damage**: nociception L1 (mild-moderate gradient)
  hoặc L0 territory (severe tissue damage)
- **Source 5 — Social damage**: aggregate of agent co-presence inputs
  going silent (vision faces + audition voices + tactile CT + olfaction
  + hormonal oxytocin + mirror disruption). Not separate category —
  compound L1 deficit.

**Schema layer (PFC level)** — 3 sources:
- **Source 1 — Information mismatch**: PFC prediction error, schema-
  level expectation violated
- **Source 3 — Threat schema**: PFC anticipatory prediction về future
  L1 threats (lo lắng, sợ tương lai)
- **Source 6 — Melody learning**: PFC schema restructuring during chunks
  reorganization. Arc valley dissonance trong learning.

**Clean 3+3 split**. Reframe interpretation:
- Body-input dissonance = L1 deviations, fast onset, specific
  localization, direct neural substrate, resolved via L1 fix (eat,
  sleep, warm up, restore contact)
- Schema dissonance = PFC prediction errors, slower onset, diffuse
  localization, executive substrate (ACC, dlPFC), resolved via cognitive
  processing (info, planning, reappraisal, time)

**Both feel như "khó chịu"** to PFC observation, nhưng different
mechanisms + different resolutions. Framework should distinguish cho
intervention purposes.

**Real-world dissonance usually mixed** — multiple sources active
simultaneously. Job loss = schema threat (Source 3) + L1 cortisol
(Source 2 secondary) + sleep disruption (Source 2) + anticipatory
ruminating (Source 3).

**Reference**: Body-Input-Enumeration.md §7.1 cho extensive 6-source

### §6.2 Self-signal interoception keystone

**§4.2.3.9 self-signal interoception** (from §4.2.3 above) là architectural
prerequisite cho reading any body-input dissonance.

**Mechanism**: body fires deficit signals from any L1 input. PFC
observation (Feeling folder layer) requires self-signal interoception

**Critical role**: §4.2.3.9 is **prerequisite** cho body-base health,
không just one-of-many inputs. Equivalent role to "executive function"

**Why architectural keystone**:

- Modern attention capture (B35) suppresses self-signal cumulatively
- Codependent attunement (B11) forces self-signal atrophy
- Therapeutic targets all train self-signal (mindfulness, focusing,

### §6.3 Silent deficiency mechanism

**"Silent deficiency phenomenon"** từ original outline reframed as:

```
  Body-input deficit accumulating
            +
  §4.2.3.9 self-signal interoception compromised
            ↓
  Body fires dissonance signals (L1 deviations real)
            +
  PFC reading capacity reduced
            ↓
  Deficits accumulate without PFC awareness
            ↓
  "Silent" deficiency — diffuse "khó chịu không rõ vì sao"
```

**Multi-input compound + self-signal erosion = silent deficiency**.
Either component alone produces less severe pattern:
- L1 deficits với strong self-signal: PFC notices, takes corrective
  action, dissonance not "silent"
- Strong self-signal với mild deficits: notices early, low severity

Compound creates pathology.

**Common modern pattern** (B5 karoshi, B1 workaholic, B35 screen
addiction): chronic external attention demand suppresses self-signal +
multi-input L1 starvation simultaneously → silent compound deficit
reaches eventual crisis threshold.

**Reframe explains mechanism cleanly**. Mô hình cũ described "silent
deficiency phenomenon" descriptively; reframe provides mechanism via
§4.2.3.9 self-signal as keystone.

intervention design framework.

precise adaptation rates, biomarkers, dose-response cho interventions.

future quantitative data. Reframe positions body-base framework cho
