        Compiled → Tool-mode

    Hormone fading → schema INJECTS motivation → maintains behavior

    ① IAT (implicit) and self-report (explicit) = NO CORRELATION

    ③ IAT at baseline DID predict trajectory over 4 years
    ④ Negative implicit → more likely to SEPARATE within 1 year

    → Body: MAY BE DIFFERENT (compiled negative tags invisible to PFC)
    → = Body KNOWS, PFC DOESN'T

  🟢 Gottman (1990s longitudinal):
    Physiological markers (heart rate, cortisol)
    PREDICTED satisfaction changes over 3 years
    → Body-level indicators MORE predictive than verbal reports
