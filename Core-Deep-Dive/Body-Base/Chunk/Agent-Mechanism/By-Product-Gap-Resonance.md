## §2 — SCOPE: 4 CONDITIONS + DRIVE RIÊNG + WHAT ISN'T RESONANCE

### §2.1 — 4 Conditions ĐỒNG THỜI cho resonance (★ NEW v1.1)

```
⭐⭐ 4 ĐIỀU KIỆN CẦN ĐỒNG THỜI:

  ① POSITIVE VALENCE — approach dominant:
    Cả 2 entity fill gap qua approach (novelty, interest, curiosity).
    Approach × Approach = self-reinforcing → highest quality.
    Avoidance × Avoidance = break khi threat removed → lowest quality.
    Mixed = phổ biến nhất → quality tùy ratio.
    🟢 Elliot 2006: approach/avoidance motivation.

  ② MUTUAL AGENT-MODE — Self-Pattern-Modeling ON cả 2 phía:
    Entity predict state CỦA NHAU, không chỉ predict output.
    Tool-mode (predict action→outcome) → dissolves khi goal ends → 0 resonance.
    Agent-mode (predict entity state→response) → CÓ THỂ compile → bền vững.
    = Self-Pattern-Modeling phải ON. (Self-Pattern-Modeling §1: Modeling = active forward simulation.)

  ③ GAP OVERLAP — gap distributions overlap đủ:
    By-product match REQUIRES gap direction overlap.
    Overlap CAO → match probability CAO → resonance DỄ emerge.
    Overlap THẤP → vẫn possible nhưng cần PFC effort.
    Gap overlap = Trục ① trong 5 Trục (§5).

  ④ DRIVE RIÊNG MAINTAINED — entity phải CÓ gap + drive riêng:
    Resonance = mutual by-product match.
    By-product = output khi entity fill gap CỦA MÌNH.
    → Entity PHẢI CÓ gap + drive riêng → mới CÓ by-product.
    Drive riêng bị suppress → mất by-product → resonance CHẾT.
    = TẠI SAO Compiled-Suppress = LEVERAGE POINT in Resonance Decline (§10).

  THIẾU 1 CONDITION → KHÔNG PHẢI RESONANCE:
    Positive nhưng 1 chiều → parasocial.
    Mutual nhưng Tool-mode → transactional.
    Agent-mode nhưng gap KHÔNG overlap → mismatch.
    Overlap nhưng drive bị suppress → over-clone → decay.

🟡 4 conditions framework = synthesis from multiple drills.
🟢 Individual conditions: established (per-citation).
```
