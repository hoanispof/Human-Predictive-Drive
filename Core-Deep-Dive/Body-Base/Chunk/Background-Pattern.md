## §2 — 2D MODEL: COMPILE DEPTH × LINK DENSITY

### §2.1 — Two Independent Dimensions

```
⭐ SCHEMA DEPTH HAS 2 INDEPENDENT DIMENSIONS:

  DIMENSION 1 — COMPILE DEPTH (existing, Schema.md §4.1):
    = How STRONGLY each chunk is wired
    = f(emotional peak × multi-modal × repetition per-chunk)
    → Bitten by a dog: 1 event × emotional peak × multi-modal = HIGH depth
    → Studying boring material: many repetitions × no emotion × single modal = LOW depth
    → = HOW STRONGLY each chunk is wired

  DIMENSION 2 — LINK DENSITY (new concept):
    = How MANY OTHER CHUNKS the pattern is linked to over time
    = f(duration × ongoing experience × context consistency × sleep cycles)
    → 10 years in the same context: HIGH density (thousands of linked chunks)
    → 1 isolated event: LOW density (few linked chunks)
    → = HOW WIDELY the pattern is connected

  INDEPENDENT: any combination is possible:
    → High Depth + Low Density: bitten by a dog once
    → High Depth + High Density: chronic childhood abuse
    → Low Depth + Low Density: "it's raining, bring an umbrella"
    → Low Depth + High Density: cultural "don't make trouble for others"
```
