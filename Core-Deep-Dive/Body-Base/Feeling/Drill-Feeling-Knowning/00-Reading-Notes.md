### §1.3 — [Feeling-Accuracy.md](../Feeling-Accuracy.md) (1874 lines) notes

#### §1.3.1 — §1 Temporal Order Invariant — **CORE cho Theme A**

**The core invariant**:
> "Body computes → Feeling emerges → PFC observes. This order is **NEVER REVERSED**. PFC KHÔNG generate feeling. PFC cannot create body state."

**4 evidences**:
1. **Libet research** 🟢: Unconscious brain activity precedes conscious decision by 300-500ms
2. **Damasio VMPFC patients** 🟢: VMPFC damage → logic + memory + language intact, feeling-based decision LOST → paralyzed
3. **Interoception** 🟢: Insula integrates body state BEFORE cortical processing. PFC does NOT access raw body signals directly.
4. **Gendlin felt sense** 🟢: 50+ years clinical — "felt sense cannot be generated by thinking. Must be ACCESSED, not created."

**Implications**:
- A. "Think yourself into feeling" = **impossible**. Có thể trigger schema → schema trigger body state (indirect). Nhưng không thể directly will feeling into existence.
- B. PFC can INHIBIT feeling expression, not generate source.
- C. Awareness matters: Body computes whether PFC notices or not.

**→ CRITICAL cho Theme A**: Framework claim rõ — Feeling is fully body-based, PFC OBSERVES. Answer user's meta-question: NO, chúng ta KHÔNG ép feeling vào body-base — framework stance là feeling THỰC SỰ thuộc body-base, PFC observes + (indirectly) triggers via schema.

**Philosophical note**:
> "Hard problem of consciousness still open. Framework does NOT claim to solve it. Framework claims: at FUNCTIONAL level, body-first is observable."

#### §1.3.2 — §3 6 PFC Error Modes — **Theme A + Theme E relevant**

1. **RATIONALIZATION** — Body fires, PFC creates plausible story. VD: "Feel irritated → coworker is annoying" vs actually "hungry + slept badly + over-stimulated". Nisbett & Wilson 1977 🟢.

2. **MISATTRIBUTION** — Physical ↔ emotional label swap. VD: hungry → "angry" (hangry), anxiety → "stomach problems". Schachter & Singer 1962 🟢.

3. **DISPLACEMENT** — Feeling về A → directed at B (safer target). VD: "Stressed from boss → yell at spouse". ← **Theme E relevance**: "Khi nào giúp mà thấy khó chịu" có thể là displacement.

4. **OVER-COMPLEX LABELING** — Simple body need → grand narrative. VD: "existential dread" when actually bad sleep + skipped breakfast. Intellectual trap for over-educated.

5. **UNDER-LABELING / DENIAL** — Strong feeling → "I'm fine". Alexithymia + cultural training (stoic, male socialization).

6. **CULTURAL SCRIPT OVERRIDE** — Actual feeling overridden by culturally acceptable label. VD: men "tender longing" → forced into "physical want". Barrett granularity insight.

**Root causes** (§3.7):
- Compression mismatch (multi → single)
- Linguistic structure (SVO grammar forces single-cause)
- Cultural script limits
- Self-concept protection
- Social pressure to explain
- Single-cause bias (evolutionary efficiency)
- Temporal myopia
- Agency preservation
- Feel-Explanation bias

#### §1.3.3 — §4 Pain Special Case — **relevant Theme D (if pain as right/wrong signal)**

**4.1 Location accuracy**:
- **Somatic pain** (skin, muscles, joints): HIGH accuracy (well-mapped somatosensory cortex, precise within cm)
- **Visceral pain** (organs): LOW accuracy (convergent afferents → referred pain: heart attack → LEFT ARM, appendicitis → initially periumbilical, diaphragm → shoulder)

**4.3 Emotional pain = physical pain circuits**:
> "Eisenberger 2003: social pain activates ACC, same as physical pain. Subsequent: rejection, grief, heartbreak all real pain circuits. 'Broken heart syndrome' (Takotsubo) real."

**4.4 Psychogenic pain is REAL pain**:
> "Schema fires pain circuits. Same neural pathways as 'real' pain. 'Psychogenic' DOES NOT mean 'fake'."

**→ Theme D connection**: "Cảm thấy có gì đó không đúng" might trigger same circuits. Both wrongness feelings (body Feel-Consciousification) + cognitive error detection (ACC) = body signals.

#### §1.3.4 — §5-7 Feeling Literacy Training

> "We understand others by **simulating their state in our own body**."

#### §1.13.1 — Core distinction Imagine-Final vs Anchor-Schema — **CRITICAL cho Theme A**

**Framework's new distinction**:
> "IMAGINE-FINAL = SẢN PHẨM CỦA PFC (top-down). PFC tạo ra, build, optimize, loại bỏ."
>
> "ANCHOR-SCHEMA = YÊU CẦU CỦA VÔ THỨC (bottom-up). Vô thức CẦN sync point để dàn nhạc đồng bộ."

**Why concept needed**:
Imagine-Final KHÔNG giải thích được 4 cases:
1. "Biết muốn gì nhưng KHÔNG hành động" → Imagine-Final có, Anchor-Schema chưa
2. "Không giải thích được tại sao nhưng BIẾT phải làm" → Compiled schema đã TỰ thành anchor, Imagine-Final mờ
3. Động vật không có PFC vẫn drive có hướng → Anchor tồn tại không cần PFC
4. Tôn giáo work mà không cần PFC hiểu sâu → direct-to-unconscious anchor

**→ CRITICAL cho Theme A + Theme F**: Framework có bidirectional architecture:
- **Top-down** (PFC → vô thức): Imagine-Final, Logic
- **Bottom-up** (vô thức → PFC): Anchor-Schema, Feeling, compiled schemas
- Both operate simultaneously, feedback loops
- **NOT pure top-down PFC observation** — vô thức has its own requirements

#### §1.13.2 — §1 Dàn nhạc metaphor + 6-step flow

**Vô thức = dàn nhạc**: Hàng nghìn compiled schemas LUÔN fire theo trigger riêng. Parallel, không dừng.

**Anchor-Schema = main player**: 1-2 schemas được "bật to" hơn → cả dàn nhạc sync theo.

**Không có Main Player** → cacophony → "chán/lost/anxiety".

**6-step flow từ dissonance tới sync**:
1. **Body-base dissonance** (trigger)
2. **Anchor-Schema scan** — compiled schema có sẵn? → nếu CÓ skip tới ⑤
3. **PFC scan + imagine** (nếu ② fails) — generate candidates
4. **Body evaluate + choose** — body confirm + PFC confirm
5. **Anchor amplify → vô thức sync** — schemas liên quan fire mạnh hơn
6. **Domain feedback → re-evaluate** — loop

**KEY**:
> "BƯỚC ② LÀ BƯỚC QUAN TRỌNG NHẤT. Phần lớn cuộc sống: bước ② → CÓ anchor sẵn → skip tới ⑤. Vô thức handle ~95%. PFC = backup, không phải primary."

**→ Theme A**: ~95% of feeling/decision doesn't involve PFC actively. PFC engages only when compiled anchor fails (~5%).

#### §1.13.4 — Positive vs Negative Trust — **CRITICAL cho Theme E giving dynamics**

**Positive Trust** — "Tôi tin reward PHÍA TRƯỚC":
- Anchor hướng TỚI cái hay
- Body feeling khi dissonance: "khó nhưng XỨNG"
- Reward: opioid → "xứng đáng"
- **Sustainable**: reward per step → reinforce cycle

**Negative Trust** — "Tôi tin sẽ TRÁNH harm":
- Anchor TRÁNH XA cái dở
- Body feeling: "khó, nhưng dừng sẽ TỆ HƠN"
- Reward: cortisol DROP → relief ("phù, thoát")
- **NOT sustainable**: relief = diminishing, cần threat tăng dần → **NEURAL WEAR**

**Comparison table**:

| | POSITIVE TRUST | NEGATIVE TRUST |
|---|---|---|
| Anchor | TỚI cái hay | TRÁNH XA cái dở |
| Dissonance feel | "Khó nhưng xứng" | "Khó nhưng dừng sẽ tệ hơn" |
| Reward | Opioid ("pleasant") | Relief ("hết đau") |
| Sustainable | CÓ | KÉM |
| Long-term | Melody smooth | Neural wear (cortisol chronic) |

**Mix is common**: "Founder startup: trust 'product vision' (positive) + trust 'hết tiền nếu dừng' (negative)"

**→ CRITICAL cho Theme E**: **Directly decomposes user's giving cases**:
- **Cây xoài dư → hàng xóm** = POSITIVE trust (reward: mirror vui + mirror reward + community belonging)

**Framework's position**: Both are valid anchor types, but NEGATIVE TRUST long-term = neural wear. Healthy giving = positive trust based.
