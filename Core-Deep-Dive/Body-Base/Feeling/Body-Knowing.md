### §1.2 — 3 Properties

```
⭐ 3 ĐẶC TÍNH DEFINING BODY-KNOWING:

  ① COMPILED (prerequisite)

     Body-Knowing REQUIRES compiled chunks. No chunks = no Body-Knowing.
     Infant: minimal Body-Knowing (few compiled chunks).
     Expert: deep Body-Knowing (thousands of compiled chunks in domain).
     Compilation = Hebbian: repeat + verify + reinforce → automatic pathway.

     → Body-Knowing KHÔNG bẩm sinh (trừ few innate reflexes)
     → Body-Knowing = PRODUCT of experience + compilation
     → Richer experience → richer Body-Knowing library

  ② BODY-DIRECT (mechanism)

     Body-Knowing bypasses PFC deliberation.
     Fire through compiled pathways: basal ganglia, cerebellum, insula,
     established cortical connections.
     Cost ≈ 0 (path đã tạo sẵn, no PFC draft needed).

     → Nhanh: near-instant (milliseconds, not seconds)
     → Rẻ: no PFC processing load (bypasses serial bottleneck)
     → Vô thức-driven: body fires TRƯỚC PFC awareness
     → 🟢 Libet 1985: body activity 300-500ms before conscious awareness

  ③ PFC-ENCODABLE (experience)

     Person "just knows." Body-Knowing crosses PFC observation threshold.
     PFC CÓ THỂ detect result — dù không luôn articulate HOW or WHY.
     CÓ THỂ articulate (if domain = shareable), KHÔNG CẦN articulate.

     → Distinguish from pure implicit (body acts, PFC unaware):
       Pure implicit = body acts, person doesn't "know" they know
       Body-Knowing = body acts AND person "knows" (PFC detects result)
     → = Gendlin "felt sense" (1978): body knows before words arrive
     → = Polanyi "tacit knowledge" (1958): "we know more than we can tell"

  🟢 Compiled processing: Hebbian learning established
  🟢 Body-first: Libet 1985, Damasio 1994 somatic markers
  🟢 Tacit knowledge: Polanyi 1958, Reber 1967 implicit learning

### §5.4 — Mechanism: Multi-Weak-Signal-Convergence + Convergence Zone

```
⭐ MECHANISM BEHIND ALL PRE-BODY-KNOWING STATES:

  MULTI-WEAK-SIGNAL-CONVERGENCE (Feeling.md §7.3):
    Multiple chunks fire WEAKLY and simultaneously.
    Each individual signal: too weak for PFC to detect alone.
    But AGGREGATE: body detects coherent pattern → fires somatic signal.
    PFC detects "something" but CANNOT isolate source.
    = "Vague" is the system working CORRECTLY on complex input.
    NOT malfunction — correct response to multi-dimensional signal.

  CONVERGENCE ZONE (Feeling.md §7.4):
    = Region in semantic space where activation from MANY chunks OVERLAP
    = NOT a single compiled chunk
    = Intersection of many activation paths — "crossroads without street names"

    Properties:
      ① No label → PFC can't "grasp" it clearly
      ② No single source → cannot point to specific chunk
      ③ HAS location in semantic space (intersection of paths)
      ④ HAS direction — approximate sense of "over there"
      ⑤ DYNAMIC — becomes clearer as more chunks fire coherently

    Resolution:
      New chunk COMPILED from convergence zone pattern.
      Feeling was FIRST → chunk comes AFTER.
      = Pre-Body-Knowing = PREVIEW of a chunk that doesn't exist yet.
      = Body detecting REAL pattern that chunk system hasn't formalized.

  GAP-DIRECTION CONNECTION (Gap-Direction.md §1):
    Pre-Body-Knowing = gap WITH direction but PFC can't yet encode it.
    "Chưa biết = không có gap" → but pre-Body-Knowing = gap IS forming.
    Direction exists BEFORE PFC can articulate it.

  🟢 Tip-of-tongue phenomenon: Brown & McNeill 1966
  🟢 Incubation: Wallas 1926 (unconscious convergence → insight)
  🟢 Expert intuition: Klein 1998 (many weak signals fire coherently)
  🟡 Convergence Zone as structural concept = framework synthesis

### §5.5 — Expert vs Novice Reliability

```
⭐ PRE-BODY-KNOWING RELIABILITY DEPENDS ON COMPILED BASE:

  EXPERT (large compiled library in relevant domain):
    Pre-Body-Knowing signals = MORE TRUSTWORTHY.
    Many chunks → strong convergence → signal reflects real pattern.
    Firefighter "something's wrong" → EVACUATE → building collapses.
    Trustworthiness: HIGH (domain-specific, verified by outcomes).

  NOVICE (small compiled library):
    Pre-Body-Knowing signals = LESS TRUSTWORTHY.
    Few chunks → weak convergence → signal may be noise.
    Student "something's wrong" → CANNOT identify what → may be noise.
    Trustworthiness: LOW (insufficient patterns to match against).

  CROSS-DOMAIN CAUTION:
    Expert in domain A, novice in domain B:
    Pre-Body-Knowing in A = trust. Pre-Body-Knowing in B = caution.
    Mathematician's hunch about math = reliable.
    Mathematician's hunch about relationships = no more reliable than anyone.

  CONDITIONS FOR RELIABLE INTUITION (Kahneman + Klein 2009 🟢):
    ① High-validity environment (regular patterns exist)
    ② Adequate practice (enough compiled chunks)
    ③ Quick feedback (domain verifies compiled patterns)

### §5.6 — 3 Reactions to Pre-Body-Knowing (Somatic-Articulation-Loop §2.3)

```
⭐ WHEN PRE-BODY-KNOWING SIGNAL FIRES — 3 POSSIBLE REACTIONS:

  REACTION A — DISMISS ("probably nothing, ignore it")
    Most common reaction — especially in verbal-dominant cultures.
    Signal fades. Implicit knowledge does not surface.
    Opportunity missed.
    Cultural training: "irrational = untrustworthy" → dismiss body signals.

  REACTION B — OVER-TRUST ("I feel it, so it must be true")
    Adopt signal as absolute truth without domain verification.
    Risky: body CAN be wrong (trauma, bias, projection, Self-Referencing patterns).
    Can lead to: confirmation bias, delusional thinking, bad decisions.
    → Pre-Body-Knowing signal ≠ truth. Signal = invitation to investigate.

  REACTION C — ENGAGE WITH HUMILITY ("something's there, let me explore")
    Acknowledge signal without dismissing or over-trusting.
    Attempt articulation. Admit uncertainty.
    Open to refinement.
    = "Trust but verify" applied to body signals.
    → FRAMEWORK RECOMMENDS Reaction C.

  Historical precedent: Darwin (1840s-1858)
    Had pre-Body-Knowing about evolution for years.
    Wrote to Lyell & Hooker with extreme hesitation:
    "It is like confessing a murder."
    = Classic Reaction C: engage with humility, decades of verification.
```

### §5.7 — Distinguishing Somatic Signal from Fabrication (Somatic-Articulation-Loop §2.3)

```
⭐ HOW TO DISTINGUISH PRE-BODY-KNOWING FROM "MAKING THINGS UP":

  GENUINE PRE-BODY-KNOWING SIGNAL:
    → Body has distinct feeling (location: chest, gut, throat)
    → "Pulled toward" topic — attention keeps returning
    → Frustration when phrasing attempt fails (body rejects wrong words)
    → Satisfaction when phrasing is close ("settle" feeling)
    → Not boring — alive, pressing, wants attention

  FABRICATION (no actual body signal):
    → Body neutral (no somatic feeling)
    → Made up to fill conversation silence or expectation
    → No resistance to changing story (no body-filter)
    → Not "pulled toward" anything
    → Boring to self — no engagement

  RULE OF THUMB:
    If you feel "pulled" to keep exploring it → pre-Body-Knowing signal.
    If you can drop it without any body reaction → likely fabrication.

  ⚠️ CAUTION — TRAUMA-SOURCED SIGNALS:
    Some pre-Body-Knowing signals originate from PAST TRAUMA,
    not current reality.
    "I don't trust this person" → may be because they RESEMBLE
    a past abuser, not because they are actually untrustworthy.
    Body detects PATTERN but pattern may be OLD, not CURRENT.
    → Cross-check with domain evidence is always necessary.

  🟢 Trauma and somatic hijack: van der Kolk 2014
  🟢 Somatic markers: Damasio 1996
  🟡 Fabrication vs signal distinction = framework synthesis (from Somatic-Articulation-Loop §2.3)
```

### §5.8 — Value: Do NOT dismiss, Do NOT over-trust

```
⭐ PRE-BODY-KNOWING = VALUABLE SIGNAL:

  DUAL CHECK (Ask-AI.md v3.1 §6.1):

## §8 — COMPILED/FRESH SPECTRUM

> **Source:** Inter-Body-Mechanism.md v2.0 §3, Logic-Feeling.md v3.0 §1.2,
> Body-Feedback-Label.md v2.1 §8.
> Body-Knowing = compiled end. Fresh = not-yet-Body-Knowing end.

### §8.1 — Spectrum, Not Binary

```
⭐ BODY-KNOWING = COMPILED END CỦA SPECTRUM:

  COMPILED (Body-Knowing) ──────────────── FRESH (Not-yet-Body-Knowing)
  automatic                                     PFC deliberate
  body-direct                                   PFC-mediated
  cost ≈ 0                                      cost > 0
  "biết mà không cần giải thích"                "phải nghĩ ra"
  near-instant                                  cần thời gian
  Hebbian reinforced                            mỗi lần = effort mới

  SPECTRUM, KHÔNG PHẢI BINARY:
    → Mỗi chunk nằm SOMEWHERE trên spectrum
    → Mỗi moment = tổng hợp nhiều chunks ở nhiều positions
    → "Bán-compiled": steps 1-3 automatic, step 4 needs fresh draft
    → Expert có thể "partially stuck": Body-Knowing phần quen, fresh phần mới
    → Semi-compiled = semi-Body-Knowing
```

### §8.2 — Transition Both Ways

```
FRESH → COMPILED = LEARNING (Body-Knowing formation):
  Repeat + verify OK → Hebbian strengthen → automatic → Body-Knowing.
  VD: Einstein — toán FRESH (tuổi nhỏ) → COMPILED (adult)
  VD: Driver — lái xe FRESH (ngày 1) → COMPILED (ngày 1,000)
  VD: Therapist — case types FRESH (year 1) → COMPILED (year 10)
  = §4 Formation Process

COMPILED → FRESH = DISRUPTION (Body-Knowing loss):
  Context shift / error detected / trauma → phải re-evaluate deliberately.
  VD: Bạn thân betray → compiled positive DISRUPTED → fresh re-evaluate
  VD: Career shift → old domain compiled → new domain = FRESH lại từ đầu
  VD: New evidence → old Body-Knowing invalid → must re-learn

  → Body-Knowing KHÔNG PHẢI permanent:
    Can form (learn), can dissolve (disruption), can reform (re-learn)
    Dynamic, not static. Responsive to environment.
```

### §8.3 — 3-Cost Model

```
⭐ FRESH PROCESSING = 3 INDEPENDENT COSTS:

  ① PFC DRAFT COST:
    → PFC phải chain novel paths → processing load (serial bottleneck + catecholamine)
    → 🟢 Kurzban 2013: cognitive effort = opportunity cost signal

  ② SUPPRESS COST:
    → Must suppress compiled patterns that fire automatically
    → Old Body-Knowing fires → new situation requires override → effort
    → Double cost: draft new + suppress old

  ③ UNCERTAINTY COST:
    → Fresh = no prior data on outcome → body cannot predict reward
    → Uncertainty itself = costly (body = predictive machine, holding open = cortisol)
    → 🟢 Peters & Büchel 2010: uncertainty = aversive signal

  → Body PREFERS compiled (Body-Knowing) over fresh: lower total cost.
  → Body RESISTS switching from compiled to fresh: 3 costs.
  → = Why changing mind is HARD even when evidence says to change.
  → = Why "unlearning" is harder than "learning" (2 costs vs 1 cost).

  Source: Inter-Body-Mechanism.md §4 (3 independent cost sources)
```
