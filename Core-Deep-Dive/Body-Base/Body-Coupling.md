# Body-Coupling — The Mechanism of Deep Body-Entity Bonding

> **A mother nurses her sick child through the night. The child is still sick. She keeps nursing.**
> **A rival goes bankrupt. You feel relief. Then a week later — emptiness.**
> **A colleague of ten years resigns. You think: "Oh, okay."**
> **Strict parents. You feel simultaneously loving and angry. AT THE SAME TIME.**
>
> Four agents. Four responses that are fundamentally different. Why?
>
> Not because of "how much feeling" is present.
> But because the body has COUPLED with each agent at a DIFFERENT LEVEL.
>
> Mother: child = body-base extension (merged channels).
> Rival: enemy = body-base entanglement (opposed channels).
> Colleague: ❸ ≈ 0 = no coupling (replaceable instance).
> Strict parents: simultaneously extension AND entanglement = MIXED coupling.
>
> Same underlying mechanism: |❸| per-agent valence compiles deep enough
> → the body treats the entity AS PART OF the body-base.
> Direction (positive/negative/mixed) determines coupling TYPE.
> Depth (deep/shallow) determines whether coupling forms at all.
>
> Love deeply → LONG MEMORY. Hate deeply → ALSO LONG MEMORY. Neutral → FORGOTTEN.
> Because |❸| determines persistence — NOT direction.
>
> v3.0: Mixed coupling = THE MOST COMMON outcome.
> "Simultaneously loving and angry" = NORMAL, not pathological.
> The closer and longer a relationship → the more channels compile on both sides → MIXED emerges naturally.
>
> But WHY does a mother keep caring for her child without burning out? → Hardware-Subsidy MAXIMUM.
> Why does a close friendship of twenty years gradually fade? → Hardware-Subsidy = NONE — must self-maintain.
> Why does exhaustion at work → no patience at home? → PFC budget ALREADY DEPLETED.
> Why does Level 5 "must be here" feel like coupling OR addiction? → Entity-Access gradient.
