### Session 3: ADHD × Consciousness Architecture
**Topic**: ADHD as Natural Experiment for Consciousness Mechanism
**Status**: `DONE` (2026-07-01) → Drill-ADHD-Consciousness.md v1.0

```
MỤC TIÊU:
  ① Map ADHD through 5-Checkpoint model:
     - CP1 (TRN gate): ảnh hưởng? → PFC → TRN → selective gating impaired?
     - CP2 (Tonic mode): ảnh hưởng? → Probably NOT (ADHD conscious normally)
     - CP3 (High-order nuclei): ảnh hưởng? → CM-Pf signaling?
     - CP4 (Cortical recurrence): ảnh hưởng? → Probably NOT
     - CP5 (Global broadcast): ảnh hưởng? → YES — PFC hold duration impaired
     → Prediction: ADHD = CP5 impaired, CP1-CP4 intact

  ② Map ADHD through 3-parameter model:
     - Signal strength: ĐỦ (dopamine production normal, clearance fast)
       → Nhưng signal DURATION short → functionally WEAKER over time
     - Anchor quality: KHÔNG trực tiếp ảnh hưởng
       → NHƯNG: short duration → less time to BUILD anchor
       → = Indirect anchor impairment via duration
     - Attention: BIMODAL (hypo for mundane, hyper for rewarding)
       → DRD4 threshold → binary: filter OR lock-on

  ③ Map ADHD through sustain hierarchy:
     - Primary sustain (thalamo-cortical resonance): INTACT
       → = ADHD vẫn CÓ consciousness bình thường
       → = Critical evidence: primary sustain ≠ PFC-dependent
     - Additional sustain (PFC WM hold): IMPAIRED
       → = ADHD consciousness QUALITY giảm (duration short)
       → = Hyperfocus = when additional sustain RESTORED by reward dopamine
     - KEY INSIGHT: ADHD = natural dissociation 
       giữa primary vs additional sustain

  ④ Hyperfocus as consciousness quality restoration:
     - Reward dopamine → PFC hold RESTORED → consciousness quality = normal+
     - = CÙNG HARDWARE, chỉ cần đủ dopamine → quality phục hồi
     - = Strongest evidence for "dopamine = additional sustain fuel"
     - Duration during hyperfocus: hàng GIỜ (vs normal 2-5 giây per pattern)
     - BUT: resolution during hyperfocus = NARROW (deep but narrow)

  ⑤ Meta-consciousness in ADHD:
     - Trẻ ADHD: KHÔNG BIẾT mình quên nhanh hơn (no frame of reference)
     - Lớn lên: biết qua external comparison (prediction-delta accumulation)
     - "Knowing you forget" = meta-consciousness (Level 3)
     - ADHD meta-consciousness: DELAYED development? Or DIFFERENT mechanism?

  ⑥ Framework predictions (testable):
     - P1: ADHD consciousness threshold HIGHER → fewer patterns enter stage
       → Testable: subliminal priming studies in ADHD
     - P2: ADHD pattern duration on stage SHORTER
       → Testable: WM decay rate measurement
     - P3: Hyperfocus = additional sustain RESTORED
       → Testable: compare WM performance during hyperfocus vs mundane task
     - P4: ADHD forgetting = encoding failure, not retrieval failure
       → Testable: recognition vs recall performance dissociation

CÂU HỎI CẦN TRẢ LỜI:
  Q1: ADHD có phải "natural experiment" tốt nhất cho consciousness?
      → Hay có condition khác tách primary/additional sustain rõ hơn?
  Q2: Hyperfocus consciousness quality: TRÊN hay BẰNG bình thường?
      → Nếu TRÊN: tại sao? (deeper focus? fewer distractions?)
  Q3: ADHD awareness of ADHD qua các lứa tuổi:
      → Age nào bắt đầu meta-consciousness về own ADHD?
  Q4: ADHD × dreaming: consciousness quality khi ngủ mơ có khác không?
      → Dreaming = PFC offline → ADHD PFC already under-fueled → double hit?
      → Hay dreaming = primary sustain only → ADHD dreaming = NORMAL?
  Q5: Medication (methylphenidate) × consciousness quality:
      → Medication restores PFC hold → consciousness quality improves?
      → = Additional evidence for PFC = additional sustain
```
