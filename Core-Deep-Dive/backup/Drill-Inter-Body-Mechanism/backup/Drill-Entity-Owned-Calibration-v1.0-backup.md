# Drill: Entity-Owned Calibration — How Entity-Owned Self-Regulates (or Fails To)

> **Entity-Owned = predict access + gap expectation (Entity-Owned v2.0).**
> **Nhưng BAO NHIÊU Entity-Owned là ENOUGH?**
>
> **Quá ít → bond atrophy (no bids → no EC → drift apart).**
> **Quá nhiều → entity autonomy violated (suppress drive riêng).**
> **Goldilocks = vùng giữa. KHÔNG CỐ ĐỊNH.**
>
> **Calibration cần 3 LAYERS ĐỒNG THỜI:**
> **Layer 1 — Body Signal: "tôi đang cảm gì khi muốn access?"**
> **Layer 2 — Engine Simulation: "entity sẽ experience gì nếu tôi access/ép?"**
> **Layer 3 — Feedback Integration: "entity respond thế nào? → adjust?"**
>
> **Failure ở BẤT KỲ layer nào → calibration FAIL.**
>
> **Exit Cost = Signal Weight:**
> **Bạn bè: exit dễ → "no" = signal mạnh → TỰ calibrate.**
> **Parent→child: exit impossible → "no" = signal yếu → chỉ INTERNAL calibration giúp.**
>
> **Hardware Subsidy = Calibration BIAS:**
> **Hormone fires → Entity-Owned FEELS justified → C masked as A.**
> **= Biology itself creates bias toward Entity-Owned inflation for kin.**
>
> **Compiled Entity-Owned behaviors = BELOW PFC → calibration IMPOSSIBLE.**
> **Fresh Entity-Owned = PFC active → calibration POSSIBLE.**
> **Trục 4 depleted → shift Compiled → auto-pilot → excess.**
>
> **Engine use quality (Simulation Engine §9):**
> **Curiosity mode → productive calibration → thú vị → Generative reward.**
> **Threat mode → rumination → calibration FAIL → "mình tệ quá?"**
>
> **Optimal Zone ≠ Zero C. Justified C = NECESSARY (Baumrind 1966).**
> **Bids for connection = mild C = bond MAINTENANCE (Gottman 2001).**
>
> **KHÔNG THỂ PRESCRIBE "Entity-Owned bao nhiêu là đủ."**
> **= Infinite regress (Logic-Feeling-Balance §6-7).**
> **= Domain feedback = trọng tài DUY NHẤT (Reward-Calibration §5).**
> **= Observe → adjust → observe → adjust = LOOP = relationship.**

## §4 — HARDWARE SUBSIDY = CALIBRATION BIAS

```

  Entity-Owned v2.0 §13: Hardware Subsidy = ANTI-HABITUATION.

  NHƯNG CÙNG CƠ CHẾ = CALIBRATION BIAS:
  
    Mechanism:
      Hormone fires (oxytocin, vasopressin) → Entity-Owned FEELS justified
      → "Tôi thương thật" = PARTIALLY TRUE (A hormone = genuine)

      Mẹ nhìn con:
        A fires: oxytocin (existence reward) → genuine → "thương con"
        C fires: anxiety ("con phải ăn cho mẹ yên") → self-referential
        → Body signal = MIXED (opioid + cortisol CÙNG LÚC)
        → PFC interpret TOÀN BỘ as "thương con" (A explains everything)
        → C INVISIBLE vì A provides "cover story"
        → = Hardware subsidy MASKS excess Entity-Owned

  SO SÁNH PER BOND TYPE:
  
    ┌──────────────┬───────────┬───────────────┬─────────────────────────┐

    ├──────────────┼───────────┼───────────────┼─────────────────────────┤
    │ Con→mẹ      │ MODERATE  │ MODERATE      │ Moderate: attachment    │
    │              │           │               │ drive present but ↓      │
    ├──────────────┼───────────┼───────────────┼─────────────────────────┤
    │ Romantic     │ TEMPORARY │ HIGH→LOW      │ Limerence: VERY HARD    │
```

```
  R1  Baumrind 1966, 1991 — Parenting styles: Authoritative/Authoritarian/
      Permissive/Neglectful. Authoritative = best outcomes.

      structure = optimal. Autonomy ≠ absence of structure.
  R3  Gottman & DeClaire 2001 — Bids for connection. Turning toward vs
      turning away. Bid-response ratio predicts relationship outcomes.
  R4  Christensen & Heavey 1990 — Demand-Withdraw interaction pattern.
      Escalation cycle when bids become demands.
  R5  Fonagy 2002 — Mentalization: capacity to understand behavior in
      terms of mental states (self AND other). Trainable.
  R6  Bateman & Fonagy 2006 — Mentalization-Based Therapy (MBT).
      Clinical evidence for mentalization training effectiveness.
  R7  Trapnell & Campbell 1999 — Self-Reflection vs Self-Rumination.
      2 dispositions previously confounded. Opposite correlates.
  R8  Joireman, Parrott & Hammersla 2002 — Self-reflection →
      positive empathy correlation. Self-rumination → negative.
  R9  Coan & Sbarra 2015 — Social Baseline Theory. Brain outsources
      emotion regulation to trusted others. Neural evidence.
  R10 Mikolajczak et al. 2019 — Parental burnout: depleted → overcontrol
      OR emotional distance. Cross-cultural replication.
  R11 Lopes, Salovey & Straus 2003 — Emotion regulation ↔ social
      interactions. Bidirectional, controlling personality+intelligence.
  R12 Koudenburg et al. 2024 — Social interaction → self-concept clarity.
      "Sense of me" forms through "sense of we."
  R13 McNulty et al. 2013 — Implicit vs explicit partner attitudes.
      PFC rationalization of actual relationship quality.
  R14 Fukushima et al. 2011 — Interoceptive accuracy → empathic
      accuracy correlation. Body-readout skill trainable.
  R15 Gendlin 1978 — Focusing. Body-felt sense awareness technique.
  R16 Trivers 1972 — Parental investment theory. Highest investment →
      highest bias toward offspring.
  R17 Feldman 2012 — Bio-behavioral synchrony. Oxytocin × parent-child.
  R18 Strathearn et al. 2009 — Maternal brain reward: own infant face.
  R19 Fraley & Shaver 1998 — Avoidant suppress but physiology elevated.
      Attachment change possible but slow.
  R20 Zeki & Romaya 2008 — Hate vs Love circuits. Shared: putamen+insula.

  R21 Maccoby 1992 — Power asymmetry in parent-child relationships.

  R23 Segrin et al. 2013 — Parental anxiety → overparenting, not child need.
```
