| Term | Definition |
|---|---|

### PFC Operations

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| **HOLD** (= PFC Amplify) | Amplify new/weak pattern. CAN compile → sustainable. Cost: ①. Region: dlPFC, FEF. |
| **SUPPRESS** (= PFC Inhibit) | Block already-compiled pattern. CANNOT compile "not" → unsustainable. Cost: ②. Region: rIFG, vlPFC. |

### PFC Costs

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| **① PFC draft cost** | Processing load from HOLD. f(chain_length × novelty). DECREASES as it compiles → ≈ 0. |
| **② Suppress cost** | Efference mismatch from SUPPRESS. f(intensity × duration). DOES NOT decrease. = "Not being me." |
| **③ Uncertainty cost** | Multiple options, none compiled → hold open → cortisol. f(options × time × stakes). |
| **PFC budget** | Total PFC capacity. FINITE. SHARED. Limited by: serial bottleneck, catecholamine state, allocation competition, cortisol interference. |
