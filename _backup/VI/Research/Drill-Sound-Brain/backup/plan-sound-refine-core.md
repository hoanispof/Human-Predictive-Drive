      Musical anhedonia: 3-5% population.
      Auditory cortex: NORMAL (hear music fine).
      NAcc: NORMAL (food/money/sex pleasure fine).

      = Domain-specific DISCONNECT, not global deficit.

    TẠI SAO ĐÂY LÀ STRONGEST TEST CASE:
      Proof-by-contradiction cho multi-step model:
      NẾU PE = reward (single step):
        Auditory cortex works → PE fires → PHẢI sướng
        → Anhedonia KHÔNG THỂ xảy ra
      MULTI-STEP MODEL:
        PE fire (Steps 1-3: OK) + pathway broken (Step 4: FAIL) → no reward
        → Anhedonia CÓ THỂ xảy ra. CONFIRMED.

    Compare với §3.3 Parkinson's:
      Parkinson = GLOBAL dopamine loss → wanting impaired, liking partly preserved
      Musical anhedonia = DOMAIN-SPECIFIC disconnect → PE ok, reward KHÔNG
      = 2 clinical cases, 2 kiểu disruption, CÙNG confirm multi-step

    THÊM: Motor entrainment CAN remain intact in musical anhedonia
      → Entrainment pathway (auditory → motor) ≠ reward pathway (auditory → NAcc)
      → = 2 INDEPENDENT pathways from auditory processing
      → Entrainment ≠ pleasure (rhythm WITHOUT reward)

    🟢 Hou et al. 2024 (PsyCh Journal): analytical KNOWLEDGE increases liking
