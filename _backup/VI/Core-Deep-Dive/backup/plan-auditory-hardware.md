### §1 — 6 Unique Hardware Properties (~180-220 dòng)

```
PLACEMENT: Core section. Mỗi property ~ 30-35 dòng.

═══ PROPERTY 1: ALWAYS-ON (Sentinel Function) ═══

  CLAIM: Auditory = DUY NHẤT experience modality always-on ở mức hardware.
    Con người KHÔNG CÓ cơ chế đóng tai tự nhiên (không có "eyelid" cho tai).
    Trong giấc ngủ: auditory brainstem response VẪN hoạt động.
    → Sound monitoring = 24/7, kể cả khi ý thức tắt.

  EVIDENCE TO USE:
    🟢 Anatomy: không có cơ tương đương cơ vòng mắt (orbicularis oculi) cho tai.
    🟢 Auditory brainstem response (ABR): persists during sleep (clinical neuroscience).
    🟢 Noise wakes sleepers: auditory → thalamus → arousal network VẪN active.
    🟡 "Sentinel function" framing = framework synthesis.

  SO SÁNH MODALITY:
    Visual: mắt nhắm → OFF. Cần ánh sáng + hướng nhìn → NOT always-on.
    Somatic: body cảm nhận luôn → PARTIAL always-on, NHƯNG internal-facing.
    Auditory: external-facing + always-on + 360° → UNIQUE combination.

  FRAMEWORK MAPPING:
    → Always-on = WHY sound là first-alert cho Threat (tiger roar lúc ngủ).
    → Always-on = WHY background sound → Background-Pattern formation liên tục.
    → = "Microphone luôn bật" → mọi thứ đi qua auditory channel TRƯỚC KHI PFC aware.


═══ PROPERTY 2: CROSS-BARRIER (Xuyên Vật Cản) ═══

  CLAIM: Sound xuyên vật cản mà visual KHÔNG thể.
    Qua tường, quanh góc, trong bóng tối, qua vegetation.
    → Mở rộng perimeter cảnh giác VƯỢT line-of-sight.

  EVIDENCE TO USE:
    🟢 Physics: sóng âm diffraction + transmission qua vật rắn.
    🟢 Evolutionary biology: nocturnal primates rely on auditory (established).
    🟢 Ví dụ concrete: nghe bước chân bên kia tường → biết có người
       MÀ KHÔNG cần nhìn thấy.

  SO SÁNH MODALITY:
    Visual: cần line-of-sight + photon → bị chặn bởi tường, bóng tối.
    Somatic: detect vibration qua mặt đất (partial cross-barrier), nhưng range ngắn.
    Olfactory: cross-barrier (mùi bay qua), nhưng CHẬM + diffuse + low precision.
    Auditory: cross-barrier + FAST + directional (binaural localization) → UNIQUE combo.

  FRAMEWORK MAPPING:
    → Cross-barrier = WHY auditory cho Prediction TRƯỚC visual confirmation.
    → = WHY tiếng bước chân → spatial prediction mà không cần mắt.
    → = WHY threat detection works in darkness/shelter.


═══ PROPERTY 3: PRE-BIRTH COMPILATION (Duy Nhất Trước Sinh) ═══

  CLAIM: Auditory = DUY NHẤT modality bắt đầu compile chunks TRƯỚC KHI SINH.
    Cochlea functional từ ~26 tuần thai.
    Fetus NGHE: giọng mẹ, nhịp tim, environmental sounds.
    → Trẻ sinh ra ĐÃ CÓ auditory chunk library.

  EVIDENCE TO USE:
    🟢 DeCasper & Fifer 1980: newborn (2-3 ngày) prefer giọng mẹ hơn stranger.
    🟢 DeCasper & Spence 1986: newborn prefer TRUYỆN mà mẹ đọc lúc mang thai.
    🟢 Moon, Cooper & Fifer 1993: newborn discriminate native vs foreign language.
    🟢 Cochlear development: functional ~26 weeks (established developmental biology).

  SO SÁNH MODALITY:
    Visual: ~0 prenatal (tử cung tối). Starts at birth.
    Motor: reflex only prenatal. Voluntary from birth.
    Somatic: partial prenatal (touch, temperature). Nhưng KHÔNG compile complex patterns.
    Auditory: compile COMPLEX patterns prenatal (voice, rhythm, language) → UNIQUE.

  FRAMEWORK MAPPING:
    → Pre-birth = chunks FIRST ever compiled = auditory.
    → = Foundational substrate: attachment chunks có auditory component TỪ ĐẦU.
    → = WHY giọng mẹ = powerful emotional trigger (earliest compiled chunks).
    → Chi tiết: 04-Auditory-System-Arc.md (911L developmental timeline).


═══ PROPERTY 4: TEMPORAL > SPATIAL ENCODING ═══

  CLAIM: Auditory encode TIME patterns, Visual encode SPACE patterns.
    → 2 modalities = complementary prediction system.
    → Speech = temporal sequence → auditory.
    → Navigation = spatial structure → visual.

  EVIDENCE TO USE:
    🟢 Auditory temporal resolution: ~2-3ms (gap detection). 
    🟢 Visual temporal resolution: ~30-50ms (flicker fusion).
    🟢 Visual spatial resolution: foveal acuity ~1 arcminute.
    🟢 Auditory spatial resolution: ~1-2° azimuth (binaural, much coarser than visual).
    🟢 Established psychophysics: modality appropriateness hypothesis (Welch & Warren 1980).

  SO SÁNH MODALITY:
    Visual: SPATIAL dominant (shape, color, position, distance).
    Auditory: TEMPORAL dominant (rhythm, sequence, tempo, prosody).
    Motor: temporal (action sequence), nhưng OUTPUT channel, không INPUT.
    → Auditory + Visual = temporal + spatial = COMPLETE prediction coverage.

  FRAMEWORK MAPPING:
    → Temporal encoding = WHY language = auditory (speech = temporal sequence).
    → Temporal encoding = WHY music = auditory (rhythm, melody = temporal patterns).
    → = WHY chunks compiled qua auditory TEND TO BE sequential/temporal.
    → Complements visual spatial chunks → multi-modal = deeper chunks.


═══ PROPERTY 5: DISPROPORTIONATE ACTIVATION EFFICIENCY ═══

  CLAIM: 1 dòng melody → kích hoạt ~5-6 hệ thống não ĐỒNG THỜI.
    Auditory cortex + motor (entrainment) + emotion (amygdala/insula)
    + prediction (PFC/IFG) + memory (hippocampus) + reward (NAcc/VTA).
    → KHÔNG modality nào khác kích hoạt NHIỀU hệ thống đến vậy từ 1 input line.

  EVIDENCE TO USE:
    🟢 Koelsch 2014 (Brain basis of music perception — review):
      Music activates: auditory, motor, limbic, frontal, parietal, reward.
    🟢 Grahn & Brett 2007: motor areas active during PASSIVE listening.
    🟢 Salimpoor 2011: caudate + NAcc (reward) during music.
    🟢 Blood & Zatorre 2001: music → amygdala, insula, orbitofrontal.
    🟡 "Activation efficiency" as COMPARATIVE metric = framework concept.
      = Individual activations 🟢, comparison framing = 🟡.

  SO SÁNH MODALITY:
    Visual: kích hoạt visual cortex (multiple areas V1-V5) + some emotion.
      Nhưng 1 hình ảnh tĩnh KHÔNG tự động activate motor + reward.
    Somatic: kích hoạt insula + somatosensory + emotion. Ít activate motor/reward.
    Auditory: DUY NHẤT automatic motor (Grahn 2007) + reward + prediction + emotion
      TỪ PASSIVE INPUT.

  FRAMEWORK MAPPING:
    → Activation efficiency = WHY music = powerful emotional tool.
    → = WHY 1 bài hát thay đổi mood nhanh hơn 1 bức ảnh.
    → = WHY music therapy effective across nhiều conditions.
    → Chi tiết: Drill-Sound-Brain/08 §2 (melody multi-system activator).

  ⚠️ CAVEAT: "~5-6 systems" = ESTIMATE dựa trên review papers.
    Exact number phụ thuộc cách count systems.
    Claim = "disproportionately many" chứ KHÔNG phải "exactly 5.7."


═══ PROPERTY 6: DUAL TONIC/CYCLIC FUNCTION ═══

  CLAIM: Auditory hoạt động ở CẢ 2 chế độ ĐỒNG THỜI.
    Tonic: background awareness luôn bật (sentinel, ambient monitoring).
    Cyclic: foreground attention peaks (alerting sounds, speech, music peaks).
    → Visual chủ yếu Cyclic (cần saccade, cần hướng nhìn).
    → Auditory = cả hai CÙNG LÚC.

  EVIDENCE TO USE:
    🟢 Auditory cortex responds to novel sounds even during attention elsewhere
      (mismatch negativity — Näätänen 1995, established ERP).
    🟢 Cocktail party effect: background processing + foreground attention
      (Cherry 1953 — classic).
    🟡 "Tonic/Cyclic" labeling = framework terms (Gap-Body-Need.md §3).

  SO SÁNH MODALITY:
    Visual: primarily Cyclic (foveal attention shifts, saccades).
      Background visual processing exists (peripheral vision) nhưng YẾU hơn.
    Somatic: partial Tonic (body always sensing). Nhưng CHỦ YẾU Tonic, ít Cyclic.
    Auditory: STRONG at BOTH → cocktail party = simultaneous Tonic + Cyclic.

  FRAMEWORK MAPPING:
    → Dual function = WHY background music → Background-Pattern formation (Tonic)
       WHILE foreground conversation → Chunk formation (Cyclic).
    → = WHY sound environment affects processing EVEN khi không chú ý.
    → Chi tiết: Drill-Sound-Brain/05 §7 (sound oscillates Tonic ↔ Cyclic).
```
