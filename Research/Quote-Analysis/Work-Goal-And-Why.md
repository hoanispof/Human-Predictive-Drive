```
  🟢 Intrinsic vs extrinsic motivation: Deci & Ryan (Self-Determination Theory) 2000
  🟢 Approach vs avoidance motivation: Elliot & Church 1997
  🟡 Positive/Negative Trust mapping: framework synthesis
```

## §5 — FAILURE MODES: KHI NÀO QUOTE SAI HOẶC INCOMPLETE

### §5.1 — Goal Rõ + Why Rõ Nhưng KHÔNG Effective

```
⭐ 5 FAILURE MODES:

  FAILURE 1 — VALUE CONFLICT (gap direction mismatch):
    Goal: "Ship fast" + Why: "Market window closing"
    Kỹ sư value quality → body REJECT → anti-match
    → Tệ hơn không giải thích why
    → CAUSE: why conflict với personal Anchor-Schema

  FAILURE 2 — WHY QUÁ ABSTRACT (chain quá dài):
    Goal: "Build AI" + Why: "Change the world"
    → Schema chain: task → team → company → industry → world
    → Valence propagation yếu dần qua 5 links → tới task ≈ 0
    → CAUSE: each link in chain = propagation LOSS

  FAILURE 3 — LEADER NÓI WHY NHƯNG BODY NHÂN VIÊN KHÔNG ACCEPT:
    Goal rõ + Why explained + nhưng leader KHÔNG TRUSTED
    → External inject REJECTED → why stays PFC-only
    → CAUSE: Trust violation history → anchor candidate rejected
    → Anchor-Schema §2: trust break → rebuild chậm hơn build lần đầu

  FAILURE 4 — NEGATIVE TRUST WHY (threat-based):
    Goal: "Hit target" + Why: "Hoặc bị layoff"
    → Functional SHORT-TERM (negative trust = strong driver)
    → UNSUSTAINABLE: relief reward diminishing, cortisol chronic
    → CAUSE: avoidance tag compile → "giỏi nhưng GHÉT"

  FAILURE 5 — ANCHOR TYPE MISMATCH (5 types):
    Meaning.md v2.2: 5 types life-level Anchor-Schema:
      GOAL type: "Xây dựng thứ vĩ đại"
      STATE type: "An lạc trong tâm hồn"
      IDENTITY type: "Tôi là người tốt"
      FAITH type: "Chúa có kế hoạch"
      ROLE type: "Công việc tôi quan trọng"

    Musk's why typically GOAL-type: "build Mars colony"
    → GOAL-type người → fire MẠNH
    → STATE-type người → "hay nhưng... rồi sao?" → KHÔNG fire
    → Cùng why, KHÁC anchor type → KHÁC response hoàn toàn
    → CAUSE: each person's life-level anchor type = DIFFERENT
```

## §6 — CALIBRATION: CÁCH APPLY KHÔNG CỨNG NHẮC

```
⭐⭐ 6 NGUYÊN TẮC CALIBRATION:

  ① GOAL PHẢI VIVID, KHÔNG CHỈ CLEAR:
    "Clear" (PFC hiểu) ≠ "Vivid" (body pre-feel).
    Revenue target = clear nhưng NOT vivid.
    "Users smile when they open our product" = clear AND vivid.
    Calibrate: goal phải ở Imagine-Final Gradient ≥ Level 3.

  ② WHY PHẢI GENUINE, KHÔNG CHỈ LOGICAL:
    Logical why = PFC-only → stays PFC → KHÔNG propagate.
    Genuine why = body resonance → compile → Anchor-Schema.
    Test: nếu nói "why" mà CHÍNH MÌNH không feel → người khác cũng sẽ không.
    Calibrate: why phải consistent với leader's OWN Anchor-Schema.

  ③ MATCH ANCHOR TYPE CỦA NGƯỜI NGHE:
    GOAL-type people → respond to achievement why.
    STATE-type people → respond to experience/process why.
    ROLE-type people → respond to importance/contribution why.
    IDENTITY-type people → respond to character/values why.
    Calibrate: 1 why KHÔNG fit all → per-person hoặc multi-angle.

  ④ POSITIVE TRUST, KHÔNG NEGATIVE:
    "Because we'll build something amazing" (positive) >
    "Because we'll die if we don't" (negative).
    Cả 2 đều motivate. Chỉ 1 sustainable.
    Calibrate: check body state — approach or avoidance?

  ⑤ REPEAT ĐỦ ĐỂ COMPILE:
    Goal + why giải thích 1 lần = PFC-only = temporary.
    Cần REPEAT → compile vào Background-Pattern → automatic.
    = Tại sao all-hands, rituals, storytelling WORK:
      repetition → Nguồn ④ External Inject → compile deep.
    Calibrate: goal + why phải embedded trong culture,
      không chỉ trong 1 email.

  ⑥ ALLOW BODY ACCEPTANCE TIME:
    Explained ≠ Accepted. Acceptance = process, not instant.
    Anchor-Schema.md §3 Nguồn ①: Imagine-Final → Anchor-Schema
      = QUÁ TRÌNH DÀI (tháng-năm, qua BUILD → SAVE → BACKGROUND → REFINE).
    Calibrate: không expect immediate effect.
      Domain feedback ✓ reinforces → trust builds gradually.
      Early disillusionment ≠ wrong why — trust chưa đủ.
```

## §7 — HONEST ASSESSMENT

```
⭐ FRAMEWORK GIẢI THÍCH ĐƯỢC GÌ:

  ✅ TẠI SAO goal → better work (Gap Direction + Simulation Engine target)
  ✅ TẠI SAO why → better work (Anchor-Schema + Valence Propagation)
  ✅ TẠI SAO "look forward" quan trọng (opioid preview = evidence of body acceptance)
  ✅ TẠI SAO "enjoy" quan trọng (genuine compiled + by-product match)
  ✅ KHI NÀO nó FAIL (5 failure modes — value conflict, abstract, trust, negative, type mismatch)
  ✅ CÁCH calibrate (6 principles)
  ✅ BƯỚC ẨN (body acceptance) mà quote skip
  ✅ Individual differences (anchor type, compiled history, gap direction per-person)

  ⚠️ CAVEAT — FRAMEWORK LIMITATIONS:

  🟡 "Valence propagation qua schema chain" = framework synthesis.
     Mechanism cụ thể (neurotransmitter nào, synapse nào truyền valence
     DỌC chain) chưa có direct neural evidence.

  🟡 "Compiled Quality Dimension (genuine/schema/threat)" = framework distinction.
     Consistent với dual-process + somatic marker research
     nhưng 3-way split chưa có specific neuroscience study.

  🟡 Anchor-Schema "body acceptance" = framework concept mới.
     Derived từ established components (goal commitment, somatic marker,
     implicit learning) nhưng integrated model chưa validated riêng.

  🟢 ESTABLISHED SUPPORT:
     Goal-setting theory (Locke & Latham 1990) — specific+difficult goals → performance
     Self-Determination Theory (Deci & Ryan 2000) — autonomy+competence+relatedness
     Somatic Marker Hypothesis (Damasio 1994) — body signals guide decision
     Prediction error → reward (Schultz 1997) — dopamine + prediction match
     Flow theory (Csikszentmihalyi 1990) — challenge/skill match → enjoyment
     Transformational leadership (Bass 1985) — vision → follower motivation

  🔴 HYPOTHESIS:
     "Mỗi link trong schema chain = propagation loss" chưa measurable.
     "Anchor type mismatch = why fails" chưa có controlled study.

  FRAMEWORK vs RE-DESCRIPTION:

  Quote: "Goal + Why → Better Work" (WHAT happens).
  Framework: Gap Direction + Schema Chain + Body Acceptance + Compiled Quality
    = WHY it happens + WHEN it fails + HOW to calibrate (MECHANISM).
  = THÊM predictive power: predict 5 failure modes mà quote không mention.
  = Nếu CHỈ re-describe → không predict failures.
  = Predict failures = evidence cho explanation, không chỉ description.
```
