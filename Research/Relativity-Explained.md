## §3 — Level 1: Pythagoras

**Intuition:**
You stand at one corner of a square courtyard. You want to reach the opposite corner.
Walk along two sides: a meters to the right, b meters up.
Or take the diagonal: c meters straight across.

```
        b
   ┌─────────┐
   │         /│
   │       /  │
   │     / c  │ a
   │   /      │
   │ /        │
   └──────────┘
```

**Real Math:**
```
c² = a² + b²
c = √(a² + b²)
```

**Examples:**
- a = 3, b = 4 → c = √(9 + 16) = √25 = 5
- a = 5, b = 12 → c = √(25 + 144) = √169 = 13

**Extended to 3D:**
```
d² = x² + y² + z²
```
Distance in 3D space. Same idea, one more dimension.

**Why This Matters:**
Pythagoras says: distance is INVARIANT — rotate your coordinate axes (spin the map),
and the real distance between two points DOES NOT CHANGE. x and y values shift, but x² + y² stays the same.

Relativity will use EXACTLY this idea — but add time to it.

## §4 — Level 2: The Speed of Light Is Constant

### §4.1 — A Natural Question

You stand still and throw a ball at 20 km/h. Fine.
You stand on a car moving at 80 km/h and throw the ball in the same direction → the ball travels at 100 km/h.
Speeds ADD. Normal intuition.

So if you are aboard a spaceship traveling at 200,000 km/s
and switch on a flashlight (light travels at 300,000 km/s) → should light travel at 500,000 km/s?

**NO.** Light still travels at exactly 300,000 km/s.
Whether you move toward it, away from it, or stand still — light is always 300,000 km/s.

### §4.2 — Evidence: Michelson-Morley (1887)

At the time, everyone believed light needed an "ether" to propagate
(the way water waves need water, sound waves need air).
Earth orbits the Sun at ~30 km/s → if ether existed,
light traveling in Earth's direction of motion should be faster or slower than light traveling perpendicular to it.

Michelson and Morley built an extraordinarily precise measuring apparatus.
Result: **NO difference.** Zero. Repeated. Still zero.

This became the most famous "failed" experiment in the history of science
— "failed" because it did not find what it was looking for,
but CORRECT because what it was looking for does not exist.

### §4.3 — The Speed of Light

```
c = 299,792,458 m/s (exact — this IS the definition of the meter since 1983)
≈ 300,000 km/s
≈ 1,080,000,000 km/h (1 billion km/h)
```

Earth to Moon: ~1.3 seconds.
Earth to Sun: ~8.3 minutes.
Earth to the nearest star (Proxima Centauri): ~4.2 YEARS.

### §4.4 — Einstein's 2 Postulates (1905)

All of Special Relativity rests on 2 postulates:

```
① The laws of physics are the SAME in all inertial frames
   (= frames moving at constant velocity, not accelerating)

② The speed of light in vacuum = c for ALL observers,
   regardless of how the light source or observer is moving
```

Postulate ① makes sense: sit in a train moving at constant speed, close the windows,
and there is no way to know whether the train is moving or stationary.
Every experiment gives the same result.

Postulate ② is SHOCKING. It contradicts the intuition that "speeds add."
But experiments confirm: ② is CORRECT.

From these 2 postulates → EVERYTHING else in Special Relativity follows naturally.

## §5 — Level 3: The Spacetime Interval

### §5.1 — The Problem

Pythagoras gives distance in space: d² = x² + y² + z²

But if light does not obey "speed addition" → pure spatial distance
is NOT sufficient to describe the universe. Space and time must be combined.

### §5.2 — Spacetime Interval

Minkowski (1908) realized: Einstein's math becomes MORE NATURAL when written as:

```
s² = -(ct)² + x² + y² + z²
```

Compared:
```
Pythagoras (space):       d² = x² + y² + z²         (all plus signs)
Minkowski (spacetime):    s² = -(ct)² + x² + y² + z² (time carries a MINUS)
```

**Only one sign changes.** Add a time axis (ct) with the OPPOSITE sign.

### §5.3 — Why the Minus Sign?

The minus sign means space and time **compete**.
With a fixed total budget, more on one side means less on the other.

**Analogy — the car:**

Imagine you are a car that always travels at exactly 100 km/h — no faster,
no slower, cannot stop.

You drive on a field with two directions:
- North = space
- East = time

```
  East (time)
    ↑
    |  ↗ car (always 100 km/h, only turning)
    | /
    |/
    └──────→ North (space)
```

- Car points East (stationary): 100% toward time → time flows at maximum rate
- Car turns partly North (moving): East component decreases → time SLOWS DOWN
- Car points fully North (moving at c): 0% East → time STOPS

That is the entirety of time dilation. One car turning its direction.

### §5.4 — Invariance

Just like Pythagoras: x² + y² does not change when you rotate axes.
Spacetime: -(ct)² + x² + y² + z² does not change when you switch reference frames.

Two different observers may measure different t and different x,
but s² is always THE SAME. That is "invariance" — the thing nobody can dispute.

## §6 — Level 4: The Lorentz Factor γ — The Magic Number

### §6.1 — Derivation from the Light Clock

This is the SIMPLEST way to derive the γ formula, requiring only Pythagoras.

**Light clock:** imagine a clock that works by bouncing
a single light beam up and down between two mirrors separated by distance L.
Each bounce = one "tick."

**Case 1 — Clock at rest:**

```
  ═══════  ← top mirror
     ↑
     | L      light travels straight up
     |        time for one tick: t₀ = L/c
     ↓
  ═══════  ← bottom mirror
```

**Case 2 — Clock moving to the right at speed v:**

An outside observer sees the light travel DIAGONALLY (because the mirrors are moving):

```
  ═══════                    ═══════
     ↑ \                    / ↑
     |   \     light      /   |
   L |     \  diagonal  /     | L
     |       \         /       |
     ↓         \     /         ↓
  ═══════        ↘ ↙        ═══════
                  
  ←───── vt' ─────→
  (mirror has moved during time t')
```

The light travels diagonally = a longer path. But the speed of light DOES NOT CHANGE (= c).
Longer path + same speed → MORE TIME NEEDED → t' > t₀.

**Apply Pythagoras to the right triangle:**

```
(ct')² = L² + (vt')²

Since L = ct₀, substitute:

(ct')² = (ct₀)² + (vt')²

c²t'² - v²t'² = c²t₀²

t'²(c² - v²) = c²t₀²

t'² = c²t₀² / (c² - v²) = t₀² / (1 - v²/c²)

t' = t₀ / √(1 - v²/c²)
```

### §6.2 — The Lorentz Factor γ

Name that denominator:

```
┌─────────────────────────────┐
│                             │
│   γ = 1 / √(1 - v²/c²)    │
│                             │
│   t' = γ × t₀              │
│                             │
└─────────────────────────────┘
```

γ (gamma) = the Lorentz factor. A single number. It governs EVERYTHING in Special Relativity.

### §6.3 — γ Table

| Speed v | v/c | v²/c² | 1 - v²/c² | γ = 1/√(...) | What it means |
|---------|-----|-------|-----------|--------------|---------------|
| Walking 5 km/h | 0.0000005% | ~0 | ~1 | 1.000000000... | No perceptible difference |
| Car 100 km/h | 0.00001% | ~0 | ~1 | 1.000000000... | No perceptible difference |
| Airplane 900 km/h | 0.00008% | ~0 | ~1 | 1.0000000000004 | ~ns deviation per hour |
| Earth orbiting the Sun | 0.01% | 10⁻⁸ | ~1 | 1.000000005 | |
| 10% c | 10% | 0.01 | 0.99 | 1.005 | Almost unnoticeable |
| 50% c | 50% | 0.25 | 0.75 | 1.155 | 1 year → 1.15 years |
| 87% c | 87% | 0.75 | 0.25 | 2.000 | 1 year → 2 years |
| 90% c | 90% | 0.81 | 0.19 | 2.294 | 1 year → 2.3 years |
| 95% c | 95% | 0.9025 | 0.0975 | 3.203 | 1 year → 3.2 years |
| 99% c | 99% | 0.9801 | 0.0199 | 7.089 | 1 year → 7 years |

**Pattern:** At low speeds, γ ≈ 1 — completely unnoticeable.

## §7 — Level 5: Time Dilation

### §7.1 — Formula

```
t' = γ × t₀

t' = time measured by someone at rest (observing from outside)
t₀ = time measured by the moving person (their own clock)
γ = 1/√(1 - v²/c²)
```

The moving person ALWAYS measures SHORTER time.
The clock is not wrong — time ACTUALLY passes slower for the moving person.

### §7.2 — Verification 1: GPS

GPS satellites orbit at ~14,000 km/h, at ~20,200 km altitude.

**Special Relativity (speed):**
v ≈ 3.87 km/s → γ ≈ 1.0000000000836
→ Satellite clock runs SLOW by ~7 μs/day (microsecond = one millionth of a second)

**General Relativity (weaker gravity at altitude):**
→ Satellite clock runs FAST by ~45 μs/day
(weaker gravity → less curved spacetime → time runs faster)

**Net effect:** 45 - 7 = +38 μs/day (satellite clock runs FASTER than ground)

38 microseconds sounds tiny? Light travels 11.4 km in 38 μs.
→ No relativistic correction → GPS drifts **11 km per day**, compounding.

GPS corrects for BOTH effects (SR + GR). If Einstein were wrong → GPS would not work.
You use GPS every day = you are USING relativity every day without knowing it.

### §7.3 — Verification 2: Muons

A muon is a subatomic particle created when cosmic rays strike the atmosphere (~15 km above ground).
Muon lifetime: 2.2 μs (microseconds).
Muon speed: ~0.998c.

**Without relativity:**
Distance = v × t = 0.998c × 2.2 μs ≈ 660 m
→ Muon only travels 660 m → NEVER reaches the ground (15,000 m needed)

**With relativity:**
γ at 0.998c ≈ 15.8
Time in the ground frame: 15.8 × 2.2 μs ≈ 34.8 μs
Distance = 0.998c × 34.8 μs ≈ 10,400 m
→ Enough to reach the ground (plus some muons originating at lower altitudes)

**Actual result:** Correct. Ground-based detectors catch the muons.
Approximately 10,000 muons pass through your body every second — living evidence
you cannot feel, but can count.

### §7.4 — Verification 3: Hafele-Keating (1971)

Four cesium atomic clocks were carried aboard commercial airplanes.
Two circumnavigations of the Earth: one eastward, one westward.
Results compared against clocks on the ground.

**Prediction:** ~275 ns (nanosecond) difference combining SR + GR.
**Measured result:** consistent within experimental error.

No spaceship needed. A commercial airliner + precise clocks = SUFFICIENT.

### §7.5 — Twin Paradox

Twin brothers A and B. A stays home; B travels by spaceship at 90% c for 10 years (by B's clock).

γ at 90% c = 2.294.
A measures: B is away for 2.294 × 10 = 22.94 years.

B returns: B aged 10 years, A aged 23 years. B is 13 years YOUNGER.

**This is not a true paradox** — the "paradox" arises only because intuition says
"why not say A moves relative to B?" Answer: because B ACCELERATES when turning around —
B leaves the inertial frame. The asymmetry comes from the acceleration.

## §8 — Level 6: Length Contraction

### §8.1 — Formula

```
L' = L₀ / γ

L₀ = "rest length" (measured by someone traveling with the object)
L' = length measured by someone who sees the object in motion
γ = 1/√(1 - v²/c²)
```

A moving object CONTRACTS along its direction of motion.
Only the direction of motion — transverse dimensions are unchanged.

### §8.2 — Example

A 1-meter iron rod flying past you at 87% c (γ = 2):

```
  At rest:   |←──── 1.00 m ────→|

  At 87% c:  |←── 0.50 m ──→|        (contracted to half)

  At 99% c:  |← 0.14 m →|            (contracted to 14 cm)
```

Not an optical illusion. If you measure SIMULTANEOUSLY (using a proper simultaneity convention),
the rod IS shorter. But someone traveling with the rod still measures 1 meter.

### §8.3 — Muon from a Different Frame

In §7.3, we explained how muons reach the ground via time dilation (the muon's lifetime extends).
There is another way — FROM THE MUON'S POINT OF VIEW:

The muon "sees" the atmosphere CONTRACT:
15 km / γ = 15 km / 15.8 ≈ 950 m
→ In the muon's frame, the atmosphere is only 950 m thick → the muon crosses it in 2.2 μs.

Two different explanations, SAME RESULT. That is the internal consistency of relativity.

## §9 — Level 7: E = mc²

### §9.1 — The Full Formula

```
E = γmc²
```

Total energy = γ × rest mass × c².

When at rest (v = 0, γ = 1):

```
┌───────────────┐
│               │
│   E₀ = mc²   │
│               │
└───────────────┘
```

The most famous equation in physics. It says:
**Mass and energy are two forms of the same thing.**
Mass CAN be converted to energy, and vice versa.

### §9.2 — How Large Is c²?

```
c² = (3 × 10⁸)² = 9 × 10¹⁶ m²/s²
```

1 kg of matter contains: E = 1 × 9 × 10¹⁶ = 9 × 10¹⁶ Joules
= 25 billion kWh
= enough to supply electricity for ~2.8 million households for one year.

1 kilogram. All that energy. Just convert mass → energy COMPLETELY.

### §9.3 — Verification: Nuclear Energy

The Hiroshima atomic bomb converted ~0.7 grams of matter → energy.
0.7 grams. Not 0.7 kilograms. 0.7 GRAMS. = the destructive power of 15,000 tons of TNT.

Nuclear power plants: uranium fission converts ~0.1% of mass → energy.
0.1% — enough to power a city.

The Sun: hydrogen → helium (fusion), ~0.7% of mass → energy.
The Sun burns ~4.26 million tons of matter EVERY SECOND — has been burning for 4.6 billion years → ~5 billion years remain.

### §9.4 — Verification: PET Scan

PET (Positron Emission Tomography) — used to scan brains and tumors:
A positron (antimatter electron) meets an electron → they ANNIHILATE →
entire mass → 2 gamma photons flying in opposite directions.
The PET scanner detects both photons → triangulates position.

If you or someone you know has had a PET scan = you have witnessed E = mc² directly.

### §9.5 — Extended Formula: Energy + Momentum

More complete:

```
E² = (pc)² + (mc²)²
```

Where p = momentum.

If mass = 0 (photon):
```
E = pc     (photon energy = pure momentum)
```

If at rest (p = 0):
```
E = mc²    (reduces back to the familiar E = mc²)
```

Photons have no mass but carry energy and momentum.
That is why light can push objects (radiation pressure, solar sail).

## §10 — Level 8: The Equivalence Principle

### §10.1 — Einstein's Thought Experiment

Einstein asked: if you are inside an elevator WITH NO WINDOWS:

**Case A:** The elevator is stationary on Earth.
You stand on the floor, feeling "heavy." Drop a ball → it falls to the floor.

**Case B:** The elevator is in outer space (far from all celestial bodies),
but a ROCKET pushes it upward with acceleration g (= 9.8 m/s²).
You stand on the floor, feeling "heavy." Drop a ball → it falls to the floor.

```
  Case A:                  Case B:
  ┌──────────┐              ┌──────────┐  ↑ rocket pushing
  │    🧍    │              │    🧍    │  ↑ acceleration g
  │    ⚽↓   │              │    ⚽↓   │  ↑
  │          │              │          │
  └──────────┘              └──────────┘
     gravity                 acceleration

  FROM INSIDE: IMPOSSIBLE TO DISTINGUISH A FROM B
```

**The Equivalence Principle:**
```
Gravity = Acceleration (locally)
The effects of a gravitational field ≡ The effects of acceleration
```

If two things CANNOT BE DISTINGUISHED → they ARE THE SAME THING.

### §10.2 — Consequence: Light Bends

Inside an accelerating elevator:
Fire a horizontal light beam → while light travels from left to right,
the elevator floor rises → the beam APPEARS TO CURVE DOWNWARD.

If gravity = acceleration → light must also BEND in a gravitational field.

**Verification: 1919 Solar Eclipse (Eddington)**
During the eclipse, stars BEHIND the Sun became visible (normally blocked).
Starlight bent around the Sun → stars appeared "displaced."
Eddington measured: exactly as Einstein predicted. Front-page news worldwide.

### §10.3 — Consequence: Gravitational Time Dilation

Inside an accelerating elevator: a clock at the floor (near the rocket) runs SLOWER than
a clock at the ceiling (far from the rocket) — due to the Doppler effect.

If gravity = acceleration → clocks near the ground run SLOWER than
clocks at altitude. Stronger gravity → slower time.

**Verification:** Pound-Rebka (1959) — measured redshift between the ground floor and the 22nd floor
of a Harvard building. Difference ~2.5 × 10⁻¹⁵. Measured. Correct.

This is the +45 μs/day component in GPS (§7.2).

## §11 — Level 9: Curved Spacetime

### §11.1 — From "Attractive Force" to "Curvature"

Newton (1687): gravity = attractive force between two bodies, F = GMm/r².
Works BRILLIANTLY for almost everything. But:
- Does not explain WHY gravity exists
- Wrong when gravity is strong (near a black hole)
- Wrong when objects move fast (near c)
- Incompatible with "c = constant" (Newton's gravity acts instantaneously)

Einstein (1915): THERE IS NO ATTRACTIVE FORCE. Mass/energy CURVES spacetime.
Objects follow the straightest possible path ON THE CURVED SURFACE → from a distance: they look "attracted."

### §11.2 — Geodesic — the Straight Line on a Curved Surface

On a flat plane: a straight line is the shortest path between two points.
On a sphere (the Earth): a "straight line" = a great circle.
A plane flying from Hanoi to New York does not follow a straight line on a flat map
— it arcs over the North Pole. That IS the shortest path on a sphere.

In curved spacetime: objects follow GEODESICS = the straightest possible path.
Earth is not being "pulled" by the Sun — Earth travels straight through spacetime
that the Sun has curved → a circular orbit IS "straight" on a curved surface.

### §11.3 — Analogy: Rubber Sheet + Bowling Ball

Stretch a rubber sheet. Place a bowling ball (= the Sun) in the center → the sheet SAGS.
Roll a marble (= Earth) → it circles the depression.

```
        flat                    curved

   ─────────────        ──────╲    ╱──────
                                ╲  ╱
                                 ╲╱
                              (bowling)
```

**Limitations of this analogy:**
- The sheet is 2D; spacetime is 4D
- The marble rolls on the sheet because of external gravity (it "falls" into the depression)
  → curved spacetime NEEDS NO external gravity — the curvature ITSELF is gravity
- The analogy helps visualize but is NOT fully accurate

### §11.4 — Verification: Mercury's Orbit

Newton predicted planetary orbits with great precision — EXCEPT Mercury.
Mercury's perihelion (closest orbital point) advances ~5,600 arcseconds per century.
Newton explained ~5,557 (from gravitational pulls of other planets).
Missing: 43 arcseconds per century. Small, but measurable, and nobody could explain it.

Einstein (1915): General Relativity predicts exactly 43 arcseconds.
Nothing added. Curved spacetime delivers it automatically.

Einstein reportedly said when he finished the calculation: "I was SHOCKED... my heart raced."
That was the moment he knew the theory was CORRECT.

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────┐
│                                     │
│   Gμν + Λgμν = (8πG/c⁴) × Tμν     │
│                                     │

```

One equation. Describes EVERYTHING about gravity + spacetime.
(Actually 10 equations compressed into 1 via tensor notation)

### §12.2 — Decoding Each Part

**Left side — Geometry (how spacetime is curved):**

```
Gμν = Einstein tensor
    = describes CURVATURE (how much, in which direction)
    = computed from the metric tensor gμν (measures distance at each point)
```

```
Λgμν = cosmological constant term
     = Λ (lambda) = cosmological constant
     = Einstein added it in 1917 to keep the universe "static"
     = he dropped it when Hubble discovered the universe was expanding ("greatest blunder")
     = 1998: universe discovered to be ACCELERATING → Λ comes back = dark energy
```

**Right side — Matter + Energy:**

```
Tμν = stress-energy tensor
    = describes WHAT IS THERE (mass, energy, pressure, momentum)
    = the "material" that determines how spacetime curves

8πG/c⁴ = proportionality constant (connects geometry units to physics units)
G = Newton's gravitational constant
c = speed of light
```

**Reading the equation:**
```
SHAPE of spacetime = WHAT IS INSIDE IT

"Matter tells spacetime how to curve,
 curved spacetime tells matter how to move"
                                    — John Wheeler
```

### §12.3 — What Is a Tensor?

**Scalar:** 1 number. Example: temperature = 30°C. No direction.

**Vector:** 1 arrow. Example: wind at 20 km/h heading North. Has direction.
In 3D: needs 3 numbers (x, y, z).

**Tensor:** a MULTI-DIMENSIONAL table of numbers.
In 4D spacetime: a rank-2 tensor = a 4×4 table = 16 numbers.
Each cell describes curvature/pressure/momentum in one PAIR of directions.

```
         t    x    y    z
    t [ Ttt  Ttx  Tty  Ttz ]
    x [ Txt  Txx  Txy  Txz ]
    y [ Tyt  Tyx  Tyy  Tyz ]
    z [ Tzt  Tzx  Tzy  Tzz ]
```

Game dev analogy:
- **Heightmap** = 2D array: each cell = 1 number (elevation). Scalar field.
- **Wind map** = 2D array: each cell = 1 vector (direction + magnitude). Vector field.
- **Stress map** = 2D array: each cell = 1 TABLE (stress for each pair of directions). Tensor field.

The spacetime metric gμν = a 4×4 table AT EACH POINT in spacetime,
telling you how to "measure" distance between two nearby points.
Flat spacetime → gμν is a simple table (Minkowski metric).
Curved spacetime → gμν varies by position → the equations become complex.

### §12.4 — Why It Is Hard to Solve

Einstein's equations are NOT LINEAR — the output (curvature) feeds back into the input.
Curvature affects how matter moves,
moving matter changes curvature again → a feedback loop.

Like a system of equations where the left side depends on the right AND the right depends on the left.
Exact solutions exist only for SIMPLE CASES (high symmetry).
Most situations require numerical computation (numerical relativity).

## §13 — Level 11: Black Holes

### §13.1 — Schwarzschild (1916)

Karl Schwarzschild found the first exact solution to Einstein's equations
— spacetime surrounding a NON-ROTATING, UNCHARGED spherical mass.
He solved it while serving in the MILITARY on the Eastern Front, WWI. He died months later.

### §13.2 — Schwarzschild Radius

```
┌──────────────────┐
│                  │
│   rs = 2GM/c²   │
│                  │
└──────────────────┘
```

If the entire mass M is compressed within radius rs → black hole.

| Object | Mass M | rs | Comparison |
|--------|--------|----|------------|
| Human 70 kg | 70 kg | 10⁻²⁵ m | ~100 million times smaller than a proton |
| Earth | 5.97 × 10²⁴ kg | 8.87 mm | A marble |
| Sun | 1.99 × 10³⁰ kg | 2.95 km | A small town |
| Sgr A* (Milky Way) | 4 × 10⁶ M☉ | 11.8 million km | ~17 × the Sun's radius |
| M87* (galaxy M87) | 6.5 × 10⁹ M☉ | 19.2 billion km | Larger than the solar system |
| Observable Universe | ~10⁵³ kg | ~13.7 billion light-years | ≈ universe's radius (coincidence?) |

### §13.3 — Event Horizon

At r = rs: NOT "strong gravitational pull."
Rather: spacetime is curved to the point where ALL FUTURE DIRECTIONS point inward.

Like standing on Earth — every direction on the surface is "sideways,"
no direction takes you off the Earth.
Event horizon: every direction in spacetime is "downward,"
no direction leads outward — not even for light.

Space and time SWAP roles inside the event horizon:
- Outside: you are forced to move forward in TIME (you cannot stop time)
- Inside: you are forced to move forward in SPACE (toward the center, you cannot stop)

### §13.4 — Time Near a Black Hole

```
t_far = t_local / √(1 - rs/r)
```

- r = distance from center
- When r >> rs: t_far ≈ t_local (normal)
- When r → rs: denominator → 0 → t_far → ∞

Someone falling into a black hole: FEELS NOTHING SPECIAL crossing the event horizon
(if the black hole is large enough — tidal forces are small).
But a distant observer sees: the falling person SLOWING DOWN → FREEZING → FADING.
Light redshifts → dims → disappears. The falling person "freezes" at the event horizon
— from the outside observer's perspective.

### §13.5 — Verification: Direct Images

**2019 — Event Horizon Telescope (EHT):**
Eight telescopes across the globe combined into a single Earth-sized telescope.
Captured an image of M87* — the black hole at the center of galaxy M87, 55 million light-years away.

**2022 — Sgr A*:**
Image of Sgr A* — the black hole at the center of the Milky Way, 26,000 light-years away.

Both images: a bright ring surrounding a central dark shadow.
Bright ring = photons skimming near the event horizon, bent in orbit around the black hole.
Dark shadow = event horizon (photons that fell in cannot escape).
Shape and size: EXACTLY as General Relativity predicted.

## §14 — Level 12: Gravitational Waves

### §14.1 — Concept

An accelerating mass → creates ripples propagating through spacetime.
Like throwing a stone into a pond → water waves. But here the waves ARE SPACETIME ITSELF rippling.

Einstein predicted this (1916). Many doubted: the waves would be too weak to ever detect.

### §14.2 — LIGO

**Laser Interferometer Gravitational-wave Observatory.**
2 detectors: Hanford (Washington) + Livingston (Louisiana), 3,000 km apart.
Each detector: 2 perpendicular vacuum tubes, each 4 km long.
Lasers fire through both tubes, reflect back, and interfere.
A gravitational wave passing through → stretches one tube, compresses the other → interference pattern shifts.

What does it measure?

```
h = ΔL / L ≈ 10⁻²¹
```

h = strain.
ΔL = change in length.
L = 4 km = 4,000 m.

ΔL = h × L = 10⁻²¹ × 4,000 = 4 × 10⁻¹⁸ m.

Compare:
- Proton diameter: ~10⁻¹⁵ m
- LIGO measures: 10⁻¹⁸ m = **1/1,000 of a proton diameter**

Analogy: measuring the distance from Earth to Proxima Centauri (4.2 light-years)
to a precision of **the width of a human hair.**

### §14.3 — First Detection: GW150914 (September 14, 2015)

Two black holes spiraling inward → merging:
- Black hole A: ~36 M☉ (solar masses)
- Black hole B: ~29 M☉
- Before merger: 65 M☉
- After merger: ~62 M☉
- **Missing: 3 M☉ → converted to gravitational wave energy**

3 M☉ = 3 × 2 × 10³⁰ kg → E = mc² = 5.4 × 10⁴⁷ Joules.
Released in ~0.2 seconds.
Peak power at that moment: **~3.6 × 10⁴⁹ Watts**
= greater than the combined luminosity of ALL OBSERVABLE STARS IN THE UNIVERSE.

The signal reached LIGO as a "chirp" — rising frequency then silence.
Waveform: MATCHED EXACTLY the numerical relativity simulation.

Nobel Prize in Physics 2017: Weiss, Barish, Thorne.

## §15 — Level 13: Cosmology

### §15.1 — Hubble (1929): The Universe Is Expanding

Edwin Hubble measured the spectra of distant galaxies:
light was REDSHIFTED — wavelengths stretched.
The farther the galaxy → the greater the redshift → receding FASTER.

```
v = H₀ × d

v = recession speed
d = distance
H₀ = Hubble constant ≈ 70 km/s/Mpc
    (Mpc = Megaparsec = 3.26 million light-years)
```

Every additional 3.26 million light-years of distance → recession speed increases by 70 km/s.

**Galaxies are not flying outward.** Spacetime itself is EXPANDING.
Galaxies do not move THROUGH space — the space BETWEEN them is stretching.

Analogy: draw dots on a balloon and blow it up.
The dots do not move on the balloon's surface — but the distances between them INCREASE.
Every dot sees every other dot RECEDING — no dot is at the "center."

### §15.2 — The Big Bang

If the universe is expanding → run time backward → everything was CLOSER together.
Go back far enough (~13.8 billion years) → the entire observable universe was compressed into an unimaginably small, hot point.

The Big Bang is NOT "an explosion in empty space."
The Big Bang = space itself BEGAN EXPANDING. There is no "outside" to explode into.
There is no "before" the Big Bang in the ordinary sense
(time began with the Big Bang — asking "what came before" is like asking "what is north of the North Pole").

**Verification: Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB)**
Penzias + Wilson (1965, Nobel 1978): detected the "afterglow" of the Big Bang
— microwave background radiation at 2.725 K, uniform across the entire sky.
WMAP + Planck satellite detailed measurements: tiny fluctuations in the CMB
MATCH PRECISELY the predictions from the Big Bang model.

### §15.3 — Friedmann Equation — The Universe's "Heartbeat"

Alexander Friedmann (1922) solved Einstein's equations for a homogeneous universe:

```
(ȧ/a)² = (8πG/3)ρ - kc²/a² + Λc²/3
```

- a(t) = scale factor (size of the universe at time t, relative to today where a = 1)
- ȧ = da/dt = rate of expansion
- ρ = total energy density (matter + radiation + ...)
- k = curvature:
  - k = +1: closed universe (spherical)
  - k = 0: flat universe (infinite)
  - k = -1: open universe (saddle-shaped)
- Λ = cosmological constant (dark energy)

**3 scenarios:**
```
               a(t)
                ↑
                │        ╱ Λ > 0: accelerating expansion forever (Big Freeze)
                │       ╱
                │      ╱─── k=0, Λ=0: decelerating expansion, stops at t→∞ (Flat)
                │     ╱ ╱
                │    ╱╱    ╲
                │   ╱╱      ╲ large ρ, Λ=0: expand then COLLAPSE (Big Crunch)
                │  ╱╱        ╲
                │ ╱╱          ╲
                │╱╱
                └──────────────→ t
              Big Bang
```

### §15.4 — Dark Energy: The Universe Is Accelerating

1998: Perlmutter, Schmidt, Riess measured Type Ia supernovae (standard candles)
at great distances → found: they were DIMMER than expected
→ farther than expected → universe expanding FASTER than expected → **accelerating**.

Λ > 0. Dark energy comprises ~68% of the universe's total energy.

95% of the universe = something nobody understands. We know it exists (we measure its effects).

Nobel Prize in Physics 2011: Perlmutter, Schmidt, Riess.

### §15.5 — Observable Universe

The universe is ~13.8 billion years old.
