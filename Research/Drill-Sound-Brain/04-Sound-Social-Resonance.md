## §5 — Niche vs Mainstream Genre Filter

```
🟢 SELFHOUT 2009: NON-MAINSTREAM PREDICTS FRIENDSHIP FORMATION:

  🟢 Selfhout et al. 2009 (Journal of Adolescence 32:95-107):
    N=283 Dutch same-sex mutual best friends, mean age 12.97, 1-year longitudinal
    Music similarity → friendship formation
    ⭐ "Specific music similarity in more NON-MAINSTREAM music dimensions
       at Wave 1 were related to selecting a new friend at Wave 2"
    → Rock/Pop/Dance = NOT significant for friendship selection
    → Non-mainstream = STRONGER signal for formation

  🟢 Ter Bogt et al. 2017 (SNARE, N=1,144, SIENA):
    ⭐ Only HIGHBROW (jazz/classical) consistently predicted friendship selection
    → Urban: borderline (p=0.06 when controlling for behavior)
    → Rock, Pop, Dance = NOT significant for selection
    → = CONFIRMS non-mainstream/highbrow = actual filter

  FRAMEWORK ANALYSIS — Filter Specificity:

  MAINSTREAM (pop, Top 40):
    → Majority of population enjoy (surveys ~60-80%, varies by measure)
    → "Cùng thích pop" = thông tin ÍT (hầu hết mọi người thích)
    → Background-Pattern overlap signal = WEAK
    → Tỉ lệ false positive CAO: taste match ≠ Background-Pattern match
    → = LOW specificity filter
    → ⚠️ Exact % varies wildly by measure (sales, streaming, survey, "enjoy" vs "prefer")

  NICHE (jazz, metal, classical, ambient, progressive):
    → Smaller audience (jazz ~1% of sales, ~20% "prefer" in surveys)
    → "Cùng thích jazz" = thông tin NHIỀU (ít người thích → similarity MORE meaningful)
    → Background-Pattern overlap signal = STRONG
    → Tỉ lệ false positive THẤP: taste match → LIKELY Background-Pattern match
    → = HIGH specificity filter
    → ⚠️ "Niche" definition depends on measure — streaming vs identity vs purchase

  WITHIN NICHE (sub-genres):
    → Bebop jazz vs smooth jazz vs free jazz
    → Each sub-genre = EVEN narrower filter
    → "Cùng thích bebop" > "cùng thích jazz" cho specificity
    → BUT: too narrow → too few people → hard to FIND

  ⭐ INVERTED-U AT DISCOVERY, MONOTONIC AT ACCURACY:
    → Discovery probability: too niche = nobody shares → inverted-U
    → Signal accuracy: more niche = stronger signal → monotonic increase
    → Optimal: niche ENOUGH cho strong signal, broad ENOUGH để tìm được người

  🟢 Selfhout 2009: non-mainstream > mainstream for friendship formation
  🟢 Ter Bogt 2017: only highbrow consistently predicted friendship selection
  🟡 Filter specificity analysis = framework synthesis
  ⚠️ v1.2: Citation fixed (Journal of Adolescence, NOT JPSP). Ter Bogt findings integrated.
```
