## 10 INSIGHTS TỪ DRILL BỔ SUNG VÀO PLAN

### Nhóm 1: Kiến trúc — làm rõ VỊ TRÍ Body-Knowing

① **95/5 split**: ~95% cuộc sống = compiled schemas (Body-Knowing territory). Fresh PFC = ~5%.
   → Body-Knowing = DEFAULT mode, Fresh = exception.
   → THÊM VÀO: §1 Definition

② **Pattern matching + prediction error = unified mechanism**: Body-Knowing chạy trên
   cùng cơ chế detection duy nhất. Match → Body-Knowing. Mismatch → pre-Body-Knowing/Fresh.
   → THÊM VÀO: §1 Definition

③ **"Think yourself into knowing" = impossible**: Body-Knowing emerge từ compilation (bottom-up).
   PFC chỉ SET attention/question → body compile → Body-Knowing hình thành.
   → THÊM VÀO: §1 Definition + §4 Formation

### Nhóm 2: Multi-channel — làm rõ CHẤT LƯỢNG

④ **Multi-channel convergence**: Body-Knowing MẠNH khi nhiều channels converge.
   Expert = multi-channel strong. "Ngờ ngợ" = weak/incomplete convergence.
   → THÊM VÀO: §7 Quality

⑤ **Compiled dominates (~80%)**: Nguồn ③ Compiled Schemas = ~80% anchor fills.
   → THÊM VÀO: §1 Definition + §8 Spectrum

### Nhóm 3: Bidirectional — làm rõ FORMATION

⑥ **Bidirectional architecture**: PFC SET question (top-down) → release → body compile in
   background → reload → Body-Knowing hình thành. PFC không "wait actively."
   → THÊM VÀO: §4 Formation

⑦ **3-tầng cross-species**: Body-Knowing ở CẢ 3 tầng. Tầng 0 (reactive) → Tầng 1 (simulated)
   → Tầng 2 (directed: human). Cùng mechanism, khác layers.
   → THÊM VÀO: §2 Ba Hướng (subsection mới)

### Nhóm 4: Connections — làm rõ RELATED CONCEPTS

⑧ **Convergence Zone** (Feeling.md §7.4): = "PREVIEW of chunk chưa tồn tại"
   = pre-Body-Knowing territory. H5 multi-weak-signal convergence = MECHANISM behind "ngờ ngợ."
   → THÊM VÀO: §5 Pre-Body-Knowing

⑨ **Verbal ≠ prerequisite**: Body-Knowing exists WITHOUT verbal.
   Verbal giúp SHARE Body-Knowing nhưng không CREATE nó.
   → THÊM VÀO: §1 Definition + §2 Ba Hướng

⑩ **"Fabrication thật" vs "Somatic signal"**: Phân biệt Body-Knowing signal vs noise.
   Body-Knowing signal: body distinct, pulled toward, satisfaction khi phrase đúng.
   Fabrication: body neutral, no pull, boring to self.
   → THÊM VÀO: §5 Pre-Body-Knowing hoặc §6 Cases

### Nhóm 5: Pre-Body-Knowing 3 sub-types (v2.1 — từ Feeling Label Refine + Map)

⑪ **3 trạng thái pre-Body-Knowing KHÁC NHAU** (tất cả = PFC chưa biết rõ ràng):

   **Vague (lờ mờ)** — Earliest, weakest:
     PFC detect signal yếu, chưa biết gì về nó. Không direction, không valence rõ.
     "Thấy nhưng không rõ gì." Có thể fade nếu không engage.
     Gradient: ③-⑥ (broad). Mechanism: multi-channel yếu, PFC detect "gì đó."
     English: vague ★ (đã rename, 55 occ). Feeling §7.1 ③-⑥.

   **Hunch (ngờ ngợ)** — Intermediate, directional:
     PFC detect signal CÓ HƯỚNG, gần biết nhưng chưa encode được. "Gần Eureka."
     Valence: neutral-curious. "Có gì đó về hướng đó."
     Gradient: ⑤ (specific). Mechanism: pattern partially matched, PFC gần encode.
     English: hunch ★ (candidate, chưa renamed). Feeling §7.1 ⑤.

   **Nagging unease (lấn cấn)** — Persistent, negative:
     PFC detect signal NEGATIVE liên tục, body không chịu ngừng signal. "Biết có vấn đề, không biết vấn đề gì."
     Valence: negative. Persistent: body keeps firing dù PFC muốn ignore.
     Gradient: ③-⑤ + negative tag. Mechanism: prediction-delta detected, PFC không tìm ra nguồn.
     English: nagging unease ★ (đã rename, 13 occ).

   CẢ 3 = Body-Knowing CHƯA HOÀN THÀNH — body đã detect, PFC chưa có compiled chunks match.
   KHÁC NHAU: direction (none/có/negative), persistence (fade/moderate/chronic), valence (neutral/curious/negative).
   → THÊM VÀO: §5 Pre-Body-Knowing (3 sub-types table)

⑫ **Pre-Body-Knowing Vocabulary Taxonomy** (v2.2):
   Pre-Body-Knowing = FAMILY of states, không phải 1 trạng thái.
   CÓ THỂ liệt kê systematic bằng 2 trục: proximity (gần Body-Knowing cỡ nào) × valence (+/0/−).

- §5.1 — FORMAL DEFINITION:
  Body detected prediction-delta but PFC cannot yet encode = Pre-Body-Knowing.
  Gendlin felt sense connection (1978). NOT a single state — a FAMILY of states.
  Pre-Body-Knowing = PREREQUISITE OF KNOWING — territory between [nothing] and [Body-Knowing].

- §5.2 — FULL GRADIENT: [Nothing] → [Pre-Body-Knowing] → [Body-Knowing]
  (v2.2 — pre-Body-Knowing vocabulary taxonomy, 100% English)

  2 AXES:
    Axis 1: PROXIMITY — how close to Body-Knowing (far → near)
    Axis 2: VALENCE — positive / neutral / negative

  TABLE (~12-15 terms):

  ```
                   FAR from knowing ──────────────────── NEAR knowing
                   (body barely fires)                   (almost there)

  POSITIVE:        warm sense          resonance          recognition
                   promising           familiarity        "almost sure"

  NEUTRAL:         dim awareness       inkling            tip-of-tongue
                   vague sense         hunch              on the verge

  NEGATIVE:        unease              misgiving          foreboding
                   disquiet            nagging unease     dread
  ```

  RESEARCH-BACKED terms (marked in table):
    → tip-of-tongue (TOT): Brown & McNeill 1966 🟢 — know you know, can't retrieve
    → feeling of knowing (FOK): Hart 1965 🟢 — metacognitive judgment of future recognition
    → felt sense: Gendlin 1978 🟢 — pre-verbal body knowing
    → implicit knowledge: Reber 1967 🟢 — know without being able to articulate

  EXAMPLE — Foreign language learning (100% English):
    [nothing] → dim awareness ("looks familiar") → vague sense ("related to something...")
    → hunch ("probably means...") → tip-of-tongue ("I KNOW this but can't recall!")
    → Body-Knowing ("Ah! It means XYZ!")

  EXAMPLE — Expert diagnosis (100% English):
    [nothing] → unease ("something's off with this patient") → misgiving ("this doesn't fit
    the standard pattern") → hunch ("might be condition X") → recognition ("yes, it's X")
    → Body-Knowing ("X confirmed — I've seen this pattern before")

  ⚠️ Vietnamese is RICHER in this zone (VN ★★★★, EN ★★★ — Feeling Map §32).
  English advantage: many terms already have research backing (TOT, FOK = formal psychology).
  Framework STANDARDIZES in English for this zone — avoids mistranslation of inherently vague concepts.

- §5.3 — 3 PRIMARY SUB-TYPES (framework's core distinction):

  **Type 1: Vague** (Vietnamese origin: lờ mờ) — Earliest stage
    Signal: weak, no direction, no clear valence. May fade if not engaged.
    Proximity: FAR. Gradient: ③-⑥ (broad).
    Mechanism: multi-channel weak, PFC detect threshold barely crossed.

  **Type 2: Hunch** (Vietnamese origin: ngờ ngợ) — Intermediate, directional
    Signal: moderate, HAS direction, neutral-curious valence. Persists if domain relevant.
    Proximity: MIDDLE. Gradient: ⑤ (specific).
    Mechanism: pattern PARTIALLY matched, PFC near encoding but insufficient chunks.

  **Type 3: Nagging unease** (Vietnamese origin: lấn cấn) — Persistent, negative
    Signal: moderate, HAS direction (negative), won't stop. Body keeps firing.
    Proximity: MIDDLE + negative tag. Gradient: ③-⑤.
    Mechanism: prediction-delta + negative valence tag, PFC cannot identify source.

  All 3 = pre-Body-Knowing (body detected, PFC cannot yet encode).
  DIFFER in: direction × valence × persistence.
  Progression: vague → hunch → Body-Knowing (if engage + compile more chunks).

- §5.4 — MECHANISM: H5 + CONVERGENCE ZONE
  Convergence Zone = "PREVIEW of a chunk that doesn't yet exist" (Feeling.md §7.4)
  H5 multi-weak-signal convergence = MECHANISM behind ALL pre-Body-Knowing states
  Body detecting REAL pattern that chunk system hasn't yet formalized
  Gap-Direction: pre-Body-Knowing = gap WITH direction but PFC can't yet encode it

- §5.5 — EXPERT vs NOVICE RELIABILITY
  Expert: large compiled base → pre-Body-Knowing signals MORE TRUSTWORTHY
  Novice: small compiled base → pre-Body-Knowing signals LESS TRUSTWORTHY
  Kahneman + Klein 2009: conditions for reliable intuition

- §5.6 — 3 REACTIONS (SAL §2.3)
  A: Dismiss ("probably nothing") → opportunity missed. Most common reaction.
  B: Over-trust ("I feel it so it's true") → risky, body can be wrong.
  C: Engage with humility ("let me try to phrase it") → FRAMEWORK RECOMMENDS.
  Darwin 1840s = classic Reaction C.

- §5.7 — FABRICATION vs SOMATIC SIGNAL (SAL §2.3)
  Pre-Body-Knowing signal: body distinct, pulled toward topic, frustration when phrasing fails
  Fabrication: body neutral, no pull, boring to self
  Rule of thumb: if you feel "pulled" to keep exploring → it's pre-Body-Knowing

- §5.8 — VALUE: Do NOT dismiss (body HAS information). Do NOT over-trust (vague ≠ correct).
  Engage WITH humility = path to Body-Knowing formation (→ §4).
