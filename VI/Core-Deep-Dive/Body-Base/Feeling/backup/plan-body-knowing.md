### §1 — DEFINITION
- Body-Knowing = compiled chunks + body signal → PFC encode → "biết mà không cần giải thích"
- 3 properties: ① compiled (Hebbian-reinforced), ② body-direct (cost ≈ 0), ③ PFC-encodable (chunks match)
- ⚠️ ≠ always correct (1B trap, shelf-life, wrong context)
- ⚠️ ≠ emotional feeling alone (covers ALL domains)
- ⚠️ ≠ chỉ hướng ra ngoài (bao gồm cả nhận biết trạng thái bên trong)
- Công thức: Body-Knowing = f(compiled_chunks × signal_match × PFC_access)

**Source:** Inter-Body-Mechanism.md v2.0 §3, Feeling.md §1.1, Klein 1998, Gendlin 1978.

### §2 — BA HƯỚNG (Outward + Inward + Social)

**§2.1 — Outward (pattern recognition):**
- Einstein "biết" lời giải toán (compiled math chunks → body-direct)
- Chef "biết" thiếu muối (compiled taste patterns → insula direct)
- Therapist "biết" patient pattern (compiled clinical patterns)
- Engineer "biết" code smell (compiled architecture patterns)
- = Body-Knowing về EXTERNAL PATTERNS

**§2.2 — Inward (state recognition):**
- "Tôi biết tôi buồn" (compiled emotional vocabulary → L6 label match)
- "Tôi biết tôi mệt" (compiled body-state patterns → recognize)
- "Tôi biết tôi sợ" (compiled fear pattern → identify)
- Contrast: "Giận xả luôn" = PFC KHÔNG KỊP encode = NOT Body-Knowing
- Contrast: "Oải oải, không rõ" = PARTIAL Body-Knowing (approximate)
- Contrast: Alexithymia = Body-Knowing cho internal states IMPAIRED
- = Body-Knowing về INTERNAL STATES

**§2.3 — Social (mirror recognition):**
- "Tôi biết bạn buồn" (compiled mirror patterns → body mirrors → recognize)
- Mẹ "biết" con đang giấu chuyện (compiled decades of observation)
- = Body-Knowing về OTHER PEOPLE'S STATES

**§2.4 — CƠ CHẾ GIỐNG HỆT:**
- Biến số = compiled chunks cho DOMAIN ĐÓ (not content type)
- Có chunks → Body-Knowing. Không có chunks → NOT Body-Knowing.
- Thiền sư Body-Knows internal states (years compile) = Expert Body-Knowing
- Alexithymic KHÔNG Body-Know internal states = NO Body-Knowing cho domain đó

**Source:** Session analysis, Feeling.md §2.5 (7-Layer × Compiled/Fresh), Feeling-Sources.

### §3 — 7-LAYER MAPPING

Map Body-Knowing lên 7-Layer Fidelity Gradient (Feeling.md §2):

```
Layer     | Body-Knowing status
L1-L2     | HARDWARE — body fires, pre-PFC. NOT YET Body-Knowing (PFC chưa involved)
L3        | THRESHOLD CROSSING — signal rises. Body-Knowing POTENTIAL (PFC có thể detect)
L4-L5     | OBSERVATION — compiled → Body-Knowing ("biết"). Fresh → NOT Body-Knowing ("phải tìm")
L6        | LABELING — compiled label → CLEAR Body-Knowing. No label → vague / partial
L7        | EXPLANATION — FRESH PFC draft = NOT Body-Knowing (PFC Lawyer, may confabulate)
```

Key insight: Body-Knowing = L4-L6 COMPILED. Fidelity INVERSELY correlated with fresh.
Training = compile lower layers → expand Body-Knowing range.

**Source:** Feeling.md §2.5, Inter-Body §3.

### §4 — FORMATION PROCESS (5 Stages)

**Core discovery session 2026-05-25:** Body-Knowing có process hình thành.

```
① SILENT — Body compiled, PFC unaware. Chunks accumulate in background.
② VAGUE SIGNAL — Body fires prediction-delta, PFC detect "có gì đó" nhưng chưa encode.
   = "Ngờ ngợ" / felt sense / pre-Body-Knowing.
③ PROCESSING — PFC HOLD + search. Active (analyze) or Passive (relax + hold).
④ THRESHOLD CROSSING — Pattern crystallize → PFC encode → "Eureka!" = Body-Knowing FORMS.
⑤ CONSOLIDATION — Repeat + domain verify → stable compile.
```

Map lên Somatic-Articulation-Loop 7 stages:
  SAL Stage 1 (Accumulate) = Body-Knowing ①
  SAL Stage 2 (Fire) + 3 (Notice) = Body-Knowing ②
  SAL Stage 4 (Catalyst) + 5 (Confirm) + 6 (Sharpen) = Body-Knowing ③
  SAL Stage 7 (Felt Shift) = Body-Knowing ④
  Post-SAL (Domain verify) = Body-Knowing ⑤

3 ví dụ minh họa (từ session):
  - Nhà toán: "ngờ ngợ bài toán sai" → phân tích → "thấy lỗi bước 3!"
  - Bác sĩ tâm lý: "ngờ ngợ bệnh nhân" → quan sát thêm → "attachment anxiety!"
  - Archimedes: hold problem → tắm → "Eureka! Displacement principle!"
  - Cảm xúc: "oải oải" → sit with it / therapy → "ah, tôi lonely"

**Source:** Somatic-Articulation-Loop.md §1-§2, PFC-Function.md §2 (HOLD), Session analysis.

### §5 — PRE-BODY-KNOWING: "NGỜ NGỢ" (Felt Sense)

Phân tích formal:
- "Ngờ ngợ" = body detected prediction-delta nhưng PFC chưa encode
- = Gendlin felt sense (1978): body knows before words arrive
- = Feeling.md §7 Convergence Zone: feeling = PREVIEW of chunk chưa tồn tại
- = Gap-Direction: body POINTS toward something (has direction)

Expert vs Novice "ngờ ngợ":
- Expert ngờ ngợ: compiled base rộng → vague signal ĐÁNG TIN (nhiều chunks inform)
- Novice ngờ ngợ: compiled base hẹp → vague signal ÍT TIN CẬY
- → "Trust expert ngờ ngợ MORE" = well-grounded (Kahneman + Klein 2009 conditions)

Value of pre-Body-Knowing:
- KHÔNG dismiss (body CÓ information, chưa encode)
- KHÔNG over-trust (vague ≠ correct)
- ENGAGE WITH HUMILITY (Somatic-Articulation-Loop §2 Stage 3, reaction C)

**Source:** Gendlin 1978, Feeling.md §7 (Gradient + Convergence Zone + H5), SAL §2 Stage 3.

### §6 — PRE-BODY-KNOWING: 4 CASES KHÔNG "BIẾT"

Phân tích 4 cases từ session:

**Case A: "Giận xả luôn" — Body acts, PFC không kịp encode**
- L1-L2 fire MẠNH → body acts TRƯỚC PFC
- = NOT Body-Knowing (PFC chưa "biết" trạng thái gì)
- After: "À, tôi đã giận" = Body-Knowing arrives AFTER the fact
- Framework: amygdala hijack, PFC bypassed

**Case B: "Oải oải, label là chán" — Approximate Body-Knowing**
- L3-L4 partial → L6 approximate label
- = PARTIAL Body-Knowing (chunks match imprecise)
- Có thể sai: "chán" thực ra = "đói" / "thiếu ngủ" / "lonely"
- Framework: chunk library insufficient for precise match

**Case C: "Không biết sao cứ khó chịu" — Pre-Body-Knowing**
- L3 rises → L4-L5 try → L6 FAIL (no matching chunk)
- = NOT Body-Knowing (body state exists but no compiled chunk encode)
- = Gendlin felt sense territory — engagement needed
- Framework: gap without direction (chunk library too thin)

**Case D: Alexithymia — Structurally impaired**
- L1-L2 intact (body fires normally)
- L4-L6 structurally impaired (PFC cannot access)
- = NOT Body-Knowing permanently (for internal states)
- = ~10% population (Taylor 2000)
- Framework: access pathway broken, not signal broken

**Source:** Session analysis, Feeling.md §7, Taylor 2000.

### §7 — BODY-KNOWING QUALITY

**§7.1 — 1A vs 1B:**
- 1A Body-Knowing = domain-verified → RELIABLE ("mượt thật")
- 1B Body-Knowing = self-referencing → DANGEROUS ("mượt giả")
- Source: Feeling.md §8 (1A/1B), Inter-Body §6.4

**§7.2 — Shelf-Life:**
- Stable-arc domains: Body-Knowing ≈ ∞ (toán, vật lý)
- Shifting-arc domains: Body-Knowing LIMITED (trading, medicine)
- Collective arc evolves → individual Body-Knowing outdated
- Source: Logic-Feeling.md v3.0 §3.2

**§7.3 — Compile Context:**
- HOW chunks compiled matters (calm vs threat, supported vs isolated)
- Trauma compile = 1× trauma ≈ 100× neutral (by emotional weight)
- Source: Feeling.md §8 (quality = f(chunk_library × compile_context × current_state))

**§7.4 — PFC = Lawyer applies to Body-Knowing TOO:**
- Body-Knowing (L1-L5) = MORE reliable
- PFC explanation of Body-Knowing (L7) = may confabulate
- "I know X" is reliable. "I know X BECAUSE Y" may be lawyering.
- Source: Feeling.md §3.4, Inter-Body §7

### §8 — BODY-KNOWING × COMPILED/FRESH SPECTRUM

- Body-Knowing = compiled end (automatic, cost ≈ 0)
- Fresh = not yet Body-Knowing (PFC draft, cost > 0)
- Spectrum, not binary (semi-compiled states exist)
- Transition both ways: Fresh→Body-Knowing (learning), Body-Knowing→Fresh (unlearning)
- 3-cost model: why can't stay fresh (PFC draft + suppress + uncertainty)
- Source: Inter-Body §3-§4, Logic-Feeling.md v3.0 §1.2
