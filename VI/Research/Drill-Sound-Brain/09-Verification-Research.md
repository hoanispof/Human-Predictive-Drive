## §2 — Phase 1: Voice, Melody, Attention

### §2.1 — Insight 40-41: Voice 3-Channel Processing

```
ORIGINAL CLAIM:
  Giọng hát xử lý qua 3 kênh TÁCH BIỆT:
  ① Semantic (Broca/Wernicke, left) — cần hiểu ngôn ngữ
  ② Prosody (right hemisphere + insula) — KHÔNG cần hiểu
  ③ Voice-as-instrument (auditory cortex + reward) — KHÔNG cần hiểu
  → 2/3 channels work without language → foreign music still moves us
  → Semantic OFF → PFC budget freed → purer emotion

VERDICT: CONFIRMED (with refinement needed)

  ✅ Semantic left + Prosody right = TEXTBOOK NEUROSCIENCE
     Kotz et al. fMRI: extensive left-lateralized network cho semantic,
     right-lateralized cho emotional prosody.

  ✅ Voice-as-instrument = SUPPORTED by NEW KEY EVIDENCE:
     ⭐ Norman-Haignere, Kanwisher et al. 2022 (Current Biology):
     Phát hiện SONG-SELECTIVE NEURAL POPULATION trong auditory cortex
     → respond to SINGING but NOT to instrumental music or speech alone
     → anatomically near nhưng DISTINCT from speech-selective population
     = STRONGER evidence than drill originally cited

  ✅ 2/3 channels without language = CONFIRMED:
     Fritz et al. 2009: Mafa people (Cameroon, 0 Western music exposure)
     recognized basic emotions (happy, sad, scared) in Western music
     above chance → emotional info transmits WITHOUT semantic comprehension
     Balkwill & Thompson 1999: Western listeners correctly identified
     emotions in Hindustani ragas

  🟡 "PFC freed → purer emotion" = LOW-MODERATE confidence:
     Ferreri et al. 2013 (fNIRS): music DECREASES PFC activity during encoding
     Frontiers 2026: lyrics "dampen emotional intensity and perceived positivity"
     Ding 2025: foreign lyrics reduce semantic interference
     → Supports direction BUT "purer" is inference, not directly tested
     → Familiarity effects may counteract (Pereira 2011: familiar music
       activates reward MORE than unfamiliar)

  ⚠️ CORRECTION NEEDED:
     "3 INDEPENDENT channels" → "3 PARTIALLY SEPARABLE processing streams"
     Gordon & Schon 2010: melody and words are "intertwined" in song perception
     Norman-Haignere 2022: populations anatomically adjacent, likely interact
     = Cross-talk exists. "Independent" overstates separation.

NEW CITATIONS:
  🟢 Norman-Haignere et al. 2022 (Current Biology) — song-selective neurons
  🟢 Fritz et al. 2009 — cross-cultural emotion recognition (Mafa)
  🟢 Balkwill & Thompson 1999 — cross-cultural emotion in Hindustani ragas
  🟢 Ferreri et al. 2013 — music decreases PFC activity (fNIRS)
  🟢 Gordon & Schon 2010 — melody-word intertwining
  🟢 Loui 2015 — dual-stream neuroanatomy of singing
```

### §2.2 — Insight 42: Melody ~5-6 Systems

```
ORIGINAL CLAIM:
  Melody line → ~5-6 systems simultaneously (auditory, motor, prediction,
  emotion, memory, language). = Most "activation-efficient" element.
  Explains: earworm (redundancy), first-lock, Alzheimer last-to-degrade.

VERDICT: PARTIALLY SUPPORTED

  ✅ Distributed network activation = CONFIRMED:
     Koelsch 2014 (Nature Reviews Neuroscience): music activates bilateral
     superior temporal, inferior frontal, parietal, premotor, cerebellum,
     + subcortical reward. "5-6 systems" = reasonable summary.

  ✅ Motor during passive listening = CONFIRMED + REPLICATED:
     Grahn & Brett 2007 (J Cogn Neurosci): basal ganglia + SMA activate
     during beat perception WITHOUT movement. Multiple replications confirm.

  ✅ Prediction system = CONFIRMED:
     Huron ITPRA theory. Janata 2009: dorsal MPFC tracks tonality in real time.

  ✅ Alzheimer melody preservation = CONFIRMED (with nuance):
     Jacobsen et al. 2015 (Brain): musical memory regions show minimal
     cortical atrophy + preserved glucose metabolism in AD.
     ⚠️ NUANCE: it is MUSICAL MEMORY broadly (not melody uniquely above
     other elements) that is preserved.

  🟡 Earworm = multi-system encoding = PLAUSIBLE but not proven causal:
     Jakubowski et al. 2017: melodic features (contour, intervals, repetition)
     key earworm triggers. Herholz et al. 2012: musical imagery activates
     overlapping auditory + sensorimotor networks. Spreading-activation
     model proposed. BUT: no study explicitly frames earworm as CAUSED BY
     multi-system encoding.

  ❌ "Melody = FIRST element locked" = NOT SUPPORTED:
     No study found claiming melody is first musical element encoded.
     Racette & Peretz 2007: initial encoding melody + lyrics TOGETHER
     actually harder than lyrics alone.
     → Intuitive but NO empirical backing.

  ❌ "Activation efficiency" as COMPARATIVE metric = NOT SUPPORTED:
     No neuroimaging study compares "systems activated / input complexity"
     across melody vs harmony vs rhythm vs bass.
     Framework is constructing this metric — it does NOT exist in literature.

  ⚠️ CORRECTIONS NEEDED:
     ① "Melody = first locked" → REMOVE or tag 🔴 hypothesis
     ② "Activation efficiency" → tag as 🟡 framework concept, not 🟢
     ③ "Alzheimer melody specifically" → "musical memory broadly"
     ④ "Earworm = multi-system redundancy" → add "plausible mechanism,
        not proven causal"

NEW CITATIONS:
  🟢 Jacobsen et al. 2015 (Brain) — musical memory preserved in AD
  🟢 Janata 2009 (Cerebral Cortex) — MPFC tonality tracking
  🟢 Herholz et al. 2012 — musical imagery networks
  🟢 Zatorre, Chen & Penhune 2007 — auditory-motor interactions
```

### §2.3 — Insight 38b: Attention Paradox ⚠️ OVERSTATED

```
ORIGINAL CLAIM:
  PFC analysis chiếm workspace → emotion/reward bị đẩy ra nền.
  Depth layers hoạt động vô thức. "Phân tích → hết sướng."
  Optimal = PFC MINIMAL.

VERDICT: ⚠️ OVERSTATED — key sub-claim CONTRADICTED

  ✅ GWT capacity limits = CONFIRMED:
     Baars 1988/1997: consciousness = serial, capacity-limited "bright spot."
     Widely accepted.

  ✅ ERAN unconscious processing = CONFIRMED:
     Koelsch 2002-2011: music-syntactic processing pre-attentive at ~150-200ms,
     works even when music is ignored, works in non-musicians.
     → Unconscious harmony processing is REAL and ROBUST.

  🟡 Transient hypofrontality (Dietrich 2004) = PARTIALLY SUPPORTED:
     Limb & Braun 2008: "extensive deactivation" of dorsolateral PFC
     during jazz IMPROVISATION.
     ⚠️ BUT: this was during PERFORMANCE, NOT passive listening.
     ⚠️ Medial PFC actually INCREASED → "PFC shutdown" is oversimplification.
     = Specifically LATERAL PFC self-monitoring decreases during flow.

  ❌ "Analysis kills pleasure" = CONTRADICTED by key evidence:
     ⭐ Hou et al. 2024 (PsyCh Journal): acquiring ANALYTICAL knowledge
     INCREASED music liking MORE than historical knowledge.
     = Direct contradiction of "phân tích → hết sướng"

     Brattico et al. 2016 (Frontiers in Neuroscience): enhanced fronto-insular
     activity in musicians during liking → experts access ADDITIONAL
     pleasure channels through cognitive appreciation.

     ⚠️ THE CRITICAL DISTINCTION MISSING FROM DRILL:
       Online analytical EFFORT (real-time monitoring during listening)
         → CAN interfere with flow-state immersion
       Offline analytical KNOWLEDGE (accumulated understanding)
         → INCREASES appreciation and liking

     = "Phân tích → hết sướng" WRONG as stated.
     = Correct version: "Effortful real-time monitoring → interfere with flow.
       BUT accumulated analytical understanding → enhance appreciation."

  ❌ "Optimal = PFC MINIMAL" = NOT SUPPORTED:
     Hou 2024 directly contradicts. Musicians ENJOY music through different
     (additional) pathways including cognitive appreciation.
     Expertise ADDS pleasure channels, does not remove them.

  ⚠️ REWRITE NEEDED:
     Section §7b cần restructure:
     ① Keep: ERAN unconscious processing (confirmed, valuable)
     ② Keep: Flow states reduce lateral PFC self-monitoring
     ③ REWRITE: "analysis kills pleasure" → distinction effort vs knowledge
     ④ ADD: Hou 2024, Brattico 2016 as key counter-evidence
     ⑤ REMOVE: "optimal = PFC minimal" absolute claim

NEW CITATIONS:
  ⭐ Hou et al. 2024 (PsyCh Journal) — analytical knowledge increases liking
  🟢 Brattico et al. 2016 — expert enhanced pleasure through cognition
  🟢 Limb & Braun 2008 — PFC during jazz improvisation
  🟢 Koelsch 2002 — ERAN expertise effects
```

## §3 — Phase 2: Architecture, Micro-Reward, Inverted-U

### §3.1 — Insight 33: Music Architecture = Prediction

```
ORIGINAL CLAIM:
  Each music feature = solution to brain prediction constraint.
  Stats: ~94% pop = 4/4, BPM ~120 dominant, I-IV-V ~62%.
  Cross-cultural universals = prediction constraints.

VERDICT: PARTIALLY SUPPORTED (1 bad stat, 1 missing co-explanation)

  ✅ 4/4 ~94% = CONFIRMED:
     440 pop songs study: 94% in 4/4. Multiple sources: >90% of pop last 100 years.
     ⚠️ Western-specific: Balkan, Indian, Turkish, Greek use asymmetric meters (7/8, 9/8)
     → "Lowest complexity = widest accessibility" = Western-centric claim.

  ✅ ~120 BPM dominant = CONFIRMED:
     Spontaneous Motor Tempo (SMT) clusters ~120 BPM = walking cadence.
     Tempo reproduction accuracy peaks near 120 BPM (Frontiers 2022, 2025).
     Dance music averages ~120 BPM.

  ❌ I-IV-V ~62% = NOT SUPPORTED (NO SOURCE FOUND):
     McGill Billboard corpus: I, IV, V individually most frequent.
     I-V-vi-IV appears in ~10% post-1990. BUT "62%" figure
     appears in NO published research.
     → Must REMOVE this specific number.

  ✅ Cross-cultural universals = CONFIRMED (statistical):
     Savage et al. 2015 (PNAS): 18 statistical universals across 304 recordings,
     9 world regions. Discrete pitch, isochronous beat, limited pitch sets.
     Mehr et al. 2019 (Science): music in every society, consistent associations.
     ⚠️ STATISTICAL universals, not absolute. Closest absolute (discrete pitch
     OR isochronous beat) covered 301/304 recordings.

  🟡 Prediction optimization = PARTIALLY SUPPORTED:
     Huron 2006 (Sweet Anticipation): strong theoretical + empirical support
     via ITPRA model. Vuust & Witek 2014: predictive coding in music.

     ⚠️ BUT: SOCIAL BONDING = CO-EQUAL DRIVER:
     Savage et al. 2021 (BBS): music = "coevolved system for social bonding."
     Isochronous beat has "clear functions for group performance but little
     or no function in solo performance."
     → Prediction is NOT sole explanation.

  ⚠️ CORRECTIONS NEEDED:
     ① REMOVE "I-IV-V ~62%" — no source
     ② ADD social bonding/coordination as co-equal driver alongside prediction
     ③ NOTE 4/4 dominance = Western-specific

NEW CITATIONS:
  🟢 Savage et al. 2015 (PNAS) — 18 statistical universals
  🟢 Mehr et al. 2019 (Science) — universality and diversity in human song
  ⭐ Savage et al. 2021 (BBS) — music as coevolved social bonding system
  🟢 Frontiers 2022, 2025 — SMT ~120 BPM evidence
```

### §3.2 — Insight 37: Continuous Micro-Reward

```
ORIGINAL CLAIM:
  Music = continuous micro-reward conveyor belt.
  Hundreds of small prediction-resolution rewards per song.
  Same 7-step mechanism, different temporal pattern (continuous vs discrete).

VERDICT: PARTIALLY SUPPORTED

  ✅ Continuous prediction processing = CONFIRMED (theory):
     Vuust, Heggli, Friston & Kringelbach 2022 (Nature Reviews Neuroscience):
     "Music in the brain" — brain "continuously constructs predictions of
     what happens next." PCM model frames music as ONGOING prediction-error.

  🟡 "Hundreds of micro-rewards" = REASONABLE INFERENCE, NOT MEASURED:
     Salimpoor 2011 measured dopamine at PEAK moments only.
     Ferreri et al. 2019 (PNAS): dopamine causally mediates pleasure,
     extends beyond stereotypical "chills." BUT electrodermal responses
     were PHASIC (burst-like at high-pleasure moments), NOT tonic/continuous.
     → Prediction processing = continuous. Reward signaling = likely phasic
       with continuous low-level component.

  🟡 Same mechanism different temporal = PARTIALLY:
     Gold, Pearce & Mas-Herrero 2019: inverted-U of predictability in music
     = same prediction-error reward logic. PCM: music "coopts a ubiquitous
     feature of the central nervous system."
     → Same mechanism = supported. "Uniquely continuous" = not compared.

  ⚠️ CORRECTION NEEDED:
     ① "Hundreds of micro-rewards" → "continuous prediction processing with
        phasic reward peaks" (more accurate to evidence)
     ② "Conveyor belt" metaphor → ok as metaphor if noted as 🟡

NEW CITATIONS:
  ⭐ Vuust et al. 2022 (Nature Reviews Neuroscience) — Music in the brain
  🟢 Ferreri et al. 2019 (PNAS) — dopamine causal role
  🟢 Gold, Pearce & Mas-Herrero 2019 (J Neuroscience) — predictability inverted-U
```

### §3.3 — Insight 38: Inverted-U Trajectory

```
ORIGINAL CLAIM:
  Phase 0 (0 exposure): no gap → no desire
  Phase 1 (1-5 listens): building
  Phase 2 (5-30 listens): Goldilocks peak
  Phase 3 (30-200): precision deepening
  Phase 4 (200+): saturation

VERDICT: SHAPE CONFIRMED, NUMBERS WRONG

  ✅ Inverted-U shape = STRONGLY CONFIRMED:
     ⭐ Chmiel & Schubert 2017: reviewed 57 studies,
     87.7% COMPATIBLE with inverted-U model.
     Berlyne 1971 foundational. Zajonc mere exposure.

  ❌ Specific numbers = NOT SUPPORTED:
     Schellenberg et al. 2008: liking peaked at 2-8 EXPOSURES
     under focused listening, declining by 32.
     → "5-30 = peak" is TOO HIGH.
     → "200+ = saturation" has NO empirical anchor.
     (Research rarely tests beyond ~32 exposures in controlled settings.)

  🟡 Complexity × window size = SUPPORTED in direction:
     Simple pieces peak EARLIER, complex pieces LATER (general finding).
     Exact numbers not quantified.

  ⚠️ CORRECTIONS NEEDED:
     ① Change "1-5 building, 5-30 peak" → "peak at ~2-8 exposures
        under focused listening" (Schellenberg 2008)
     ② Change "30-200" → "beyond peak, gradual decline"
     ③ Change "200+" → "saturation timeline varies, untested at high N"
     ④ Note: numbers VARY by complexity + listening mode
        (incidental vs focused = different curves)

NEW CITATIONS:
  ⭐ Chmiel & Schubert 2017 — 57 studies, 87.7% inverted-U
  🟢 Schellenberg et al. 2008 — peak at 2-8 exposures
```

## §4 — Phase 3: Subsidy, Selection, Compound, Shape

### §4.1 — Insight 8: No Hardware-Subsidy

```
ORIGINAL CLAIM:
  Music has NO hardware-subsidy (no oxytocin/prolactin).
  Only general μ-opioid (Mallik 2017).
  Frequency compensates (2-4h/day vs ~200h/year for friend, Hall 2018).

VERDICT: PARTIALLY SUPPORTED ("NO" quá tuyệt đối)

  🟡 "No hardware-subsidy" = TOO ABSOLUTE:
     ⚠️ Oxytocin DOES increase from music in some studies:
     Kreutz 2014: group singing → oxytocin increase
     Busse et al. 2026 (systematic review): "general consensus that
     music listening enhances oxytocin synthesis"
     → BUT: inconsistent, context-dependent, weaker than mother-infant system.
     One study: salivary oxytocin DECREASED during choral singing.

     Norman-Haignere 2015: music-selective neural population in auditory cortex,
     present even in newborns (Perani 2010). But this is PERCEPTUAL processing
     circuit, NOT neurochemical bonding circuit.

     Correct framing: "No DEDICATED bonding-specific hardware-subsidy
     comparable to pair-bonding. Music co-opts GENERAL reward (μ-opioid,
     dopamine) and BORROWED social neurochemistry (endorphin, occasional
     oxytocin)."

  ✅ Mallik 2017 = CONFIRMED:
     Scientific Reports. Naltrexone (mu-opioid antagonist) reduced both
     positive AND negative emotional responses to music.

  ✅ Hall 2018 = CONFIRMED:
     Jeffrey Hall, JSPR (published 2019). ~200 hours to close friend.
     ~50 hours casual, ~90 hours friend. Figure accurate.

  ✅ 2-4 hours/day = CONFIRMED:
     IFPI 2023: 20.7 hours/week globally (~3h/day).
     Edison Research: Americans ~4+ hours all audio.
     Streaming users: 2-3.5 hours/day.

  ✅ Dunbar 2012 + Weinstein 2016 = CONFIRMED:
     Music performance → endorphin (pain threshold proxy) + social bonding.
     Bonding via ENDORPHIN (like primate grooming), NOT music-specific.

  ⚠️ CORRECTION NEEDED:
     "KHÔNG có hardware-subsidy" → "không có DEDICATED bonding-specific
     hardware-subsidy. Dựa vào general reward + borrowed social neurochemistry.
     Oxytocin response có nhưng inconsistent và yếu hơn pair-bonding system."

NEW CITATIONS:
  🟢 Kreutz 2014 — group singing oxytocin
  🟢 Busse et al. 2026 — systematic review oxytocin + music
  🟢 Perani et al. 2010 — newborn music-selective processing
```

### §4.2 — Insight 22: Selection vs Reinforcement

```
ORIGINAL CLAIM:
  Bidirectional: selection (~60-70%) + reinforcement (~30-40%).
  Boer 2011: values not personality mediate.

VERDICT: DIRECTION CONFIRMED, SPECIFIC NUMBERS NOT SUPPORTED

  ✅ Boer 2011: values not personality = CONFIRMED:
     Two lab experiments + one dyadic field study.
     Shared music preferences → social bonds BECAUSE they signal
     similar VALUE ORIENTATIONS. Personality ruled out explicitly.

  ✅ Bidirectional = CONFIRMED:
     Selfhout et al. 2009 (J Adolescence, N=566, 1-year longitudinal):
     music preference similarity promotes friendship FORMATION (selection).
     Ter Bogt et al. 2017 (SNARE, N=1,144, SIENA modeling):
     BOTH selection (highbrow music) AND influence (dance music).
     → Selection > influence consistently.

  ❌ "~60-70% / ~30-40%" = NO SOURCE:
     No study found quantifying this specific split.
     Research confirms selection > influence DIRECTIONALLY,
     nhưng exact effect sizes KHÁC nhau tùy genre + context.
     → Must REMOVE these specific numbers.

  🟡 Selfhout 2009 "niche genres predict friendship" = NUANCED:
     Original study: ALL four genre categories (rock, urban, pop/dance,
     highbrow) predicted friendship formation, NOT exclusively niche.
     SNARE research: non-mainstream genres (rock, urban) particularly
     linked to friendship selection.
     → "Niche > mainstream" direction supported nhưng original claim
       about Selfhout specifically needs correction.

  ⚠️ CORRECTIONS NEEDED:
     ① REMOVE "~60-70% / ~30-40%" — no source for specific numbers
     ② Change to: "selection dominant, reinforcement secondary (exact
        ratio varies by genre and context)"
     ③ Selfhout: "all genres predicted friendship, with non-mainstream
        genres showing particularly strong selection effects"

NEW CITATIONS:
  🟢 Selfhout et al. 2009 — longitudinal friendship formation
  🟢 Ter Bogt et al. 2017 — SNARE selection + influence
```

### §4.3 — Insight 29: Compound = Multiplicative

```
ORIGINAL CLAIM:
  Coherent multi-modal = MULTIPLICATIVE compound reward.
  Incoherent = multiplicative DISSONANCE.

VERDICT: PARTIALLY SUPPORTED ("multiplicative" overstated)

  ✅ Coherent multimodal enhances reward = CONFIRMED:
     2024 study: congruent visual performer movements significantly
     increased subjective pleasure + skin conductance.
     Mu-rhythm entrainment as neural mechanism.

  🟡 "Multiplicative" = OVERSTATED:
     Neuroimaging: multisensory integration CAN BE super-additive

     fMRI: audiovisual valence incongruence → increased frontoparietal
     processing demands + caudate activation. Consistent with
     dissonance-like cost.

  ⚠️ CORRECTION NEEDED:
     "Multiplicative" → "potentially super-additive (sometimes > sum
     of parts, nhưng also sometimes additive or sub-additive depending
     on conditions)"

NEW CITATIONS:
  🟢 Visual congruency study 2024 — PMC
  🟢 eLife 2024 — multisensory integration conditions
```

```
ORIGINAL CLAIM:
```
